How to Check & Download AAI Junior Assistant, Senior Assistant Result 2025
  1. Visit the official AAI website – Open the Airports Authority of India (AAI) recruitment portal.
  2. Go to the Career/Recruitment Section – On the homepage, click on the “Careers” or “Recruitments” tab.
  3. Find Result Notification – Look for the link titled “Result for Junior Assistant & Senior Assistant Recruitment 2025”.
  4. Click on the Result Link – A PDF will open containing the list of selected/qualified candidates.
  5. Check Your Roll Number / Name – Use Ctrl + F to search for your roll number, name, or registration ID in the merit list.
  6. Download & Save PDF – Save the result file on your device for future reference.
  7. Print Copy – Take a printout of the result for document verification and joining process.
