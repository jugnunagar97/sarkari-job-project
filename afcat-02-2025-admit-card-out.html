AFCAT 02/2025 Admit Card

Post Date: August 19, 2025 5:17 PM

Indian Air Force (Bhartiya Vayu Sena), has released the Admit Card on its official website for the Recruitment of Air Force Common Admission Test (AFCAT) 02/2025. The exam will be conducted on August 2025, and the last date to submit the application form was 01 July 2025. Candidates can check their Exam City Details / Admit Card using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the portal. The direct link is provided below.

How To Check Air Force AFCAT 02/2025 Admit Card
  • Visit the official AFCAT website: afcat.cdac.in
  • Click on “Candidate Login” and select AFCAT‑02/2025 cycle.
  • Log in using your registered email ID and password.
  • After logging in, look for the link to download the admit card/hall ticket.
  • Download and carefully check your assigned exam city and centre details on the admit card.
Indian Air Force AFCAT 02/2025 Online Form : Exam Pattern
  • Exam Mode : Online (CBT-Based)
  • Question Type : (Multiple Choice Questions) Objective Type
  • No. Of Question : 100 Marks
  • No. Of Marks : 300 Marks
  • Subject (Syllabus) : General Intelligence, Reasoning, General Knowledge, General Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude, English Comprehension
  • Pre Exam Duration : 02 Hours
  • Marking Scheme : Correct Answer: +3 Mark, -1 Negative Marking
  • Exam Language : Hindi & English
Mode Of Selection Indian Air Force AFCAT 02/2025 Online Form
  • Written Exam: Candidates must qualify for the written examination as the first step.
  • Air Force Selection Board (AFSB): Shortlisted candidates appear for the AFSB interview, which assesses various skills and aptitudes.
  • Document Verification: Candidates must provide valid documents to verify their eligibility and credentials.
  • Medical Examination: A thorough medical examination ensures candidates meet the required physical and medical standards.
