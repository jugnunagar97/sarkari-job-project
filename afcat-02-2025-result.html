How To Check & Download Air Force AFCAT 02/2025 Result
  1. Visit the Official AFCAT Portal: Go to the Indian Air Force AFCAT official website.
  2. Login with Details: Enter your Email ID / Username and Password created during registration. Fill the captcha/security code if asked.
  3. Find the Result Link: On the dashboard, click on “AFCAT 02/2025 Result” or the latest notification available.
  4. Check Your Score & Qualifying Status: Your result will show:
    • Marks obtained
    • Cut-off marks
    • Qualifying status (Pass/Fail)
  5. Download & Save the Result: Click on Download Result / Scorecard. Save it as a PDF and take printouts for future reference (AFSB interview, document verification, etc.).

AFCAT 02/2025 Result 2025: FAQs

Question: When will the AFCAT 02/2025 Result 2025 be released?

Answer: The result has been released. Candidates can check their result on the official AFCAT website.

Question: How can I download my AFCAT 02/2025 Result?

Answer: By logging in with your email ID and password on the official AFCAT website and clicking on the Result/Scorecard link.

Question: What details will be mentioned on the result?

Answer: Candidate’s name, roll number, marks obtained, cut-off marks, and qualifying status.

Question: What happens after qualifying AFCAT 02/2025?

Answer: Shortlisted candidates will be called for the AFSB (Air Force Selection Board) Interview.

Question: What is the official website for AFCAT?

Answer: The official website for AFCAT is https://afcat.cdac.in/AFCAT/.
