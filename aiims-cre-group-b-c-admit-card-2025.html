AIIMS CRE Group B, C Admit Card 2025

Post Date: August 22, 2025 5:34 PM

All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi, has released the Admit Card on its official website for the Recruitment of Various Group B & C Exam 2025. The exam will be conducted on 25-26 August 2025, and the last date to submit the application form was 31 July 2025. Candidates can check their Exam Date / Admit Card using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the portal. The direct link is provided below.
