AIIMS CRE Group B, C Result 2025

Post Date: September 25, 2025

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi, has released the results for the Common Recruitment Examination (CRE) for Group B and C posts 2025 on its official website. Conducted on 25-26 August 2025 to fill over 2300 vacancies for posts like Assistant, Dietician, and Technician, the results were announced on 25 September 2025. Candidates can check their merit list and qualifying status using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth at www.aiimsexams.ac.in. Direct links are provided below.

How to Check & Download AIIMS CRE Group B, C Result 2025
  • Go to the AIIMS Exams Official Portal: Open your browser and navigate to www.aiimsexams.ac.in.
  • Find the “Results” or “CRE Results” Section: On the homepage, look for a menu or tab labeled “Results”, “Recruitment”, or “CRE”.
  • Locate the AIIMS CRE Group B & C Result Link: Find the specific link for “CRE Group B & C Result 2025” or “AIIMS CRE Result 2025 — Group B / C Posts”.
  • Open the Result PDF / Login Page: The result may open as a PDF merit list showing roll numbers/names of shortlisted candidates, or it may require logging in with credentials.
  • Enter Your Credentials (if required): Provide your Registration Number/Roll Number and Date of Birth/Password if prompted.
  • Submit and View the Result: After submitting, your result (score, qualification status, etc.) will appear, or the merit list PDF will open.
  • Download / Save the Result: Download the PDF or use the “Print to PDF” option to save it.
  • Check Your Details Carefully: Verify your Name, Roll Number, Category, Marks/Score, and Qualifying Status.
  • Take a Printout: Save a printout of the result for future reference (e.g., document verification).
