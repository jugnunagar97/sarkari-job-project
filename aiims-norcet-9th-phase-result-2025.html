AIIMS NORCET 9TH PHASE RESULT 2025

Post Date: September 18, 2025

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi has released the Stage-I Result for the NORCET 9th Phase on its official website, aiimsexams.ac.in. The Preliminary exam was conducted on September 14, 2025, with applications accepted from July 22 to August 11, 2025. Candidates can download the result PDF, which lists roll numbers of the 19,334 candidates qualified for the Stage-II (Mains) exam scheduled for September 27, 2025. No login credentials are required; use Ctrl + F to search for your roll number. The recruitment aims to fill 3,700 Nursing Officer posts across AIIMS institutes. Stay updated at sarkaariresult.org for further updates.
