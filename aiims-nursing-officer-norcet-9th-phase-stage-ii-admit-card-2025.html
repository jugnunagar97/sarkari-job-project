How to Check & Download AIIMS NORCET 9th Phase Stage-II Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official AIIMS Exams website at www.aiimsexams.ac.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Admit Card” section.
  • Click on the link for “NORCET 9 Admit Card 2025”.
  • Enter your Registration ID, Password/Date of Birth, and Captcha code.
  • Click on Login / Submit.
  • Your Admit Card will appear on the screen.
  • Download it and take a printout (preferably in color).
  • Verify details like Name, Roll Number, Exam Date, Venue, and Instructions.
  • Carry a valid Photo ID (Aadhar Card, PAN, Voter ID, etc.) and a passport-size photo to the exam center.
