HOW TO DOWNLOAD ARMY SCHOOL AWES ADMIT CARD 2025
  1. Visit the official AWES Website at awesindia.com.
  2. On the homepage, look for the link related to "OST (Online Screening Test) Admit Card 2025".
  3. Click on the link and you will be redirected to the login page.
  4. Enter your Application Number and Password / Date of Birth.
  5. Your admit card will be displayed on the screen.
  6. Download the admit card and print at least two copies for future reference.
