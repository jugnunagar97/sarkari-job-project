Army School AWES TGT, PGT, PRT Result 2025

Post Date: October 8, 2025 | Updated on: October 9, 2025 at 10:00 AM IST

The Army Welfare Education Society (AWES) has released the Result for the recruitment of TGT, PGT, PRT Teacher Examination 2025 on its official website. The Army School AWES application process started on 05 June 2025, and candidates were able to submit their applications until 16 August 2025. The Screening Test is scheduled to be conducted on 20, 21 September 2025. Candidates can check and download their Result by logging into the portal (link provided below) using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number.

  • Online Apply Last Date: 16 August 2025

  • Correction Last Date: 22-24 August 2025

  • Admit Card: 08 September 2025

  • Note: Candidates are advised to confirm from the AWES official website.

Application Fee

  • For General / OBC: ₹ 385/-
  • For SC / ST: ₹ 385/-
  • Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, Internet Banking, IMPS, Cash Card / Mobile Wallet

Army School AWES TGT, PGT, PRT Notification 2025: Age Limits (As on 16 August 2025)

  • Fresh Candidates: Should be below 40 years of age.
  • For NCR Schools: TGT/PRT: Below 29 years, PGT: Below 36 years
  • Experienced Candidates: Should be below 57 years of age.
  • AWES provides age relaxation for the TGT, PGT, PRT position as per their regulations.

Army School AWES TGT, PGT, PRT Examination 2025: Exam Pattern
  • Exam Mode: Online (CBT-Based)
  • Question Type: (Multiple Choice Questions) Objective Type
  • No. Of Question Tier I: 200 Question
  • Total Marks Tier I: 200 Marks
  • Subject (Syllabus): Section A (Basic GK and Current Affairs), Section B (Pedagogy, Curriculum and Education Policy), Section C (Academic Proficiency)
  • Exam Duration: 03 Hours
  • Marking Scheme: Correct Answer: +1 Mark, Deduction of ¼ mark for each incorrect answer
  • Exam Language: Hindi & English
