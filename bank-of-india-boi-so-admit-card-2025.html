Bank of India BOI SO Admit Card 2025

Post Date: May 6, 2025

The Bank of India (BOI) has released the Admit Card for the recruitment of Specialist Officers (SO) for 180 positions. The BOI SO Exam is scheduled for 17 May 2025. Candidates can download their admit card from the official website by logging in with their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. The application process concluded on 23 March 2025.

How to Check & Download Bank of India BOI SO Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official Bank of India website: https://bankofindia.co.in.
  • Scroll down and click on the “Career” tab under the ‘Important Links’ section.
  • Click on the “View All” button under the ‘Recruitment Notice’ tab.
  • Look for the link titled “Download Call Letter for Specialist Officer 2025” and click on it.
  • Enter your Registration Number/Roll Number and Password/Date of Birth, fill in the captcha code, and click on the “Login” button.
  • Your admit card will appear on the screen. Download it and take a printout to carry on the day of the examination.
