BCECE-LE Mop-up Round Seat Allotment Result 2025

Post Date: September 12, 2025 10:18 PM

The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) has published the Mop-up Round Seat Allotment Result for BCECE (LE) 2025, facilitating lateral entry into the second year of graduate-level Engineering, Para-Medical, and Pharmacy courses. The application process ran from 16 April 2025 to 18 May 2025, with the entrance exam held on 09 June 2025. Candidates can find comprehensive details about the BCECE-LE Mop-up Round Seat Allotment Result 2025 below.

BCECE LE Examination 2025 : Short Details
Important Dates Application Fee

Online Apply Start Date: 16 April 2025

Online Apply Last Date: 18 May 2025

Pay Exam Fee Last Date: 18 May 2025

Online Correction Date: 19-20 May 2025

Exam Date: 09 June 2025

Admit Card Available: 29 May 2025

Result Declared Date: 01 July 2025

For General, BC, EBC: ₹ 2200/-

For SC, ST, DQ: ₹ 2200/-

Payment Mode (Online): You can make the payment using the following methods:

  • Debit Card
  • Credit Card
  • Internet Banking
  • IMPS
  • Cash Card / Mobile Wallet

BCECE LE Online Form 2025 : Age Limits As On 31 December 2025

  • Minimum Age: No Age Limit (For Graduate Engineer)
  • Minimum Age: 18 Years (Para-Medical & Pharmacy)
  • Maximum Age: No Age Limit
  • Check More Details For BCECE LE 2025 Visit Official Website.

Total Seats

13,780 Seats
BCECE LE Online Form 2025 : Admission Details
Exam Name Education Qualification
BCECE LE 2025
  • Engineering Course Group: Passed Minimum THREE Years / TWO Years (Lateral & Entry) Diploma examination with at least 45% marks (40% marks in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) in any branch of Engineering and Technology. OR Passed B.Sc. Degree from a recognized University as defined by UGC, with at least 45% marks (40% marks in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) and passed 10+2 examination with Mathematics as a subject. OR Passed D.Voc. Stream in the same or allied sector. (The Universities will offer suitable bridge courses such as Mathematics, Physics, Engineering drawing, etc. for the students coming from diverse backgrounds to achieve desired learning outcomes of the programme).
  • Para Medical Degree Course Group: Passed in two years Para Medical Diploma Course recognized by the Government of Bihar/ Government of India for enrollment on the basis of this competitive examination in Para Medical Degree Course. Candidates appearing in the final examination of two year Para Medical Diploma course can also apply; But it will be necessary to have passed by the time of interview/counseling. Candidates will be considered eligible for enrollment in the second year of higher para medical degree related to the para medical degree course/course from which they have passed two year para medical diploma examination. If higher para medical degree course is not available in the course / course from which the candidate has passed the para medical degree course / course, then such candidates will not be considered eligible to appear in this lateral entrance examination.
  • Pharmacy Degree Course Group: Passed in two years Diploma Pharmacy Course recognized by the Government of Bihar / Government of India for enrollment on the basis of this competitive examination in the Pharmacy Degree Course. Candidates appearing in the final examination of two year pharmacy diploma course can also apply; But it will be necessary to have passed by the time of interview/counseling.
How To Check & Download BCECE-LE Online Counselling/ Choice Filling 2025

  • How to access the BCECEB website?

    Open the official website of the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB).

  • Where to find BCECE-LE updates?

    On the homepage, look for the section labeled “BCECE-LE 2025” or similar. Click to access all updates and options related to Lateral Entry counselling.

  • How to check the counselling notification?

    Find and open the official notification titled “Online Counselling / Choice Filling for BCECE-LE 2025”. Read the full instructions and counselling schedule carefully.

  • How to log in to the counselling portal?

    Click on the link to begin counselling. Log in using your Application Number or Roll Number and your Password or Date of Birth.

  • What to do after logging in?

    Confirm your basic details, pay the counselling registration fee if applicable, and proceed to the next step.

  • How to fill choices?

    Choose from the list of available colleges and branches, arrange them in priority order, and save your choices regularly.

  • How to lock choices?

    After filling choices, click on “Lock Choices” to confirm your selections, download and print the confirmation slip for future use.

  • How to check seat allotment?

    After the last date of choice locking, wait for the seat allotment result as per the counselling schedule, then log in to check your allotted institute and course.

  • How to download the allotment letter?

    Download your Provisional Allotment Letter from the counselling portal and take a printout.

  • What is required for document verification?

    Go to the designated verification center or allotted college with your printed allotment letter, original documents and photocopies, and passport-size photographs.

  • How to complete the admission process?

    Submit your documents for verification, pay the required admission fee at the institute, and confirm your seat officially.
