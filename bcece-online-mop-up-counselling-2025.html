BCECE Mop-up Revised Counselling Schedule 2025

Post Date: September 12, 2025 10:11 PM

The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) has released the Revised Mop-up Counselling Schedule 2025 for admissions to Engineering, Medical, Pharmacy, and Agriculture streams through the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination (BCECE) 2025. The application process was open from 9 April to 18 May 2025, with corrections allowed on 19-20 May 2025. The entrance exam was conducted on 8-9 June 2025, and results were declared on 1 July 2025. Candidates can check their results using their Roll Number or Application ID and Date of Birth on the official website, bceceboard.bihar.gov.in. The mop-up counselling is for candidates who did not secure seats in earlier rounds or wish to upgrade their allotted seats. The revised schedule, seat matrix, and other details are available on the official website. Candidates are advised to complete the online registration and choice filling for mop-up counselling promptly to secure admission.

BCECE Entrance 2025: Admission Details
Stream Education Qualification
Engineering
  • Passed or appearing in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM).
  • Minimum 45% marks (40% for SC/ST) in PCM subjects from a recognized board.
Pharmacy
  • Passed or appearing in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology (PCM/PCB).
  • Minimum 45% marks (40% for SC/ST) in PCM/PCB subjects from a recognized board.
Medical
  • Passed or appearing in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English (PCB).
  • Minimum 50% marks (40% for SC/ST) in PCB subjects from a recognized board.
Agriculture
  • Passed or appearing in 10+2 with I.Sc (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) or I.Sc (Agriculture).
  • Specific subject combinations (CBA/MCA/MBA/PCA) vary by college requirements.
How to Check & Download BCECE Entrance Result 2025

  • How to access the BCECEB website?

    Visit the official website: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.

  • Where to find the result?

    In the “Download Section” or “Latest Updates,” click on “Rank Card of BCECE-2025.”

  • What details are required?

    Enter your Roll Number or Application ID, Date of Birth, and CAPTCHA/PIN (if prompted).

  • How to view the result?

    Submit the details to view your scorecard and rank on the screen.

  • What to do after viewing?

    Download the PDF version of the result and print multiple copies for future use.
How to Participate in BCECE Mop-up Counselling 2025

  • How to start?

    Visit admissions.nic.in/Bihar for mop-up counselling registration.

  • What to do next?

    Log in with Application ID and Password, complete registration, and fill in choice of colleges and courses.

  • How to submit choices?

    Review and lock your choices before the deadline (15 September 2025).

  • What happens after choice filling?

    Check the mop-up round seat allotment result on 20 September 2025 using your login credentials.

  • What are the final steps?

    Attend document verification and admission process from 22-24 September 2025 at the allotted institute with required documents (10+2 mark sheet, BCECE rank card, ID proof, etc.).
BCECE Entrance 2025: Mode of Selection

  • What is the selection process?

    Candidates must qualify the BCECE Entrance Examination (objective-type, covering Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology based on stream).

  • What follows the exam?

    Results are declared, and a rank card is issued based on marks scored.

  • How is admission finalized?

    Candidates participate in online counselling (1st, 2nd, and mop-up rounds), where seats are allotted based on rank, choice filling, and availability. Document verification and fee payment complete the admission process.
BCECE Entrance 2025: Important Questions

  • When did the online application for BCECE 2025 start?

    The application process started on 9 April 2025.

  • What was the last date to apply?

    The last date was 18 May 2025.

  • What are the age limits?

    No age limit for Engineering; minimum 17 years for Pharmacy and Medical; minimum 16 years for Agriculture (as on 31 December 2025).

  • What is the eligibility?

    Candidates must have passed or appeared in 10+2 with required subjects: Engineering (PCM), Pharmacy (PCM/PCB), Medical (PCB with English), Agriculture (I.Sc or I.Sc Agriculture).

  • What is the official website?

    The official website is bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
