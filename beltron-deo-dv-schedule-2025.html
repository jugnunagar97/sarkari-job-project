BELTRON DEO DV Schedule 2025

Post Date: April 26, 2025

The Bihar State Electronics Development Corporation Limited (BSEDC) has published the Document Verification (DV) Schedule for the Data Entry Operator (DEO) Recruitment 2025. The DV process is set to take place from 28 April 2025 to 04 May 2025. Candidates can check their DV schedule on the official BELTRON website by logging in with their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth.

BELTRON DEO Recruitment 2025 : Vacancy Details
Post NameEligibility Criteria
Data Entry Operator (DEO)
  • Candidates must have passed 12th (Intermediate) from any recognized board in India.
  • 1-Year Computer Course, Proficiency in Hindi or English typing OR Completion of a 400-hour computer training program certified by Bihar Skill Development Mission (BSDM).
  • Typing Test: Typing on Computer in English with 30 WPM & 25 WPM in Hindi.
  • All candidates must read the official information completely before applying.
How to Check & Download BELTRON DEO DV Schedule 2025
  • Visit the official BELTRON website: https://bsedc.bihar.gov.in.
  • Locate and click on the ‘Letters & Circulars’ tab on the homepage.
  • Find the notification titled “Notice for verification of all the empanelled DEO by CBT exam 2024” dated 25th April 2025.
  • Click on the notification link to open the PDF.
  • Download or print the schedule for your reference.

BELTRON DEO Recruitment 2025 : Important Questions

Question: When did the online application for BELTRON DEO Recruitment 2025 start?

Answer: The online application started on 20 February 2024.

Question: What was the last date for the BELTRON DEO Online Form 2025?

Answer: The last date for online application was 15 March 2024.

Question: What is the age limit for the BELTRON DEO Bharti 2025?

Answer: The age limit is a minimum of 18 Years and a maximum of 59 Years as of 15 March 2024.
