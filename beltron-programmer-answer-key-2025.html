BELTRON Programmer Answer Key 2025

Post Date: August 29, 2025 2:00 PM

Bihar State Electronics Development Corporation Limited (BSEDC), has released the Answer Key for the recruitment of Programmer Examination 2025 on its official website. The BELTRON application process started on 11 November 2024, and candidates were able to submit their applications until 10 December 2024. The exam was conducted in 05-06 August 2025. Candidates can check and download their Answer Key by logging into the portal (link provided below) using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number.

BELTRON Programmer Examination 2024 : Short Details
Important Dates Application Fee

Application Start : 11 November 2024

Last Date : 10 December 2024

Last Date For Fee Payment : 10 December 2024

Exam Date : 05-06 August 2025

Admit Card : 01 August 2025

For Gen/ OBC/ EWS : ₹ 1000/-

BELTRON Programmer Notification 2024 : Age Limits As On 15 August 2024

  • Maximum Age : 59 Years

How To Check & Download BELTRON Programmer Answer Key 2025
  • Visit the official website of BELTRON (Bihar State Electronics Development Corporation Limited): bsedc.bihar.gov.in
  • On the homepage, go to the Notice Board / Latest Updates section.
  • Find the link for “Programmer Answer Key 2025”.
  • Click on the link and the Answer Key PDF will open.
  • Match your responses with the official answer key to calculate your expected score.
  • Download and save the PDF for future reference.
