Bihar BTSC Dresser Answer Key 2025

Post Date: July 30, 2025 10:54 AM

The Bihar Technical Service Commission (BTSC) has released the Answer Key for the Dresser Recruitment Examination 2025 on its official website, btsc.bihar.gov.in. The exam was conducted on 8–9 July 2025 under Advertisement No. 21/2025, with the answer key released on 30 July 2025. Candidates can access the answer key by logging in with their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. The objection window was open from 30 July to 4 August 2025. This recruitment aims to fill 3326 Dresser vacancies. Candidates are advised to check the official BTSC website for updates.

How to Check & Download Bihar BTSC Dresser Answer Key 2025

  • How to access the BTSC website?

    Visit the official BTSC website: btsc.bihar.gov.in.

  • Where to find the answer key section?

    On the homepage, locate the “Answer Key” or “BTSC Dresser Answer Key 2025” link under the Latest Notifications or Notice Board section.

  • How to log in?

    Click on the answer key link, enter your Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth, fill in the captcha code, and click “Submit.”

  • What will be displayed?

    The BTSC Dresser Answer Key 2025 PDF will display, showing correct responses for all 100 questions across all sets.

  • How to calculate your score?

    Compare your responses with the answer key, awarding +1 mark for each correct answer (no negative marking).

  • How to save the answer key?

    Download the answer key PDF and take a printout for reference.
Bihar BTSC Dresser Recruitment 2025: Mode of Selection

  • What is the selection process?

    The selection process includes a Written Examination (Computer-Based Test), Work Experience evaluation, and Document Verification.

  • What is the role of the written exam?

    The written exam (100 MCQs, 100 marks) shortlists candidates based on performance in General Knowledge, General Science, Medical and Health-Related Knowledge, and Basic First Aid.

  • What is the final step?

    Final selection is based on written exam performance, work experience (if applicable), and document verification.
