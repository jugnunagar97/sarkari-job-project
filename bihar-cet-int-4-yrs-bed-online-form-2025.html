BIHAR CET INT 4 YRS BED ONLINE FORM 2025

Post Date: September 10, 2025

Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University (BRABU), Muzaffarpur, has released the notification for the Bihar State Level Combined Entrance Test (CET) for the 4-Year Integrated B.Ed. Course (CET-Int-B.Ed-2025). The online application process has already begun on 09 September 2025 and will remain open until 26 September 2025. This entrance test is for admission into B.Sc.-B.Ed. and B.A.-B.Ed. integrated programs. Interested candidates can find all details regarding eligibility, application fees, and the application process below.

BIHAR CET INT 4 YRS BED EXAMINATION 2025 : SHORT DETAILS

Important Dates

  • Online Apply Start Date: 09 September 2025
  • Online Apply Last Date: 26 September 2025
  • Last Date For Fee Payment: 26 September 2025
  • Exam Date: Notify Later

Application Fee

  • General: ₹ 1000/-
  • BC/EBC/EWS/Women/PH: ₹ 750/-
  • SC / ST: ₹ 500/-
  • Payment Mode: Online.
BIHAR CET INT 4 YRS BED NOTIFICATION 2025 : AGE LIMITS
Age limits are as per BRABU rules. Please refer to the official prospectus for detailed information.
BIHAR CET INT 4 YRS BED ONLINE FORM 2025 : ELIGIBILITY DETAILS
Course Name Eligibility Criteria
Bihar CET Int 4 Yrs B.Ed
  • Candidates must have scored at least 50% marks in Class 12 (Senior Secondary/+2).
  • For reserved category candidates, the required marks are 45%.
HOW TO FILL BIHAR CET INT 4 YRS BED ONLINE FORM 2025

Interested candidates can submit their application online between 09 September 2025 and 26 September 2025 through the official Bihar CET Integrated B.Ed website. Candidates are strongly advised to read the official notification and prospectus carefully before filling out the form.
