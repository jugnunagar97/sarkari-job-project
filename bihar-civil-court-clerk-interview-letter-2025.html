BIHAR CIVIL COURT UDC (UPPER DIVISIONAL CLERK) INTERVIEW LETTER 2025

Post Date: September 6, 2025

The Interview Letters for the Civil Court Clerk (Upper Divisional Clerk) recruitment have been released by the District Courts of Bihar. The interview phase is currently underway, scheduled from 08 to 20 September 2025. This follows the Mains Exam which was held on 18 May 2025. Candidates who have qualified for the interview round can now download their letters from the official portal by logging in with their Registration Number and Date of Birth. Further details and direct links are available below.

BIHAR CIVIL COURT CLERK EXAM 2024 : SHORT DETAILS

Important Dates (Clerk)

  • Application Last Date: 20 October 2022
  • Mains Exam Date: 18 May 2025
  • Mains Result: 18 May 2025
  • Interview Dates: 08-20 September 2025
  • Interview Letter: 05 September 2025

Application Fee

  • General / OBC / EWS: ₹ 800/-
  • SC / ST / Female: ₹ 400/-
  • Payment Mode: Online via Debit Card, Credit Card, or Net Banking.
BIHAR CIVIL COURT NOTIFICATION 2024 : AGE LIMITS AS ON 01 OCTOBER 2024
Minimum Age 20 Years
Maximum Age 25 Years
Age relaxation is provided as per government regulations.

HOW TO DOWNLOAD BIHAR CIVIL COURT CLERK INTERVIEW LETTER 2025
  1. Visit the official Bihar Civil Court website: patna.dcourts.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, navigate to the "Recruitment" or "Notices" section.
  3. Find and click the link for "Download Interview Letter for the post of Clerk - Advt. No. 01/2022".
  4. You will be redirected to the candidate login portal.
  5. Enter your Registration Number and Password/Date of Birth.
  6. Your interview letter will be displayed. Download and print a copy for the interview.
