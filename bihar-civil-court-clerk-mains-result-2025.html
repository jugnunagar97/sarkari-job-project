How To Check & Download Bihar Civil Court Mains Result 2025
  1. Visit the Official Website: Go to the Bihar Civil Court’s official website: patna.dcourts.gov.in.
  2. Go to the Result Section: On the homepage, locate the “Notification” section.
  3. Access the Result Link: Click on the link titled “Click here to Download the Result for the Mains Exam of Post of Clerk – Advt. No.- 01/2022”.
  4. Login to Your Account: Enter your Registration Number and Password in the provided fields.
  5. Download and Print: After logging in, your result will be displayed on the screen.
