BIHAR DIPLOMA DECE LE 2025 2ND ROUND ALLOTMENT RESULT

Post Date: August 1, 2025

The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) has released the 2nd Round Allotment Result for the Diploma Entrance Competitive Examination-Lateral Entry (DECE-LE) 2025. The exam was conducted on 15 June 2025. Candidates who participated in the counselling process can now check their allotment status from the official website or the direct link provided below.

Important Dates

Online Apply Start Date: 20 March 2025

Last Date for Apply Online: 04 May 2025

Exam Date: 15 June 2025

Admit Card Available: 30 April 2025

Result Declared Date: 02 July 2025

2nd Round Allotment Result: 01 August 2025

Application Fee

For All Candidates: ₹ 2200/-

Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, etc.

Bihar Diploma DECE-LE 2025: Age Limit

  • Minimum Age: N/A
  • Maximum Age: N/A
  • For details, please refer to the official notification.

Total Seats

NA
Bihar Diploma DECE-LE 2025: Eligibility Criteria
Exam Name Eligibility Details
Bihar Diploma DECE-LE
  • 12th (Science) with Physics & Chemistry as compulsory subjects, along with Maths or Biology, OR
  • 12th (Science) with Mathematics, OR
  • 12th (Science) with a Vocational/Technical subject, OR
  • 10th + 2-Year ITI.
How To Check Bihar Diploma DECE LE 2nd Round Allotment Result 2025
  1. Go to the official BCECEB portal: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  2. Find the link for “Second Round Seat Allotment Result of DECE[LE]-2025”.
  3. Click on the allotment link.
  4. Log in using your credentials (e.g., roll number, registration number) if prompted.
  5. The allotment list/result will be displayed.
  6. Download and save the PDF for future reference.
