The exam was conducted in 15 June 2025. The BCECEB application form will started on 6th March 2025, and candidates can fill out the form until 24th May 2025. How To Check & Download Bihar ITI CAT 2025 2nd Round Allotment Result
  1. Visit Official Website: Go to the BCECEB official portal: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in
  2. Go to “Download Section”: On the homepage, look for the “First Round Seat Allotment Result of ITICAT‑2025” link and click it.
  3. Open Seat Allotment PDF: You’ll be redirected to a PDF titled “SECOND ROUND SEAT ALLOTMENT RESULT OF ITICAT‑2025”.
  4. Login (If Required): You may need to log in using your Roll Number or Registration Number to access your individual allotment status.
  5. View and Download Result: Check your details in the PDF: your name, allotted institute/trade, category rank, etc. Save or print the PDF for admission use.
