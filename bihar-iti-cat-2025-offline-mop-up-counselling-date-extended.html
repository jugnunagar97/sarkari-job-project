Bihar ITI CAT 2025 Offline Mop-Up Counselling

Post Date: September 8, 2025 5:01 PM

The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) has published the 2nd Round Allotment Result for the Industrial Training Institute Competitive Admission Test (ITI CAT) 2025 on its official website. The examination took place on 15 June 2025, with applications accepted from 6 March 2025 to 24 May 2025. Candidates can access their seat matrix by logging into the portal using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. Detailed information and links are provided below.

How To Check & Download Bihar ITI CAT 2025 2nd Round Allotment Result
  • Visit Official Website: Go to the BCECEB official portal: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in.
  • Go to “Download Section”: On the homepage, look for the “Second Round Seat Allotment Result of ITICAT-2025” link and click it.
  • Open Seat Allotment PDF: You’ll be redirected to a PDF titled “SECOND ROUND SEAT ALLOTMENT RESULT OF ITICAT-2025”, published on 14 August 2025.
  • Login (If Required): Depending on the system, you may need to log in using your Roll Number or Registration Number (and possibly a password or DOB) to access your individual allotment status.
  • View and Download Result: Check your details in the PDF: your name, allotted institute/trade, category rank, etc. Save or print the PDF for admission use.
