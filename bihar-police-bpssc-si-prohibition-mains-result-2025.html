Post Date: September 24, 2025

The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released the Mains Result for the Sub Inspector (SI) Prohibition Recruitment 2025 in the Prohibition, Excise & Registration Department on its official website. How to Check & Download Bihar Police BPSSC SI Prohibition Mains Result 2025
  • Visit the Official BPSSC Website: Go to bpssc.bihar.gov.in.
  • Locate the Results Section: Find the section for Important Notices or “Result of Mains Written Examination for SI Prohibition (Advt. No. 01/2025)”.
  • Click the Relevant Result Link: Select the link for “SI Prohibition Mains Result 2025”.
  • Log In: Enter your Registration Number, Date of Birth, and any required password or captcha.
  • View and Download the Result: Your result will appear; download the PDF or scorecard and print multiple copies.
  • Verify Details: Check your Name, Roll Number, Category, Exam Center, Date/Time, and Qualifying Status for accuracy. Contact BPSSC if discrepancies arise.
  • Next Stage: Qualified candidates proceed to the Physical Eligibility Test (PET) and Medical Examination.
