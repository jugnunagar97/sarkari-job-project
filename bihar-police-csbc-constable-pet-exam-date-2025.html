How To Check & Download Bihar Police CSBC Constable Admit Card 2025
  1. VISIT THE OFFICIAL CSBC SITE: Open the official website of the Bihar Central Selection Board of Constables (CSBC).
  2. FIND THE ADMIT CARD SECTION: On the homepage, look for a section labeled “Latest Updates”, “Recruitment”, or “Admit Card”.
  3. LOCATE THE CONSTABLE ADMIT CARD NOTICE: Search for the notification titled “Download Admit Card — Bihar Police Constable Recruitment 2025”.
  4. OPEN THE ADMIT CARD DOWNLOAD PAGE: Click the noted link to access the admit card portal.
  5. LOG IN WITH YOUR DETAILS: Enter the required information like Application Number, Date of Birth, and any captcha if prompted. Then submit the form.
  6. VIEW AND VERIFY YOUR ADMIT CARD: Once logged in, your admit card will appear. Check your name, photo, roll number, exam date, shift, and exam centre address.
  7. DOWNLOAD AND PRINT COPIES: Download the admit card and print 2–3 clear copies. Keep one safely for exam day and others as backup.
