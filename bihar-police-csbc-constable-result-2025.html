Bihar Police CSBC Constable Result 2025

Post Date: September 26, 2025 | Updated on: October 9, 2025 at 10:00 AM IST

The Central Selection Board of Constable (CSBC) has released the Result for the recruitment of Bihar Police CSBC Constable Examination 2025 on its official website. The CSBC application process started on 18 March 2025, and candidates were able to submit their applications until 25 April 2025. The exam was scheduled to be conducted in 13, 16, 20, 23, 27, 30 July & 03, 06 August 2025. Candidates can check and download their Result by logging into the portal (link provided below) using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number.

How To Check & Download Bihar Police CSBC Constable Result 2025
  • Go to the official CSBC (Central Selection Board of Constables) website.
  • On the homepage, look for a section like “Results / Recruitment / Notices”.
  • Find the link titled “Bihar Police Constable Result 2025” or “Final Merit List / Written Exam Result”.
  • Click the link to open the result (usually a PDF file).
  • Download the PDF and save it on your device.
  • Open the PDF and use the search function (Ctrl + F) to find your roll number / name.
  • If your roll number appears, you are qualified / shortlisted for the next stage.
