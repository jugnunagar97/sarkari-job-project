Bihar Post Matric Scholarship 2025 – Apply Online Now

Post Date: January 13, 2025 | 1:17 PM IST

Bihar Post Matric Scholarship (PMS) opens applications for SC/ST/BC/EBC students pursuing post-secondary education. Online applications from 07 January to 10 March 2025. Income limit ₹3,00,000. FREE application – Bihar provides comprehensive financial aid for eligible deserving students.

Bihar Post Matric Scholarship 2025: Short Details

Important Dates

  • Online Apply Start: 07 January 2025
  • Online Apply Last Date: 10 March 2025
  • Application Portal: Open throughout application period
  • Category-wise portals (BC/EBC & SC/ST) available for registration

Application Fee

  • General / OBC: ₹00/- (FREE)
  • SC / ST / PH: ₹00/- (FREE)
  • No Fee for Any Category
  • Online Payment Mode: Card/Internet Banking (if needed)

Bihar Post Matric Scholarship 2025: Key Eligibility

  • Bihar permanent resident mandatory
  • SC/ST/BC/EBC category candidates
  • Annual family income ≤ ₹3,00,000
  • Pursuing post-secondary education in Bihar

How to Fill Bihar Post Matric Scholarship Online Form 2025
  • Select appropriate portal: BC/EBC category at https://pmsonline.bihar.gov.in/ or SC/ST at https://scstpmsonline.bihar.gov.in/.
  • Click "Registration" to create new account if first-time applicant.
  • Provide valid email ID and mobile number for account creation.
  • Receive One-Time Password (OTP) on registered mobile for verification.
  • Complete registration and create login credentials.
  • Log in using username and password to access application form.
  • Fill all mandatory personal details including name, father's name, date of birth, address carefully.
  • Provide exact residential information with complete pincode details.
  • Enter income details and verify annual family income documentation.
  • Select appropriate category (BC/EBC or SC/ST) for scholarship eligibility.
  • Specify current educational course, institution name, enrollment year details.
  • Upload passport-sized photograph (JPEG, under 50 KB) with white background.
  • Upload residential certificate (PDF, under 400 KB) proving Bihar residency.
  • Upload caste certificate (PDF, under 400 KB) for category verification.
  • Upload income certificate (PDF, under 400 KB) confirming family income limit.
  • Upload bona fide certificate (PDF, under 400 KB) from institution.
  • Upload fee receipt (PDF, under 400 KB) from current institution.
  • Upload last exam passing certificate (PDF, under 400 KB).
  • Upload relevant education certificates (10th/12th/Graduation, PDF, under 400 KB).
  • Review all information thoroughly before final submission.
  • Submit application after verification – Deadline: 10 March 2025.

Bihar Post Matric Scholarship 2025: Important Questions & Answers
  1. When will Bihar Post Matric Scholarship application start?

    Bihar PMS application started on 07 January 2025 through official portals (pmsonline.bihar.gov.in for BC/EBC and scstpmsonline.bihar.gov.in for SC/ST categories).

  2. What is the last date to apply for Bihar PMS 2025?

    The final application deadline is 10 March 2025. All eligible students must submit applications before this date to be considered for scholarship.

  3. What is the income limit for Bihar Post Matric Scholarship?

    Total annual family income including the applicant's own income should not exceed ₹3,00,000 to qualify for Bihar Post Matric Scholarship.

  4. Which categories are eligible for Bihar PMS 2025?

    Backward Class (BC), Extremely Backward Class (EBC), Scheduled Caste (SC), and Scheduled Tribe (ST) students are eligible. Separate portals available for BC/EBC and SC/ST applications.

  5. What is the official website for Bihar Post Matric Scholarship?

    The official portal is https://www.pmsonline.bih.nic.in/. Separate portals: pmsonline.bihar.gov.in (BC/EBC) and scstpmsonline.bihar.gov.in (SC/ST).
