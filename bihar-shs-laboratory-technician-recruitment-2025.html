Bihar SHS Laboratory Technician Recruitment 2025

Post Date: August 23, 2025 10:13 PM

Bihar Swastha Vibhag State Health Society (Bihar SHSB), has released a notification on official website for the recruitment of Laboratory Technician Post. This recruitment is for 1075 positions. Bihar Application Form will start from 01 September 2025 & the candidates can apply till the 15 September 2025. Minimum age required is 21 & The Maximum Age Is 42 Years as on 01 August 2025. Candidates must check the complete details for Bihar SHS Laboratory Technician Recruitment 2025. Link are given below.

Bihar SHS Laboratory Technician Notification 2025 : Age Limits As On 01 August 2025

  • Minimum Age : 21 Years
  • Maximum Age : 37 Years – Male-(UR /EWS)
  • Maximum Age : 40 Years. Female-(UR /EWS)
  • Maximum Age : 40 Years. Male / Female-(BC / MBC)
  • Maximum Age : 42 Years. Male / Female-(SC / ST)
  • Bihar SHSB provides age relaxation for the Bihar SHS Laboratory Technician position as per their regulations.
