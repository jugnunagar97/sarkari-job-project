BIHAR SHSB CHO MERIT LIST 2025

Post Date: September 19, 2025

The Bihar Swastha Vibhag State Health Society (SHSB) has released the Merit List for the recruitment of Community Health Officers (CHO) on a contractual basis at Health & Wellness Centers under the National Health Mission (NHM). This recruitment is for 4500 positions. The application window closed on May 26, 2025, and the exam was conducted on July 10, 2025. The merit list, released on September 18, 2025, can be checked using your Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth on the official portal, shs.bihar.gov.in. Candidates are advised to verify details on the official SHSB website. All essential links are provided below.

HOW TO CHECK & DOWNLOAD BIHAR SHSB CHO MERIT LIST 2025
  1. Visit the official website of the State Health Society, Bihar: shs.bihar.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, locate and click on the “Careers / Recruitment / Latest Updates” section.
  3. Search for the link titled “Bihar SHSB CHO Merit List 2025” or “Community Health Officer Merit List”.
  4. Click the link to open the merit list, which is usually published in PDF format.
  5. Use the search option (Ctrl + F) to find your name, roll number, or registration number in the list.
  6. Verify your details carefully.
  7. Download the PDF to your device.
  8. Take a printout of the merit list for future reference during document verification or further selection rounds.
