BIHAR STET 2025 ONLINE FORM

Post Date: September 10, 2025

The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has released the official notification for the State Teacher Eligibility Test (STET) 2025. The online application process is set to begin tomorrow, 11 September 2025, and will conclude on 19 September 2025. This eligibility test is a mandatory requirement for candidates aspiring to become secondary and senior secondary teachers in Bihar. All interested and eligible candidates can find the complete details regarding the application process, fees, eligibility criteria, and important links below.

BIHAR STET EXAMINATION 2025 : SHORT DETAILS

Important Dates

  • Online Apply Start Date: 11 September 2025
  • Online Apply Last Date: 19 September 2025
  • Exam Date: 04 – 25 October 2025
  • Admit Card: Before Exam
  • Result Date: 01 November 2025

Application Fee

  • For Paper I OR II:
  • Gen/EWS/BC/EBC: ₹ 960/-
  • SC/ST/PWD: ₹ 760/-
  • For Both Paper I & II:
  • Gen/EWS/BC/EBC: ₹ 1440/-
  • SC/ST/PWD: ₹ 1140/-
BSEB BIHAR STET NOTIFICATION 2025 : AGE LIMITS AS ON 01 AUGUST 2025
Minimum Age 21 Years
Maximum Age (Male) 37 Years
Maximum Age (Female) 40 Years
Age relaxation is provided as per BSEB regulations.
BIHAR STET RECRUITMENT 2025 : ELIGIBILITY DETAILS
Paper Level Eligibility Criteria
Secondary Teacher (Class 9–10, Paper-I)
  • Bachelor’s degree in the relevant subject with at least 50% marks.
  • B.Ed. degree is mandatory.
Senior Secondary Teacher (Class 11–12, Paper-II)
  • Postgraduate degree in the relevant subject with Second Division marks.
  • B.Ed. degree is mandatory (not required for Computer Science).
Note: SC/ST/EBC/BC/Divyang categories get a 5% relaxation in marks.
HOW TO FILL BIHAR STET ONLINE FORM 2025

Interested candidates can submit their application online between 11 September 2025 and 19 September 2025 through the official BSEB STET website. Candidates are strongly advised to read the official notification carefully before filling out the form to ensure they meet all eligibility criteria (Last Date, Age Limit, & Education Qualification).

Bihar STET Online Form 2025 : Important Questions

Question: When will the online application for Bihar STET 2025 start?
Answer: The online application for this recruitment will start on 11 September 2025.

Question: What is the last date for the Bihar STET Online Form 2025?
Answer: The last date for online application is 19 September 2025.

Question: What is the age limit for the Bihar STET Bharti 2025?
Answer: The age limit is 21-37 for Male (UR), with relaxations for female and reserved categories up to 42 years.

Question: What is the eligibility for the Bihar STET Vacancy 2025?
Answer: For Paper I (Class 9-10), a Bachelor's degree with B.Ed is required. For Paper II (Class 11-12), a Postgraduate degree with B.Ed is required.

Question: What is the official website for Bihar BSEB?
Answer: The official website is https://secondary.biharboardonline.com/.
