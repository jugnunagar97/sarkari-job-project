How To Check & Download Bihar STET Exam Date & Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official Bihar STET / BSEB website: Use your browser to open the official site where STET notices are posted (BSEB or STET portal).
  • Go to “Notifications / News / Admit Card / Latest Updates”: On the homepage, find the section for Notifications, News, or Admit Card / Hall Ticket.
  • Look for the “STET 2025 Exam Date / Schedule / Admit Card” link: In that section, find a link that announces exam dates or a direct link to download the Admit Card / Hall Ticket.
  • Click on that link / notification: The link will usually open a PDF or a login page for admit card download.
  • If it’s a login page, enter your credentials: Enter your Application Number / Registration Number and Date of Birth / Password (or other asked details).
  • Submit / View Admit Card: After submitting, your admit card will appear. Check the exam dates, timings, and center details.
  • Download / Save the Admit Card PDF: Use “Download / Save As / Export to PDF” option in your browser to save the admit card to your device.
  • Check the exam schedule / dates given: In the admit card or notification PDF, find the exam date(s), timing, and shift details.
  • Print a hard copy: Print at least one clear copy of the admit card for the exam day.
  • Verify all the details: Ensure your name, roll number, photograph, exam center, date & shift etc. are correct. If anything is wrong, contact the exam authority immediately.

Bihar STET 2025 Admit Card & Exam Schedule Update

Bihar STET Exam Confirmed for 14 October 2025

The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has officially released the schedule for the Bihar STET 2025 (State Teacher Eligibility Test). The highly anticipated computer-based test (CBT) examination is scheduled to commence on 14 October 2025. Candidates who successfully completed their application by the extended deadline of 05 October 2025 can expect to download their Admit Card from 11 October 2025. It is critical that all candidates check the official notice and download their hall ticket immediately upon activation of the link to confirm their exact shift and test city.

Last-Minute Preparation for Bihar STET (Paper I & II)

High-Priority Revision for Bihar STET Paper I and Paper II

With the exam date of 14 October 2025 approaching rapidly, candidates must focus their preparation on intensive revision. The STET involves two papers: **Paper I (Secondary Teacher)** and **Paper II (Senior Secondary Teacher)**, testing subject knowledge and general teaching aptitude. Since Paper I requires a **Bachelor's degree with B.Ed.** and Paper II requires a **Postgraduate degree with B.Ed.**, concentrate on your specific subject's core curriculum, teaching methodology, and the mandatory General Knowledge and Aptitude sections. Practice mock tests in a CBT environment to acclimatize to the online examination format and ensure time management is efficient.

Understanding Bihar STET Eligibility and Relaxation Rules

Important Eligibility Criteria for Secondary and Senior Secondary Posts

The Bihar STET 2025 determines eligibility for teaching positions across Bihar. Candidates should note the detailed eligibility: a minimum of **50% marks** in the relevant degree (Bachelor's for Paper I, Postgraduate for Paper II) is mandatory, along with a **B.Ed. qualification** (except for certain specialized subjects like Computer Science). Significantly, the official notification provides a **5% relaxation** in the minimum qualifying marks for candidates belonging to the **SC/ST/EBC/BC/Divyang** categories. Always cross-verify your academic qualifications with the STET official notification before preparing your documents for the next stage.

Mandatory Requirements for Bihar STET CBT Examination

Checklist: Essential Documents for STET Exam Day

Since the **Bihar STET** is conducted in a **Computer Based Test (CBT) mode**, candidates must ensure they are fully prepared for the online format. For entry on **14 October 2025**, the following documents are strictly mandatory: **1. Bihar STET Admit Card:** A clear printout downloaded from the official portal. **2. Valid Photo ID:** The original photo identity proof used during registration. **3. Passport-Size Photograph:** At least one photograph matching the one uploaded on the application. Candidates must adhere to the **reporting time** printed on the Admit Card (available from 11 October 2025) to complete the security and biometric verification process before the exam begins.
