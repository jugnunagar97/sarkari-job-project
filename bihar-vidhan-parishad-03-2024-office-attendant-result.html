BIHAR VIDHAN PARISHAD 03/2024 OFFICE ATTENDANT RESULT 2025

Post Date: September 18, 2025

The Bihar Vidhan Parishad (Bihar Legislative Council) has released the Result for the Office Attendant posts (Night Watchman, Darban, Safaikarmi) under Advt. No. 03/2024 on its official website, vidhanparishad.bihar.gov.in. The recruitment covers 26 vacancies, with applications accepted from September 18–27, 2024. The written exam was conducted on August 11–16, 2025. Candidates can download the result PDF for their respective post (Night Watchman, Darban, or Safaikarmi) using the links below. Qualified candidates are eligible for document verification, details of which are available in the DV notice. Stay updated at sarkaariresult.org for further updates.

STEPS TO CHECK & DOWNLOAD BIHAR VIDHAN PARISHAD OFFICE ATTENDANT 03/2024 RESULT 2025
  1. Visit the official Bihar Vidhan Parishad website at vidhanparishad.bihar.gov.in.
  2. Locate the “Recruitment” or “Notice Board” section on the homepage.
  3. Find and click on the link titled “Office Attendant 03/2024 Result 2025” for Night Watchman, Darban, or Safaikarmi.
  4. The result will open as a PDF file listing the roll numbers and names of qualified candidates.
  5. Use Ctrl + F to search for your Roll Number or Name in the PDF.
  6. Download the PDF and save it for future reference.
  7. Print a hard copy for document verification and record-keeping.
