Bihar Vidhan Sabha 05/2024 Mains Admit Card 2025

Post Date: August 29, 2025 1:35 PM

Bihar Vidhan Sabha Sachivalaya, has released the Mains Admit Card on its official website for the Recruitment of Examination of Library Assistant, Urdu Translator, Urdu Assistant & Translator (Hindi/ English) 2025. The exam will be conducted on August 2025, and the last date to submit the application form was 25 August 2025. Candidates can check their Mains Admit Card using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the portal. The direct link is provided below.

How To Check & Download Bihar Vidhan Sabha 05/2024 Mains Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official website of Bihar Vidhan Sabha: vidhansabha.bih.nic.in
  • On the homepage, go to the Recruitment / Admit Card section.
  • Find the link for “Admit Card for 05/2024 Mains Examination 2025”.
  • Click on the link and enter your Registration Number & Password/Date of Birth.
  • Your admit card will appear on the screen.
  • Download it and take a printout for the examination.
