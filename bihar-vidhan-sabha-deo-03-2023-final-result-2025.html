Bihar Vidhan Sabha DEO 03/2023 Final Result 2025

Post Date: September 22, 2025

The Bihar Vidhan Sabha (BVS), Government of Bihar, has announced the DEO 03/2023 Final Result 2025 for the Data Entry Operator recruitment on its official website. The typing test was held on 14 September 2025, with applications closing on 21 January 2024 (Advt. No. 02/2023, 03/2023, 04/2023 & 05/2023). A total of 180 posts are available across various roles including Security Guard, DEO, Driver, and Office Attendant. Candidates can check their final merit list and selection status using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth at vidhansabha.bihar.gov.in. Direct links to download the results and related documents are provided below.

Post NameEligibility Criteria / Age Limit
Security GuardQualification: Intermediate pass from a recognized board. Physical Standards: Male: 1.6 km in 6 mins, height 167.5 cm, chest 76.5-81 cm, vision 6/12. Female: 1 km in 6 mins, height 154.6 cm, vision 6/12. Age: 18-25 years as of 01-01-2024.
Data Entry Operator (DEO)Qualification: 10+2 from a recognized board or council approved by State or Central Government. Typing Speed: 8000 key depressions per hour on a computer. Age Limit: 18-37 years as of 01-08-2023.
DriverQualification: Passed Class 10th (High School) Exam. License: Valid LMV/HMV driving license. Age Limit: 18-37 years as of 01-08-2023.
Office Attendant (OA)Qualification: Passed Class 10th (High School) or its equivalent. Age Limit: 18-37 years as of 01-08-2023.

  • Caste certificate (if applicable, for SC/ST/OBC/EWS).

How to Check & Download Bihar Vidhan Sabha DEO 03/2023 Final Result 2025
  • Visit the Official Website: Go to the official Bihar Vidhan Sabha website at vidhansabha.bihar.gov.in.
  • Go to the Recruitment/Results Section: On the homepage, click on the “Recruitment” or “Results” tab.
  • Find the Result Link: Look for the link titled “DEO (Advt. No. 03/2023) Final Result 2025”.
  • Open the Result PDF: The final result is published in PDF format containing roll numbers/names of selected candidates.
  • Check Your Roll Number/Name: Use the Ctrl + F (Find option) to quickly search for your Roll Number / Name.
  • Download the Result: Save the PDF result file on your device for future use.
  • Take a Printout: Keep a hard copy of the result for documentation and joining formalities.
