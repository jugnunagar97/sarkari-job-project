BIHAR VIDHAN SABHA JUNIOR CLERK 02/2024 RESULT 2025

Post Date: August 13, 2025

Bihar Vidhan Sabha Sachivalaya has released the result for the recruitment of Junior Clerk posts against Advt. No. 02/2024. This recruitment drive includes a total of 142 vacancies. The exam was conducted on 27 July 2025. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now check their results by logging into the official portal using their credentials. The direct link is provided below.

Important Dates

Application Start: 29 January 2024

Last Date for Registration: 15 February 2024

Re-Open Last Date: 13 December 2024

Junior Clerk Exam Date: 27 July 2025

Admit Card: 18 July 2025

Result Declared Date: 24 April 2025

How To Check & Download Bihar Vidhan Sabha Junior Clerk Result 2025
  1. Visit the official website of the Bihar Vidhan Sabha.
  2. Find and click on the “Junior Clerk 02/2024 Result 2025” link on the homepage.
  3. A new page will open with the result PDF or a login portal.
  4. If login is required, enter your roll number/registration number and other required details.
  5. View your result on the screen.
  6. Download and save it for future reference.
