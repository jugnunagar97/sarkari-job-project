Understanding Bihar Vidhan Sabha Selection Process and Examination Structure

Complete Guide to BVS Recruitment Stages and Multi-Tier Assessment Framework

The Bihar Vidhan Sabha selection process follows comprehensive multi-tier assessment framework beginning with written examinations conducted in February-April 2025 testing general knowledge, reasoning abilities, numerical aptitude, and Hindi language proficiency relevant to government secretariat functions. Successful candidates advanced to specialized skill tests including typing tests for Junior Clerk positions and stenography tests for Reporter, Personal Assistant, and Stenographer positions.

Following skill test completion, Bihar Vidhan Sabha Secretariat released final results with category-wise cutoff marks on 24 October 2025 enabling candidates to verify qualifying status. The typing test for Junior Clerk conducted on 15 September 2025 tested Hindi and English typing speed meeting prescribed standards. Shortlisted candidates undergo document verification confirming educational credentials, skill test certificates, age proof, and category documents before medical examination.

Selection Process Breakdown:

  • Written Examination: Computer-based test covering general awareness, reasoning, numerical ability, and Hindi language comprehension with negative marking for incorrect responses
  • Typing Test: Assessment of typing speed in Hindi and English meeting prescribed standards of 30-35 words per minute for respective positions
  • Stenography Test: Evaluation of stenographic skills in Hindi for Reporter (150 WPM), Personal Assistant (100 WPM), and Stenographer (80 WPM) positions
  • Document Verification: Scrutiny of original educational certificates, skill test certificates, category certificates, and identity proof before medical examination and appointment processing

Document verification stage requires candidates presenting original educational certificates including graduation degrees for officer positions, intermediate certificates for clerical positions, typing/stenography certificates, identity proof, category certificate if applicable, and residence proof. Post-verification, final appointment orders are issued for successful candidates joining Bihar Legislative Assembly Secretariat.

About Bihar Vidhan Sabha Secretariat and Legislative Assembly Operations

Understanding Bihar Legislative Assembly Structure and Administrative Support Framework

The Bihar Vidhan Sabha operates as unicameral state legislature comprising 243 elected members representing constituencies across Bihar governing legislative affairs, budget approvals, and policy deliberations. The Legislative Assembly Secretariat provides comprehensive administrative, technical, and support services facilitating smooth assembly operations including session management, documentation, stenographic services, clerical support, and facility management for members and officials.

Bihar Legislative Assembly Secretariat maintains sophisticated infrastructure requiring qualified administrative officers, clerks, stenographers, reporters, and support staff ensuring efficient operations. The administrative framework encompasses sections handling assembly proceedings, documentation, member services, finance, personnel, and general administration requiring competent professionals across hierarchical levels supporting legislative functions effectively.

Bihar Vidhan Sabha Operations:

  • Administrative operations requiring Assistant Section Officers managing sections, coordinating activities, handling correspondence, and ensuring administrative efficiency across secretariat departments supporting assembly functions
  • Clerical support through Junior Clerks handling documentation, file maintenance, correspondence typing, data entry, and general office duties ensuring smooth workflow across sections
  • Stenographic services through Reporters, Personal Assistants, and Stenographers providing verbatim reporting, stenography support, and documentation assistance for assembly proceedings and official communications
  • Support services through library attendants and office attendants ensuring facility maintenance, library operations, and general housekeeping supporting conducive working environment for members and officials

Bihar Vidhan Sabha employees across clerical, administrative, and support positions play crucial roles supporting democratic governance through efficient administrative operations, accurate documentation, and effective service delivery.
