BITSAT Admit Card 2025

Post Date: May 24, 2025

The Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS), Pilani has released the Admit Card for the Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test (BITSAT) 2025. The application process was open from 21 January 2025 to 18 April 2025. The BITSAT exam will be conducted in two sessions: 26-30 May 2025 for Session 1 and 22-26 June 2025 for Session 2. Candidates can download their admit card by logging into the official portal using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth.

BITSAT Admissions 2025: Eligibility Criteria
  • Candidates must have passed 12th with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, scoring at least 75% overall in PCM and 60% in each subject individually.
  • They must also qualify the BITSAT with a score meeting the required cut-off.
  • Candidates should read the official guidelines before applying.
How to Check & Download BITSAT Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official BITS Pilani admissions website: https://www.bitsadmission.com.
  • Click on the link labeled “Download BITSAT 2025 Hall Ticket”.
  • Log in using your Application Number and Password.
  • Once logged in, click on the “BITS Admission Test Ticket” tab.
  • Your admit card will appear on the screen.
  • Download and print the admit card for future reference.
