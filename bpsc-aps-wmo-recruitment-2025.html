BPSC Assistant Public Sanitary & Waste Management Officer Recruitment 2025

Post Date: September 10, 2025 4:38 PM

The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC), under the Urban Development and Housing Department, Government of Bihar, has released the Recruitment Notification 2025 under Advertisement No. 108/2025 for 54 vacancies for the post of Assistant Public Sanitary & Waste Management Officer. The online application process started on 5 September 2025 and will close on 30 September 2025. This recruitment aims to strengthen urban sanitation and waste management infrastructure by inducting skilled professionals with science or engineering backgrounds. Candidates can apply through the official website, www.bpsc.bihar.gov.in. Below are the complete details, including eligibility, application process, and selection criteria. Candidates are advised to read the official notification carefully before applying.

How to Fill BPSC APS & WMO Online Form 2025

  • How to access the BPSC website?

    Visit the official BPSC website: www.bpsc.bihar.gov.in.

  • Where to find the recruitment section?

    Navigate to the “Recruitment” section and select Advertisement No. 108/2025.

  • How to register?

    Complete One-Time Registration (OTR) using a valid email ID and mobile number to create login credentials.

  • How to fill the form?

    Log in, select the Assistant Public Sanitary & Waste Management Officer post, and enter personal, educational, and professional details.

  • What documents to upload?

    Upload a recent passport-size photo, signature, and required certificates (e.g., degree, caste certificate, Aadhaar).

  • How to pay the fee?

    Pay the application fee (₹100, plus ₹200 biometric fee if Aadhaar not used) online via Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI.

  • How to submit?

    Review the application, submit it, and take a printout of the confirmation page.

BPSC APS & WMO Recruitment 2025: Mode of Selection

  • What is the selection process?

    The selection process consists of a Written Examination (Paper-I and Paper-II) followed by the preparation of a Merit List based on marks obtained.

  • Is there an interview?

    No, there is no interview for this post. Selection is based solely on the written examination.
    • [](https://www.engineersacademy.org/blog/bpsc-assistant-public-sanitary-and-waste-management-officer-recruitment/)

  • What is the final step?

    Final Merit List based on the total marks (200) from Paper-I and Paper-II, with minimum qualifying marks as per category.
