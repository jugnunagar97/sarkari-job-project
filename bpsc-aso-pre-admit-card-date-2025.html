Bihar Vidhan Parishad ABO Mains Exam 2025 – Cancelled

Post Date: August 30, 2025

The Bihar Vidhan Parishad (Bihar Legislative Council) has released the Mains Admit Card for the post of Assistant Branch Officer (ABO). This recruitment is for 26 posts, and applications were accepted from September 18 to 27, 2024. The written exam (Stage-II) was scheduled for May 23, 2025, but has been cancelled. Candidates appearing in this stage can download their Mains Admit Card from the direct link given below.

Bihar Vidhan Parishad Office Attendant 2024: Educational Qualification
  • Candidates who have passed the Class 10 examination from any recognized board in India are eligible for this recruitment.
  • They must have knowledge of both English and Hindi languages.
  • The ability to ride a bicycle is also required.
How to Check & Download Bihar Vidhan Parishad Office Attendant 03/2024 Answer Key 2025
  • Go to the official Bihar Vidhan Sabha website vidhanparishad.bihar.gov.in.
  • Look for the link related to Office Attendant 03/2024 Answer Key 2025 on the homepage.
  • Click on the link and log in using your Registration Number and Password/Date of Birth.
  • The Answer Key PDF will appear on the screen.
  • Download it and, if needed, take a printout for future reference.
