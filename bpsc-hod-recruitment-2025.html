BPSC HOD Recruitment 2025

Post Date: August 30, 2025 12:31 PM

Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC), The notification for the recruitment of Head of Department (HOD) Recruitment has released on the official website. This recruitment is for 218 positions. The BPSC application form will start from 02 September 2025, and candidates can apply until 30 September 2025. The minimum age is 33 Years & the maximum age is not required as of 01 August 2025.
