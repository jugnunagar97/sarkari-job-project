BPSC School Teacher TRE 4.0 Recruitment 2025

Post Date: September 6, 2025

The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released a notification for the recruitment of School Teachers under the TRE 4.0 examination, offering 27,910 positions across various levels (Class 1 to 12). The online application process is set to begin in September 2025 and will close in September 2025. The minimum age is 18 years for Classes 1-8 and 21 years for Classes 9-12, with a maximum age of 37 years for UR males, 40 years for UR females and BC/EBC, and 42 years for SC/ST as of August 1, 2025, with relaxations as per BPSC rules. Candidates should apply via the official website bpsc.bih.nic.in and verify all details.

BPSC School Teacher TRE 4.0 Notification 2025: Age Limits (As on 01 August 2025)

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years (Class 1 to 5 & 6 to 8)
  • Minimum Age: 21 Years (Class 9 to 10 & 11 to 12)
  • Maximum Age: 37 Years (UR-Male)
  • Maximum Age: 40 Years (UR-Female)
  • Maximum Age: 40 Years (BC/EBC-Male & Female)
  • Maximum Age: 42 Years (SC/ST-Male & Female)
  • Age Relaxation: As per BPSC regulations for Special School TRE 4.0 positions.

BPSC School Teacher TRE 4.0 Online Form 2025: Eligibility Criteria
  • School Teacher (Class 1 to 5): Bachelor Degree with 50% Marks and B.Ed Degree OR Bachelor Degree with Diploma in Elementary Education OR 10+2 Inter with 50% Marks with 2 Year Diploma in Elementary Education/Special Diploma OR 10+2 Inter with 45% Marks (As per 2002 Norms) with 2 Year Diploma in Elementary Education OR 10+2 Inter with 50% Marks with 4 Year BLEd Degree OR Master Degree with 55% Marks and B.Ed-M.Ed 3 Year Degree. CTET Paper I or BTET Paper I Exam Qualified.
  • Middle School Teacher (Class 6 to 8): Graduate with 2 Year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.Ed) OR Graduate/Post Graduate with 50% Marks with B.Ed OR Graduate with 45% Marks and B.Ed (NCTE Norms) OR Graduate with 50% Marks and BA BEd or B.Sc Ed OR Graduate with 50% Marks and B.Ed Special OR Post Graduate with 55% Marks and 3 Year B.Ed-M.Ed Course. Refer to official notification for more details.
  • School Teacher (Class 6 to 10): Graduate/Post Graduate in Related Subject with Minimum 50% Marks and B.Ed Degree OR Graduate/Post Graduate with Minimum 45% Marks (As per 2002 Norms) and B.Ed Degree OR 4 Year Degree in BAEd/BScEd. STET Paper I Exam Passed. Refer to official notification for more details.
  • Secondary School Teacher (TGT) (Class 9 to 10): Graduate/Post Graduate in Related Subject with Minimum 50% Marks and B.Ed Degree OR Graduate/Post Graduate with Minimum 45% Marks (As per 2002 Norms) and B.Ed Degree OR 4 Year Degree in BAEd/BScEd. STET Paper I Exam Passed (Subject Wise). Refer to official notification for more details.
  • Secondary School Teacher (TGT)-Special (Class 9 to 10): Graduate/Post Graduate in Related Subject with Minimum 50% Marks and B.Ed Degree OR Graduate/Post Graduate with Minimum 45% Marks (As per 2002 Norms) and B.Ed Degree OR 4 Year Degree in BAEd/BScEd. STET Paper I Exam Passed (Subject Wise).
  • 10+2 School Teacher (PGT) (Class 11 to 12): Post Graduate in Related Subject with Minimum 50% Marks and B.Ed Degree OR Post Graduate with Minimum 45% Marks (As per 2002 Norms) and B.Ed Degree OR Post Graduate with 4 Year Degree in BAEd/BScEd OR Master Degree with 55% Marks and B.Ed-M.Ed 3 Year Degree. STET Paper II Exam Passed. Refer to official notification for more details.

BPSC School Teacher TRE 4.0 Online Form 2025: Required Documents
  • Photo: A passport-sized colored photo with a white or light-colored background.
  • Signature: Clear signature on white paper using a black or blue pen.
  • Educational Certificates: 10+2 (Intermediate) or equivalent from a recognized board, or higher qualification with necessary documents. Refer to official notification for details.
  • Caste Certificate: For reserved category candidates.
  • Aadhaar Card for ID Proof: Aadhaar card, Voter ID, or any other government-issued ID.
  • Domicile Certificate: Proof of residence for candidates from Bihar.
  • Income Certificate: For candidates applying under the Economically Weaker Section (EWS) category.
  • Other Certificates: Certificates for special categories (e.g., PH, Ex-servicemen) if applicable.
