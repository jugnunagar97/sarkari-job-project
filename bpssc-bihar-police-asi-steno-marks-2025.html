BPSSC Bihar Police ASI Steno Marks 2025

Post Date: September 27, 2025 | Updated on: October 9, 2025 at 10:00 AM IST

The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released the Marks on its official website for the recruitment of Steno Assistant Sub Inspector (ASI). This recruitment is for 305 positions. The BPSSC application forms were filled out from December 17, 2024, to January 17, 2025. The Bihar Police exam was conducted in April 15-17, 2025. Candidates can check their Marks by logging into the portal using their enrollment number, registration number, or date of birth.

BPSSC Steno ASI Examination 2025: Eligibility Criteria
  • Candidates must have passed Class 12th/HSC from a recognized board.
  • Must have a 1-year Diploma Certificate in Computers.
  • Hindi Stenographer: Speed of 80 words per minute (WPM).
  • Computer Typing: Proficiency in both Hindi and English with a typing speed of 30 WPM, completing 300 words in 10 minutes.
  • Computer Test: Should have good knowledge of MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and the Internet.
How To Check & Download Bihar Police BPSSC ASI Steno 01/2024 Marks 2025
  • Visit the Official Website: Go to the BPSSC official website: https://bpssc.bihar.gov.in.
  • Go to the ‘Results’ Section: On the homepage, click on the “Marks” tab.
  • Find the Relevant Result Link: Look for the link titled “Marks: Result of Eligibility Test for the post of Steno Assistant Sub-Inspector in Home (Police) Dept., Govt. of Bihar (Advt. No. 01/2024)” and click on it.
  • Download the Marks PDF: The marks will be displayed in a PDF format. Download and save the PDF for your reference.
  • Search for Your Roll Number: Open the downloaded PDF and use the Ctrl + F function to search for your Roll Number in the list of qualified candidates.
