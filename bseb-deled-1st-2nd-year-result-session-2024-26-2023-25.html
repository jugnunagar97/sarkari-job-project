BSEB DELED 1ST & 2ND YEAR RESULT (SESSION 2024-26 & 2023-25)

Post Date: September 5, 2025

The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) in Patna has officially released the results for the Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed) 1st and 2nd Year examinations. This announcement covers the 2023-25 and 2024-26 academic sessions, held after the Combined Entrance Examination 2025. Aspiring candidates can now check their results by visiting the official board portal. To access your scores, you'll need to log in using your Enrollment number, Registration number, or Date of birth. All pertinent information and links are available below.

HOW TO CHECK & DOWNLOAD BSEB DELED 1ST & 2ND YEAR RESULT
  1. Go to the Official Website: Start by opening the official portal of the Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB).
  2. Find the Results Area: On the main page, locate and click on the "Result Section" to proceed.
  3. Select the Correct Link: Find the specific link labeled “D.El.Ed 1st & 2nd Year Result 2025” and select it.
  4. Provide Your Details: You will be taken to a login page where you must input your Roll Number, Roll Code, and Date of Birth.
  5. Submit to View: After entering your credentials, click on the "Submit" or "View Result" button.
  6. See Your Result: Your D.El.Ed 1st or 2nd-year result will then be shown on the screen.
  7. Download and Print: Be sure to save a PDF version of your result or print a physical copy for future needs.
