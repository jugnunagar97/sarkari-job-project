BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha 3rd Result with Score Card 2025

Post Date: September 9, 2025 11:06 AM

The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has released the Sakshamta Pariksha 3rd Result with Score Card 2025 for the Competency Test for Local Bodies Teacher (CTT) on its official website, secondary.biharboardonline.com. The exam, conducted from 23-25 July 2025, assesses the eligibility of Niyojit Shikshaks (appointed teachers) for classes 1-12 and Physical Education Teachers. Applications were accepted from 22 February to 23 March 2025. The result, announced on 4 September 2025, includes a merit list and scorecard, with a pass percentage of approximately 32.3% (7,893 out of 24,436 candidates qualified). Candidates can download their scorecard using their Application Number and Date of Birth at cdn3.digialm.com. Check the official website for updates.

How to Check & Download BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha 3rd Result 2025

  • How to access the BSEB website?

    Visit the official website: secondary.biharboardonline.com.

  • Where to find the result?

    On the homepage, locate the “Sakshamta Pariksha 3rd Result 2025” link or navigate to the “Result” section.

  • How to log in?

    Use your Application Number/Roll Number and Date of Birth at cdn3.digialm.com, and enter the captcha code if prompted.

  • How to download the result/scorecard?

    Click “View Result” or “Submit,” then download the result PDF or scorecard and print multiple copies for reference.

  • How to check the merit list?

    Download the PDF for your class category (Class 1-5, 6-8, 9-10, 11-12, or Physical Teacher) and search for your roll number.
BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha-III 2025: Mode of Selection

  • What is the selection process?

    Selection is based on a Computer-Based Test (CBT) with objective questions covering Language, General Studies, and Subject Knowledge.

  • What is the exam structure?

    The exam consists of 150 MCQs (30 Language, 40 General Studies, 80 Subject Knowledge), conducted over 2 hours 30 minutes, with no negative marking.

  • What happens after passing?

    Qualified candidates are eligible to teach in their respective schools under Bihar’s local bodies, subject to verification.
