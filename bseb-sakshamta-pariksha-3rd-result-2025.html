Bihar Sakshamta Pariksha 3rd Result 2025: FAQs

Question: What were the overall pass statistics for the BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha Phase 3 Exam?

Answer: A total of 24,436 teachers appeared for the examination across all class levels. Of these, 7,893 candidates successfully qualified, bringing the overall pass percentage to 32.3%. This result includes all candidates for Class 1-5, 6-8, 9-10, 11-12, and the Physical Teacher category.

Question: Can you provide a breakdown of the results for each class level?

Answer: Yes, the pass percentage varied by class level. Here is the breakdown:

  • Class 1–5: Out of 21,157 teachers who appeared, 6,144 qualified, with a pass rate of 29.04%.
  • Class 6–8: A total of 1,802 teachers appeared, and 949 qualified, for a pass rate of 52.66%.
  • Class 9–10: 594 out of 1,076 teachers passed, with a pass rate of 55.20%.
  • Class 11–12: The pass rate was 51.37%, with 206 out of 401 teachers qualifying.

Question: When were the Phase 3 results for the Competency Test for Teachers declared?

Answer: The results for the Competency Test for Teachers (CTT) Phase 3 were officially announced on the BSEB website on September 4, 2025. The examination was conducted in July 2025.

Question: What details are included in the Bihar Sakshamta Pariksha 3rd Result 2025?

Answer: The result includes a scorecard, a merit list, and important instructions for successful candidates. The result is available in PDF format, which lists the roll numbers of all shortlisted candidates.

Question: How can I download my Bihar Sakshamta Pariksha Phase 3 Result?

Answer: You can download your result by following these simple steps:

  1. Begin by navigating to the official Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) website: secondary.biharboardonline.com or bsebsakshamta.com.
  2. Alternatively, you can click on the direct link provided on the page above.
  3. After clicking the link, a PDF file containing the results for classes 1-12 will appear on your screen.
  4. Check your roll number in the PDF to see your qualifying status.
  5. You can also check your individual qualifying status by logging in with your mobile number and password on the portal.

Question: What is the purpose of the BSEB Competency Test for Teachers (CTT)?

Answer: The BSEB CTT, also called the Bihar Sakshamta Pariksha, is an eligibility exam for Niyojit Shikshaks (contractual teachers) working in Bihar's local body schools. Those who clear the exam are eligible to teach in the respective schools in which they are appointed.

Question: What is the schedule for the next phase of the exam?

Answer: The schedule for Phase 4 is as follows:

  • Applications: September 6 to September 11, 2025
  • Admit Card: September 17, 2025
  • Exam Dates: September 23 to September 25, 2025
  • A fifth and final test will be conducted soon after Phase 4.
