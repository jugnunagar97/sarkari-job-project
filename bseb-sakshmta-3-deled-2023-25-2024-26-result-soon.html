BSEB Sakshamta 3 and DElEd Results 2025 to be Released Today

Published: September 4, 2025

BSEB Sakshamta 3, DElEd 2023-25 & 2024-26 Result 2025: The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) is scheduled to release the results for the Sakshamta Examination 3 today. Board Chairman Anand Kishore will announce the results during a press conference at 2:45 PM. Candidates who appeared for the exam can check their results on the official website once they are declared.

Along with the Sakshamta 3 results, the board will also release the results for the D.El.Ed second-year (session 2023-25) and first-year (session 2024-26) exams. Although a link for the D.El.Ed result is already available on the website, it is not yet active. It is expected to be activated shortly.

Sakshamta Examination 3 Was Held in July

The BSEB conducted the Sakshamta Examination 3 on July 24 and 25, 2025. The answer key for the exam was released in August, and candidates were given the opportunity to challenge it from August 13 to 15. The final results are now being announced on September 4th.

Where to Check Your Results

The results will be announced by Board Chairman Anand Kishore at 2:45 PM. After the announcement, candidates can check their scores on the official websites:

  • For **D.El.Ed course results**, visit: dledsecondary.biharboardonline.com
  • For the **Sakshamta Examination 3 result**, visit: secondary.biharboardonline.com
