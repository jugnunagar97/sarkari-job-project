BSF Head Constable RO/ RM Recruitment 2025: Vacancy Details
Post Name No. Of Post Eligibility Criteria
Head Constable (RO) 910 Candidates must have passed 10th or equivalent along with a 2-year ITI in Radio & Television, Electronics, Computer Operator & Programming Assistant, Data Preparation, or a related trade. Alternatively, candidates who have passed 12th or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics are also eligible.
Head Constable (RM) 211 Candidates must have passed 10th or equivalent with a 2-year ITI in Radio & Television, General Electronics, Computer Operator & Programming Assistant, or a related trade. Alternatively, candidates who have passed 12th or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics are also eligible.
