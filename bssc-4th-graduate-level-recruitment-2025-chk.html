Bihar BSSC 4th Graduate Level Recruitment 2025

Post Date: August 25, 2025 8:05 PM

Bihar Staff Selection Commission (BSSC), has released a notification on official website for the recruitment of 4th Graduate Level Post. This recruitment is for 1481 positions. BSSC Application Form has started on 25 August 2025 & the candidates can apply till the 24 September 2025. Minimum age required is 18 Years & The Maximum Age Is 37 Years as on 01 August 2025.
