CBSE BOARD CBSE JR. ASSISTANT FINAL RESULT 2025

Post Date: August 30, 2025

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has announced the Final Result for the Junior Assistant & Superintendent examinations 2025. The CBSE Tier-2 Exam was conducted earlier this year. Candidates who participated in the recruitment process can now check their final results on the official portal using their login credentials. The direct link is provided below.

CBSE Jr. Assistant & Superintendent 2025: Vacancy & Eligibility
Post Name No. of Posts Eligibility Criteria
Junior Assistant 70
  • 12th pass from a recognized board.
  • Typing speed of 35 wpm in English or 30 wpm in Hindi on a computer.
Superintendent 142
  • Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university.
  • Minimum 3 years of administrative experience in an educational institution.
How To Check & Download CBSE Jr. Assistant Final Result 2025
  1. Visit the official CBSE website: cbse.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the "Recruitment" or "Results" section.
  3. Look for the link titled “Junior Assistant Final Result 2025”.
  4. Click on the link, and a PDF file will open.
  5. Use the search function (Ctrl+F) to find your Name or Roll Number.
  6. Download and save the PDF for your records.
