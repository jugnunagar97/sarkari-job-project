CBSE Board Class 10th / 12th Exam Date Sheet 2025-26

Post Date: September 24, 2025 | Updated on: September 27, 2025 at 02:20 PM IST

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has released the official tentative exam date sheet for the CBSE Class 10th and 12th session 2025-2026 on its official website. CBSE Board 10th Exam will be held from 17 February 2026 to 09 March 2026 and the CBSE Class 12th Exam will be held from 17 February 2026 to 09 April 2026. All the candidates who had registered for class X and class XII of the year 2025-26 can download their Exam Date Sheet from the link given below.

CBSE Board 10th / 12th Annual Board Examination 2025-26: Short Details

Important Dates

  • CBSE Board Exam Date Sheet Issued: 24 September 2025
  • Class 10th Annual Exam Date: 17 February 2026 to 09 March 2026
  • Class 12th Annual Exam Date: 17 February 2026 to 09 April 2026
  • Note: Candidates are advised to confirm from the CBSE official website.

Application Fee

  • No Application Fees

CBSE Board 10th / 12th Examination 2025-26: Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Attendance Requirement: Students must have at least 75% attendance in their respective classes to be eligible for the board exams. In cases of genuine reasons such as medical emergencies or participation in national/international events, schools may grant relaxation upon submission of valid documents.
  • Passing Marks: A minimum of 33% marks is required in each subject to pass. For subjects involving practicals, students must secure 33% separately in both theory and practical components.
  • Internal Assessments: Students must obtain a grade higher than ‘E’ in all internal assessments. Failure to do so may result in withholding of the external examination results.
  • Compartment Exams: Students failing in one or two subjects are eligible to appear for compartment exams scheduled in May or June 2026.
  • Subject Selection: Students are required to finalize their subject choices during the submission of the List of Candidates (LOC). Changes to subject choices after LOC submission are generally not permitted.

How to Check & Download CBSE Board Class 10th / 12th Exam Date Sheet 2025-26
  • Go to the official result portal: cbse.gov.in
  • Look for the link labeled “CBSE Board Class 10th / 12th Exam Date Sheet 2025-26” on the homepage.
  • Click the link to open the login page.
  • Enter the following credentials (as printed on your supplementary exam admit card): Roll Number, School Number, Admit Card ID
  • Pass the security pin verification, then click Submit.
  • Your result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and save the marksheet (scorecard) as a PDF.
  • Take a printout and keep it safe until you receive your original CBSE marksheet from your school.
