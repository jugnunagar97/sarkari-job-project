Central Bank Credit Officer Final Result 2025

Post Date: August 27, 2025 3:52 PM

Central Bank of India, has released the Final Result for the recruitment of Credit Officer in Mainstream (General Banking) in Junior Management Grade Scale I Upon PGDBF Recruitment on its official website. The CBI application process started on 30th January 2025, and candidates were able to submit their applications until 10th March 2025. The exam was conducted in 05th April 2025. Candidates can check and download their Final Result by logging into the portal (link provided below) using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number.

  1. Visit the official website of Central Bank of India: centralbankofindia.co.in
  2. On the homepage, open the Recruitment / Career section.
  3. Find the link for “Credit Officer Final Result 2025”.

  5. Search your Roll Number / Name in the list.
