CPCB Various Post Answer Key 2025

Post Date: July 16, 2025 8:44 PM

The Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) has made available the Answer Key for the 2025 recruitment examination for various positions, including Scientist B, MTS, Data Entry Operator, LDC, Assistant, and others, on its official website. The application process ran from 07 April 2025 to 28 April 2025, with the exam held on 21-22 June 2025. Candidates can access their answer key by logging into the portal using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number. Direct links and detailed instructions are provided below.

How To Check & Download Control Board CPCB Various Post Answer Key 2025
  • Visit the Central Pollution Control Board official portal and navigate to the Recruitment / Results / Answer Key section.
  • Look for the notification labeled “Answer Key for Various Posts – 2025”, published around 16 July 2025 for Scientist B, MTS, and other posts.
  • Click on the answer key notice to open the specific download portal or PDF link.
  • A PDF file with the provisional answers will appear. Download and save it to your device.
  • Open the PDF and review your answers by comparing them to the official key. Note correct and incorrect responses for a score estimate.
  • If you identify any incorrect answers, look for instructions within the answer key notice about submitting challenges. Objections must be submitted within the allowed time, usually with a fee.
  • Keep a saved copy for your records and print a backup if you plan to challenge any answers or for reference during the selection process.

Important Questions

When will the online application for Control Board CPCB Various Post Recruitment 2025 start?

The online application for this recruitment started on 07 April 2025.

What is the last date for online application for Control Board CPCB Various Post Online Form 2025?

The last date for online application is 28 April 2025.

What is the age limit for Control Board CPCB Various Post Bharti 2025?

The age limit is a minimum of 18 years and a maximum of 27 years for most posts, 35 years for Scientist B, and 30 years for Assistant Law Officer, Assistant Accounts Officer, Senior Scientific Assistant, Technical Supervisor, Assistant, and Accounts Assistant posts.

What is the eligibility for Control Board CPCB Various Post Vacancy 2025?

Candidates must have a 3-year full-time B.Sc. degree with Physics and Mathematics OR a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering (with Physics & Math in at least one semester). They should also have passed English as a subject in either 10th or 12th class, demonstrating basic spoken and written English skills.

What is the official website for CPCB?

The official website for CPCB is https://cpcb.nic.in/.
