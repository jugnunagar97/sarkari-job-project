How to Fill CSIR CRRI Scientist Online Form 2024
  • Visit official CSIR CRRI recruitment portal at https://crridom.gov.in/recruitment.
  • Click on "Apply Online" or recruitment notification link for Scientist Group IV.
  • Select "New Registration" option to create candidate account.
  • Provide email address, mobile number, and set secure password.
  • Verify account through OTP received on registered mobile number.
  • Log in using credentials to access CSIR scientist application form.
  • Fill all mandatory personal information including name, date of birth, father's name carefully.
  • Provide complete residential address with postal code details.
  • Enter educational qualifications including 10th, 12th, degree, master's, and Ph.D. (if applicable) details.
  • Mention engineering specialization and institution names for each qualification.
  • Specify marks/percentage for each academic qualification and CGPA if available.
  • Upload recent passport-sized photograph (4.5cm x 3.5cm, white background, JPG format).
  • Upload signature (3cm x 1.5cm, clear black/blue ink on white paper, JPG format).
  • Upload Bachelor's degree certificate or mark sheet for verification.
  • Upload Master's degree certificate if applicable.
  • Upload Ph.D. certificate or research documents if applicable.
  • Upload caste certificate if applying under SC/ST/OBC category.
  • Upload disability certificate if applicable for PwBD category.
  • Upload income certificate for EWS category applicants.
  • Upload ex-servicemen certificate if applicable.
  • Provide date of birth proof (10th certificate or birth certificate).
  • Review all entered information thoroughly before submission.
  • Proceed to payment section and pay applicable fee if applicable.
  • After successful payment, save and print confirmation – Deadline: 25 January 2025.

CSIR CRRI Overview and Road Research Excellence

Understanding Premier Road Research Institute and Transportation Innovation

CSIR-Central Road Research Institute, established 1950, operates as India's premier road and transportation research organization. CRRI conducts fundamental and applied research on road materials, pavements, traffic engineering, highway safety advancing transportation technology. Institute provides technical guidance to Ministry of Road Transport, highway agencies, state governments on road construction and maintenance. CRRI maintains state-of-the-art laboratories, testing facilities enabling comprehensive research on pavement performance and traffic analysis. Institute collaborates internationally with leading road research organizations promoting knowledge exchange and global best practices.

CSIR CRRI's Research Focus Areas:

  • Pavement Research: Advanced studies on asphalt, concrete, flexible pavement durability and performance
  • Traffic Engineering: Research on traffic management, signal optimization, safety improvement
  • Sustainable Roads: Green highway construction, recycled materials, environmental protection
  • Road Safety: Research on accident prevention, safety design, injury mitigation
  • Standards Development: Contribute to Indian Road Congress standards and specifications
  • International Collaboration: Participate in global road research, knowledge sharing

Career and Research Impact: CRRI scientists join India's leading road research organization contributing to transportation infrastructure excellence. Employees conduct meaningful research influencing national highway policies and standards. Research findings improve road quality, safety, sustainability benefiting millions road users. Scientists gain international recognition publishing research advancing global road technology. Professional environment supports continuous learning, collaboration, academic growth.
