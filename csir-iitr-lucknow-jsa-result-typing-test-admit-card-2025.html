CSIR IITR LUCKNOW JSA RESULT & TYPING TEST ADMIT CARD 2025

Post Date: May 12, 2025

CSIR Indian Institute of Toxicology Research (IITR), Lucknow, has released the result and Typing Test Admit Card for the Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA) examination (Advertisement No. IITR-01/2025). The written exam for this recruitment was conducted on April 27, 2025. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now check their results and download the admit card for the upcoming typing test, scheduled for May 19, 2025.
