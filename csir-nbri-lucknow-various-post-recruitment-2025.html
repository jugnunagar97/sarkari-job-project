CSIR NBRI Lucknow Various Post Recruitment 2025 : Vacancy Details
Post Name No. Of Post Eligibility Criteria
Technical Assistant 09

Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Science from any recognized university in India or a Diploma in Engineering. For subject-wise eligibility, please refer to the official notification.
Technician 18

Candidates should have either: Class 10th with 55% marks and an ITI certificate, or 10+2 with Science and 55% marks plus 2 years of apprentice experience, or Class 10th with Science and 55% marks plus 3 years of relevant trade experience. For full details, check the official notification.
Junior Secretariat Assistant (Finance & Accounts / Store and Purchase) 09

Candidates must have passed the 10+2 (Intermediate) exam from any recognized board in India and should have computer typing skills.
