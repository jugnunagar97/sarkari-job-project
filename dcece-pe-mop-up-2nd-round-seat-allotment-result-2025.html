DCECE PE Mop-Up 2nd Round Seat Allotment Result 2025

Post Date: September 12, 2025 10:22 PM

The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) has announced the Mop-Up 2nd Round Seat Allotment Result for the Diploma Certificate Entrance Competitive Examination (DCECE) on its official portal. This result is critical for candidates seeking admission into courses like Polytechnic Engineering (PE), Paramedical (PM), and Paramedical Matric (PMM). Ensure you review the detailed information for the Bihar DCECE PE Mop-Up 2nd Round Seat Allotment Result 2025 provided below.

Bihar DCECE Polytechnic/Diploma NA
  • Candidates for Polytechnic Engineer (PE) and Paramedical Matric (PMM) must have passed or appeared for the 10th exam from a recognized board.
  • For Paramedical (PM), Pharmacy applicants need 12th with Physics, Chemistry, English, and Maths/Biology, while GNM candidates must have 12th with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, securing 40% marks (35% for SC/ST).
How To Check Bihar DCECE PE Revised Mop Up Seat Allotment Result 2025
  • Visit the official website of BCECE Board: bceceboard.bihar.gov.in
  • On the homepage, open the DCECE (Diploma Certificate Entrance Competitive Examination) section.
  • Find the link for “PE Revised Mop Up Seat Allotment Result 2025”.
  • Click on the link and enter your Roll Number/Application Number & Date of Birth.
  • The Seat Allotment Result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout for admission/college reporting.

Important Questions

When will the online application for Bihar DCECE Polytechnic/Diploma Online Form 2025 start?

The online application for this exam has started on 02 April 2025.

What is the last date for Bihar DCECE Polytechnic/Diploma Online Form 2025?

The last date to apply online for this exam is 12 May 2025.

What is the age limit for Bihar DCECE Polytechnic/Diploma Online Form 2025?

The age limit for candidates applying for this exam is as per the Bihar DCECE Polytechnic/Diploma rules. For detailed age-related information, refer to the official notification.

What is the eligibility for Bihar DCECE Polytechnic/Diploma Online Form 2025?

Candidates must have passed 12th (Science) with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with Mathematics or Biology, OR 12th (Science) with Mathematics, OR 12th (Science) with a Vocational/Technical subject, OR 10th + ITI.

What is the official website of BCECEB?

The official website of BCECEB is https://bceceboard.bihar.gov.in/
