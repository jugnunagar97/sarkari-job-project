How to Check & Download DGAFMS Group C Answer Key 2025
  • Visit the Official DGAFMS Portal: Go to dgafms24.onlineapplicationform.org and look for the “Objection Tracker” or “Answer Key” link on the homepage.
  • Log in with Your Credentials: Use your Roll Number (or Registration ID) and Date of Birth to access your response sheet.
  • Select the Exam Date/Post: Choose the correct exam date (3–6 June 2025) and your applied post (e.g., LDC, Stenographer, Fireman).
  • Download Your Answer Key & Response Sheet: The provisional answer key will appear — download the PDF and save it for calculating your score.
  • Submit Objections (Optional): If you find any errors, submit objections online by 11 June 2025, with supporting evidence and fee (if applicable).
