How To Check & Download DGAFMS Group C Answer Key 2025
  1. Visit the official DGAFMS portal at dgafms24.onlineapplicationform.org and look for the “Objection Tracker” or “Answer Key” link on the homepage.
  2. Log in with your credentials using your Roll Number (or Registration ID) and Date of Birth to access your response sheet.
  3. Select the exam date/post. Choose the correct exam date (3–6 June 2025) and your applied post (e.g., LDC, Stenographer, Fireman).
  4. Download your Answer Key & Response Sheet. Your provisional answer key will appear — download the PDF and save it for calculating your score.
  5. Submit objections (optional). If you find any errors, submit objections online by 11 June 2025, with supporting evidence and fee (if applicable).
