How To Check & Download DSSSB Various Post Final Answer Key 2025
  • Visit Official Website: Go to the official DSSSB portal: dsssb.delhi.gov.in
  • Go to “Answer Key” Section: On the homepage, click the Final Answer Key link—this opens a page listing the latest result notices.
  • Find the Relevant Notice: Look for notices stating “Various Posts Final Answer Key” or the specific post code.
  • Download the PDF: Click the download link next to the answer key notice. The PDF will list roll numbers of qualifying candidates.
  • Search for Your Roll Number: Open the PDF on your computer or mobile, use CTRL+F (or search) to locate your roll number.
