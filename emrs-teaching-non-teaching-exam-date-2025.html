EMRS TEACHING/ NON TEACHING EXAM DATE 2025

Post Date: October 8, 2025 | 7:05 pm

The Ek Lavya Model Residential School (EMRS) NESTS has released the Exam Date on its official website for the recruitment of Teaching & Non-Teaching Posts (Principal, PGT, TGT, Hostel Warden, Accountant, Clerk, Lab Attendant, etc.). Eligibility Criteria (Post Wise)
Post NameEligibility Criteria
Principal
  • Candidate must hold a Master’s Degree in any subject along with a B.Ed qualification and should also possess a minimum of 12 years of teaching experience.
TGT
  • Candidate must have a Bachelor’s Degree in the relevant subject with at least 50% marks, along with a B.Ed qualification.
  • In addition, the candidate should have passed the STET or CTET Paper II examination.
PGT
  • Candidate must possess a Master’s Degree in the relevant subject with at least 50% marks along with a B.Ed. degree.
  • However, for those who have completed an integrated 4-year degree course, a separate B.Ed. qualification is not required.
  • For subject-wise vacancy details, candidates should refer to the **official notification**.
Staff Nurse (Female)
  • Candidate must have completed B.Sc. Nursing or Post Basic Nursing and be registered with the State Nursing Council.
  • In addition, a minimum of 2.5 years of relevant work experience is required.
Hostel Warden
  • Candidate must hold a Bachelor’s Degree in any stream from a recognized university in India.
Accountant
  • Candidate must have a Bachelor’s Degree in Commerce (B.Com) from any recognized university in India.
Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA)
  • Candidate must have passed the 10+2 Intermediate Examination.
  • Should possess a typing speed of 35 words per minute in English or 30 words per minute in Hindi.
Lab Attendant
  • Candidate must have passed Class 10 with a Certificate/Diploma in Laboratory Technique or must have passed 10+2 Intermediate with Science stream.
How To Check & Download EMRS Teaching/ Non Teaching Exam Date 2025
  • Visit the official EMRS / NESTS website: Open the official website of EMRS (National Education Society for Tribal Students) in your browser.
  • Go to the “Notifications / Recruitment / Latest Updates” section: On the homepage, find the section where all new updates and recruitment notices are posted.
  • Find the EMRS Teaching / Non-Teaching recruitment notification: Look for the latest advertisement or update related to Teaching and Non-Teaching posts.
  • Check the “Important Dates / Exam Schedule” section: Inside the notification, look for a section titled “Important Dates” — it will contain details about the exam schedule.
  • Open the Exam Date / Schedule PDF: Click on the PDF link containing the exam timetable and date information.
  • Download or Save the PDF: Use the “Download” or “Save As” option to keep the PDF file on your phone or computer.
  • Check your post’s exam date: Open the downloaded PDF and find the exam date and shift details for the post you have applied for (Teaching or Non-Teaching).

EMRS Exam Dates Announced: 13, 14, and 21 December 2025

EMRS Final Exam Schedule for 7267 Teaching & Non-Teaching Posts

The **Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS) NESTS** has released the official examination schedule for the massive recruitment drive of **7267 Teaching and Non-Teaching vacancies**. The CBT (Computer Based Test) examinations are scheduled across three key dates in December: **13 December 2025, 14 December 2025, and 21 December 2025**. This staggered schedule ensures smooth execution for posts like **Principal, PGT, TGT, Hostel Warden, and Accountant**. All candidates must download the official Exam Date Notice to check the exact day and shift allocated for their specific applied position before the **Admit Card** is released.

Last-Minute Strategy for EMRS Teaching and Non-Teaching Exams

Targeted Revision for EMRS TGT, PGT, and Non-Teaching Syllabus

With the exam dates locked in for December, candidates need a highly focused study plan. **PGT and TGT** applicants must prioritize their subject-specific curriculum, educational psychology, and general awareness related to the tribal education sector. For **Non-Teaching posts** like **Accountant, JSA, and Lab Attendant**, the focus should shift to areas like Quantitative Aptitude, General English/Hindi, Computer Literacy, and the specific professional knowledge required for the role (e.g., Accounts rules for Accountant). Utilize the **Official Syllabus & Exam Pattern PDF** (link provided above) to maximize score potential in the remaining time.
