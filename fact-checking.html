Fast Checking Policy

At SRT, our commitment to providing accurate and reliable information is paramount. To ensure that our content meets the highest standards of accuracy and credibility, we adhere to a rigorous fact‑checking process. Here’s how we ensure the integrity of the information we provide:

1. Source Verification

We use information from trusted sources such as official government websites, examination authorities, press releases, and administrative documents. All sources are cross‑checked to confirm authenticity.

2. Research‑Based Content

Our articles are based on thorough research. Writers compile details from multiple reliable sources to confirm their accuracy before publication.

3. Editorial Review

Before publishing any content, it goes through an editorial review. Editors assess the information for accuracy, relevance, and clarity, ensuring it meets our editorial standards.

4. Fact‑Checking Protocols

  • Primary Sources: We prioritize primary sources of information and cross‑verify facts with secondary sources where necessary.
  • Date of Information: We ensure that the information we provide is current and relevant, checking for the most recent updates and changes.
  • Expert Consultation: When needed, we consult experts or specialists to validate complex information or data.

5. Correction Policy

If you spot any errors or inaccuracies in our content, please let us know. We are committed to making corrections promptly and transparently. You can contact us at mysarkariresult.portal@gmail.com with details of the issue.

6. Ongoing Review

Our team performs periodic reviews of older articles and pages to ensure information remains accurate and up to date, especially for time‑sensitive topics like recruitment schedules and results.

7. Transparency

We are committed to transparency about our fact‑checking processes. If you have any questions about how we verify our information, feel free to reach out.
