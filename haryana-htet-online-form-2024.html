Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test HTET Online Form 2024

Post Date: November 13, 2024 2:33 PM

The Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test (HTET) exam determines the candidates' eligibility to teach in Haryana's primary and upper schools. Candidates qualifying the Haryana TET exam will become eligible to take the teacher recruitment exam conducted by the Haryana Government. The HTET registrations started on June 1, 2025. The last date to submit the HTET application form was June 5, 2025. The HTET form was earlier available from November 4 to 15, 2024. Eligible candidates need to apply online to appear for the HTET 2025 exam. The HTET notification PDF was released on October 30, 2024, on the official website - https://bseh.org.in.
