HPPSC HPAS PRE EXAM RESULT 2025

Post Date: July 21, 2025

The Himachal Pradesh Public Service Commission (HPPSC) has released the result for the HP Administrative Services (HPAS) Preliminary Examination 2025. This recruitment is for 30 positions including Block Development Officer, Tehsildar, and Assistant Commissioner. The exam was held on 29 June 2025. Candidates can now check their results from the official website or the direct link provided below.

Important Dates

Application Start Date: 13 April 2025

Last Date to Apply Online: 10 May 2025

Exam Date: 29 June 2025

Admit Card: June 2025

Exam Result: 20 July 2025

Application Fee

For General, EWS: ₹ 600/-

For SC, ST, OBC: ₹ 150/-

Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, etc.

HPPSC HPAS Notification 2025: Age Limits as on 01/01/2025

  • Minimum Age: 21 Years
  • Maximum Age: 35 Years
  • Age relaxation is provided as per HPPSC regulations.

Total Post

30

How To Check & Download HPPSC HPAS Pre Exam Result 2025
  1. Visit the official HPPSC website: hppsc.hp.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, navigate to the “Results” or “Press Note” section.
  3. Find the link for “Result of Preliminary Exam for HPAS Combined Competitive Examination – 2025”.
  4. Click the link to download the result PDF.
  5. Open the PDF and use the search function (Ctrl+F) to find your roll number.
