IB ACIO Grade-II/Tech Recruitment 2025 – Apply Online Now

Post Date: October 25, 2025 | 10:25 AM IST

The Intelligence Bureau (IB), Ministry of Home Affairs has officially released the notification for Assistant Central Intelligence Officer (ACIO) Grade-II/Technical Recruitment 2025 on its official website. This prestigious recruitment offers 258 positions for technically skilled professionals across India's intelligence network. The online application process started on 25 October 2025 and will continue until 16 November 2025. Candidates aged 18-27 years as on 16 November 2025 possessing valid GATE score (2023, 2024, or 2025) in Electronics & Communication (EC) or Computer Science & IT (CS) along with B.E./B.Tech or Master's degree in relevant fields are eligible to apply. Selection is based on GATE score with no written examination required. Application fee is ₹200 for General/OBC/EWS and ₹100 for SC/ST/All Females candidates.

How to Fill IB ACIO Grade-II/Tech Online Form 2025
  • Visit official IB recruitment portal at https://cdn.digialm.com/EForms/.
  • Click on "Apply Online" option on the recruitment page.
  • Register with valid email ID and mobile number if you are a new user.
  • Receive One-Time Password (OTP) on registered email and mobile for verification.
  • Log in using credentials to access ACIO Grade-II/Tech application form.
  • Fill all mandatory personal details including name, date of birth, father's name, address carefully.
  • Provide complete GATE score details including exam year (2023, 2024, or 2025) and score.
  • Mention subject area: Electronics & Communication (EC) or Computer Science & IT (CS).
  • Provide educational qualification details including degree name, institution, passing year.
  • Upload recent passport-sized photograph (4.5cm x 3.5cm, JPG format, white background).
  • Upload signature in specified dimensions (3cm x 1.5cm, JPG format) with black/blue ink on white paper.
  • Upload GATE score certificate/scorecard copy for verification.
  • Review all entered information thoroughly checking against original documents.
  • Proceed to payment section and pay applicable examination fee through available methods.
  • After successful payment, save and print application confirmation with registration number.
  • Deadline: 16 November 2025 – Apply immediately to avoid last-minute delays.

Intelligence Bureau Role, Career Advancement, and National Security Contribution

Understanding IB Functions and Distinguished Intelligence Service Career Pathways

The Intelligence Bureau (IB), Ministry of Home Affairs, serves as India's primary domestic intelligence agency responsible for national security, counterintelligence operations, and internal security threats assessment. IB operates nationwide coordinating intelligence gathering, analysis, counterespionage activities, and security operations protecting national interests against external and internal threats. ACIO Grade-II/Technical positions represent entry-level officers in IB's technical cadre managing specialized technology systems, intelligence operations infrastructure, cybersecurity functions, and advanced technical applications. IB's mission encompasses collecting, analyzing, and disseminating intelligence supporting governmental decision-making, counterintelligence operations, and national security protection ensuring India's security apparatus maintains technological superiority and operational effectiveness across complex threats environment.
