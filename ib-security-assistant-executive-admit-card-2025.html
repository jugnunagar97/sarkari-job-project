Steps to Check & Download IB Security Assistant/ Exe. Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the Official Application Portal: Go to the official recruitment portal of the Intelligence Bureau (MHA).
  • Login with Your Details: Enter your User ID / Registration Number and Password / Date of Birth.
  • Fill the security code/captcha if asked.
  • Find the “Admit Card” Link: After login, look for a tab or notification that says “Check Admit Card” or “City Intimation Slip.”
  • Open the Exam City Information: Click the link to view your allotted exam city for the Security Assistant/Executive exam.
  • This will show the city where your exam centre will be located (not the exact centre address yet).
  • Download & Save the Slip: Download the exam city intimation slip to your device.
  • Take a printout for future reference.
  • Check for Admit Card Released: The admit card/hall ticket (with exact centre address, date, and reporting time) has released separately a few days before the exam.

IB Security Assistant/ Executive Admit Card 2025: Frequently Asked Questions
  1. When will the admit card be released?

    The exam city intimation slip has released on 15 September 2025, and the admit card on 26 September 2025, a few days before the exam.

  2. Where can I check my admit card?

    By logging in with your registration number and password/date of birth on the official recruitment portal of the Intelligence Bureau (MHA) at www.mha.gov.in/notifications/vacancies.

  3. What is the difference between exam city details and admit card?

    The exam city slip only shows the city of your exam centre, while the admit card provides the exact address, reporting time, and exam instructions.

  4. When will the admit card be released?

    The admit card is usually released a few days after the city intimation slip, closer to the exam date.

  5. What is the official website for IB?

    The official website for IB is https://www.mha.gov.in/notifications/vacancies.
