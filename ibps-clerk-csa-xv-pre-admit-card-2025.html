How to Check & Download IBPS Clerk CSA XV PET Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official website of Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) at www.ibps.in.
  • On the homepage, look for the notice “IBPS Clerk CWE XV Pre Admit Card 2025” under the CRP Clerical Cadre section.
  • Click on the link to redirect to the admit card login portal.
  • Provide your Registration Number / Roll Number and Password / Date of Birth (DOB).
  • Click on the Login / Submit button.
  • Your IBPS Clerk Pre Admit Card 2025 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the admit card and save it as a PDF file.
  • Take a clear printout (preferably in color) to carry to the exam center.
  • Verify your Name, Roll Number, Exam Date, Venue, Reporting Time, and Instructions mentioned on the admit card.
  • Along with the admit card, carry a valid Photo ID proof (Aadhar Card, PAN, Voter ID, etc.) and a passport-size photo as per IBPS instructions.
