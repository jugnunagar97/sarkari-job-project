HOW TO CHECK & DOWNLOAD IBPS PO MT XV 15 PRE RESULT 2025
  1. Visit the official website of IBPS: ibps.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the “CRP-PO/MT” tab.
  3. Look for the notification titled “Online Preliminary Examination Result for CRP-PO/MTs-XV”.
  4. Click on the result link to be redirected to the login page.
  5. Enter your Registration Number/Roll Number, Password/Date of Birth, and Captcha Code (if shown).
  6. Click on the Login/Submit button.
  7. Your Prelims Result/Scorecard will appear on the screen.
  8. Verify details such as Name, Roll Number, Category, Marks, Cut-off, and Qualifying Status.
  9. Save the result/scorecard as a PDF file on your device.
  10. Take a printout of the result for future use (Mains exam, document verification, etc.).
