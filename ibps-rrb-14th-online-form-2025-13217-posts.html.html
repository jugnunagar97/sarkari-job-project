IBPS RRB 14TH RECRUITMENT 2025

Post Date: September 8, 2025

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has released the official notification for the recruitment of RRB XIV (14) for various posts including Office Assistant (OA) and Officer Scale-I, II, and III. This recruitment drive aims to fill a total of 13,217 vacancies in Regional Rural Banks. The online application process began on September 01, 2025, and the last date to apply is September 21, 2025. Candidates are advised to check the complete details, including eligibility criteria, age limits, and important dates, before applying.

Eligibility Criteria
Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Office Assistant (Multipurpose)
  • Candidates must have completed their Graduation in any discipline from any recognized University/institute.
  • Proficiency in the local language as prescribed by the participating RRB.
  • Desirable: Working knowledge of a Computer.
Officer Scale-I (Assistant Manager)
  • Candidates must have completed their Graduation in any discipline from any recognized University/institute.
  • Preference will be given to candidates with a degree in Agriculture, Horticulture, Forestry, Animal Husbandry, Veterinary Science, Agricultural Engineering, etc.
  • Proficiency in the local language as prescribed by the participating RRB.
  • Desirable: working knowledge of a Computer.
Officer Scale-II (General Banking Officer)
  • Candidates must have completed their Graduation in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks from any recognized University/institute.
  • Preference will be given to candidates with a degree in Banking, Finance, Marketing, Agriculture, etc.
  • 2 years of experience as an officer in a Bank or Financial Institution.
Officer Scale-II (CA)
  • Candidates must have passed the CA Exam from ICAI India.
  • 1 year of experience as a Chartered Accountant.
Officer Scale-II (Law)
  • Candidates must have a Degree in Law from a recognized University with a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate.
  • 2 years of experience as an advocate or as a Law Officer in Banks or Financial Institutions.
Officer Scale-II (Marketing)
  • Candidates having an MBA Degree in Marketing from a recognized University.
  • 1 year of experience in the relevant field.
Officer Scale-II (Treasury Manager)
  • Candidates having a degree in CA or an MBA Degree in Finance from a recognized University.
  • 1 year of experience in the relevant field.
Officer Scale-II (Agriculture)
  • Candidates having a Graduation Degree in Agriculture / Horticulture / Dairy / Animal / Veterinary Science / Engineering / Pisciculture from a recognized university / institute.
  • 2 years of experience in the relevant field.
Officer Scale-II (IT)
  • Candidates must have a Graduation Degree in Electronics / Communications / Computer Science / Information Technology with a minimum of 50% marks from any recognized University / Institute.
  • Desirable: Certificate in ASP, PHP, C++, Java, VB, VC, OCP, etc.
  • 1 year of experience in the relevant field.
Officer Scale-III (Senior Manager)
  • Candidates having a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized University or its equivalent with a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate.
  • Minimum 5 years’ experience as an Officer in a Bank or Financial Institutions.
