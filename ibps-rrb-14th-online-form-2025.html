IBPS RRB 14th Recruitment 2025 – 13217 Posts

Post Date: September 10, 2025 4:43 PM

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has released the IBPS RRB 14th Recruitment 2025 notification on 31 August 2025 for 13,217 vacancies across Regional Rural Banks (RRBs) for posts including Office Assistant (Clerk), Officer Scale-I (Probationary Officer), Officer Scale-II, and Officer Scale-III. The online application window is open from 1 September to 21 September 2025 at www.ibps.in. Eligible candidates, aged 18-40 years (post-wise), can apply for roles such as Office Assistant (7,972 posts), Officer Scale-I (3,907 posts), Officer Scale-II (1,139 posts), and Officer Scale-III (199 posts). The selection process includes Prelims, Mains, and Interviews (for Officer posts), with exams scheduled for November/December 2025 (Prelims) and December 2025/February 2026 (Mains). Candidates are advised to check the official notification for detailed eligibility, exam patterns, and vacancy details. Download Notification.

IBPS RRB 14th Recruitment 2025: Exam Pattern
Post Name Exam Pattern
Office Assistant (Multipurpose)
  • Prelims: 80 questions, 80 marks, 45 minutes (Reasoning: 40 questions, Numerical Ability: 40 questions).
  • Mains: 200 questions, 200 marks, 2 hours (Reasoning: 40 questions, Numerical Ability: 40 questions, General Awareness: 40 questions, English/Hindi Language: 40 questions, Computer Knowledge: 40 questions).
  • Negative Marking: 0.25 marks per wrong answer.
  • No interview; selection based on Mains marks.

  • Prelims: 80 questions, 80 marks, 45 minutes (Reasoning: 40 questions, Quantitative Aptitude: 40 questions).
  • Mains: 200 questions, 200 marks, 2 hours (Reasoning: 40 questions, Quantitative Aptitude: 40 questions, General Awareness: 40 questions, English/Hindi Language: 40 questions, Computer Knowledge: 40 questions).
  • Interview: Conducted for shortlisted candidates.
  • Negative Marking: 0.25 marks per wrong answer.
  • Final merit based on Mains and Interview marks.

  • Single-Level Exam: 200 questions, 200 marks, 2 hours (Reasoning: 40 questions, Quantitative Aptitude & Data Interpretation: 40 questions, Financial Awareness: 40 questions, English/Hindi Language: 40 questions, Computer Knowledge: 40 questions).
  • Interview: Conducted for shortlisted candidates.
  • Negative Marking: 0.25 marks per wrong answer.
  • Final merit based on exam and Interview marks.
Officer Scale-II (Specialist Cadre)
  • Single-Level Exam: 240 questions, 200 marks, 2 hours 30 minutes (Professional Knowledge: 40 questions, Reasoning: 40 questions, Quantitative Aptitude & Data Interpretation: 40 questions, Financial Awareness: 40 questions, English/Hindi Language: 40 questions, Computer Knowledge: 40 questions).
  • Interview: Conducted for shortlisted candidates.
  • Negative Marking: 0.25 marks per wrong answer.
  • Final merit based on exam and Interview marks.
Officer Scale-III
  • Single-Level Exam: 200 questions, 200 marks, 2 hours (Reasoning: 40 questions, Quantitative Aptitude & Data Interpretation: 40 questions, Financial Awareness: 40 questions, English/Hindi Language: 40 questions, Computer Knowledge: 40 questions).
  • Interview: Conducted for shortlisted candidates.
  • Negative Marking: 0.25 marks per wrong answer.
  • Final merit based on exam and Interview marks.
How to Apply for IBPS RRB 14th Online Form 2025

  • How to access the IBPS website?

    Visit the official website: www.ibps.in.

  • Where to find the application form?

    Click on “CRP RRBs” on the homepage, then select “CRP Regional Rural Banks XIV” and choose the desired post (Office Assistant or Officer Scale-I, II, III).

  • How to register?

    Click “Click here for New Registration,” provide personal details (name, parents’ names, date of birth, email ID, mobile number), and save to receive a Registration ID and Password.

  • How to complete the application?

    Log in with Registration ID and Password, fill in personal, educational, and work details, select preferred RRB/State, and upload scanned documents (photograph, signature, thumb impression, handwritten declaration).

  • How to pay the fee?

    Pay online via Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, UPI, etc., and save the e-receipt.

  • How to submit?

    Review all details, submit the form, and take a printout of the application and e-receipt for future reference. Ensure submission before 21 September 2025.

  • Note:

    Candidates should read the official notification carefully before applying to ensure eligibility and accuracy of details.
IBPS RRB 14th Recruitment 2025: Mode of Selection

  • What is the selection process for Office Assistant?

    Prelims Exam (Reasoning, Numerical Ability), Mains Exam (Reasoning, Numerical Ability, General Awareness, English/Hindi Language, Computer Knowledge), Language Proficiency Test. No interview; selection based on Mains marks.

  • What is the selection process for Officer Scale-I?

    Prelims Exam (Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude), Mains Exam (Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, General Awareness, English/Hindi Language, Computer Knowledge), Interview, and Language Proficiency Test. Final merit based on Mains and Interview marks.

  • What is the selection process for Officer Scale-II and III?

    Single-Level Exam (varies by post, includes Professional Knowledge for Specialist Cadre), Interview, and Language Proficiency Test. Final merit based on exam and Interview marks.

  • What happens after selection?

    Candidates undergo Document Verification and Medical Examination before provisional allotment to RRBs based on merit and vacancies.
