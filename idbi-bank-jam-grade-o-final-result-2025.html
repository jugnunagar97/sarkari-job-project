IDBI Bank JAM Grade “O” Final Result 2025

Post Date: August 21, 2025 12:17 PM

IDBI Bank Limited, has released Final Result on official website for the recruitment of Junior Assistant Manager JAM (Grade-O). This recruitment is for 676 positions. The exam was conducted in 08th June 2025, and the last date to submit the application form was 22th May 2025. Candidates can check their Final Result using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the portal. The direct link is provided below.

IDBI Bank JAM Examination 2025 : Short Details
Important Dates Application Fee
  • Application Start Date : 08 May 2025
  • Application Last Date : 22 May 2025
  • Fee Payment Last Date : 22 May 2025
  • Exam Date : 08 June 2025
  • Admit Card Available : 02 June 2025
  • Result Declared Date : 07 July 2025

  • For General / EWS / OBC : ₹ 1050/-
  • For SC / ST / PWD : ₹ 250/-

IDBI Bank Junior Assistant Manager (JAM) Recruitment 2025 : Vacancy Details
Post Name No. Of Post
Junior Assistant Manager (JAM) Grade ‘O’ 676
Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Junior Assistant Manager (JAM) Grade ‘O’ Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline with at least 60% marks (55% for SC/ST/PwBD) from a government-recognized university. A diploma alone is not accepted. Basic computer proficiency is also required.
How To Check & Download IDBI Bank JAM Grade-O Final Result 2025
  • Visit the official IDBI Bank website.
  • Go to the Career/Recruitment section.
  • Find and click on the link for JAM Grade-O Final Result 2025.
  • Enter your Registration Number/Roll Number and Date of Birth (if required).
  • The final result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and save it for future use.
