Indian Airforce Airmen Examination 2025: Eligibility Criteria
Category Eligibility Criteria
For 10+2 Candidates
  • Must have completed 10+2/Intermediate or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English from a recognized board.
  • A minimum of 50% aggregate marks and at least 50% in English are required.
For Pharmacy Candidates
  • Should have completed Intermediate/10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English.
  • Additionally, a Diploma or B.Sc in Pharmacy with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks is necessary.
  • Enrollment requires valid registration with the State Pharmacy Council or the Pharmacy Council of India.
