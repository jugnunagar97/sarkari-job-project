Indian Coast Guard Navik GD & Yantrik Recruitment 2025 : Education Qualification
Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Navik GD

Candidates Who Have Passed Class 12th with Physics and Mathematics as subjects from a board recognized by the Council of Boards for School Education (COBSE).
Navik Domestic

Candidates Who Have Passed Class 10 from a board recognized by the Council of Boards for School Education (COBSE).
Yantrik

Candidates Who Have Passed Class 10 from a COBSE-recognized board and completed a 3 or 4-year Diploma in Electrical, Mechanical, Electronics, or Telecommunication (Radio/Power) Engineering approved by AICTE. Or Passed Class 10 and Class 12 from a COBSE-recognized board and completed a 2 or 3-year Diploma in Electrical, Mechanical, Electronics, or Telecommunication (Radio/Power) Engineering approved by AICTE.
