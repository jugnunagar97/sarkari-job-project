Indian Navy Civilian Admit Card 2025

Post Date: August 29, 2025 1:34 PM

Join Indian Navy (Nausena Bharti), has been released the Admit Card for the Indian Navy Civilian INCET 01/2025 Recruitment 2025. The examination will be conducted on Last Week Of August 2025. This recruitment drive aims to fill 1400+ vacancies. The application process for this recruitment was open from 05th July to 18th July 2025. Candidates must check the complete details for the Indian Navy Civilian Admit Card 2025 provided below.

  • Pharmacist, Fire Engine Driver : 18-27 Years

Indian Navy Civilian Recruitment 2025 : Eligibility Criteria
Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Staff Nurse
  • Candidates who have passed their class 10th / High School Examination from any recognized Board in India.
  • Certificate of training in an approved Hospital as a Nurse.
  • Registered as a fully trained nurse in the Medical and Surgical Nursing and Midwifery.
  • Desirable: Knowledge of Hindi or local language.
Chargeman (Naval Aviation)
  • Candidate should have held the rank of Petty Officer or equivalent and have seven years’ service/ experience as such in the appropriate technical branch of the Army/ Navy or Air Force. Or
  • Diploma in Aeronautical or Electrical or Mechanical or Tele-Communication or Automobile Engineering. Or
  • Matriculates with approved apprenticeship and 5 years’ experience in the trade.
Chargeman (Ammunition Workshop)

Assistant Artist Retoucher
  • Candidates who have passed their class 10th / High School Examination from any recognized Board in India.
  • Diploma or Certificate in Commercial Art, Printing Technology, Lithography or Litho Art Work awarded after at least 2 years course or training from a recognized Institute.
  • 02 years’ experience as a retoucher. Or
  • 07 years’ practical experience as a retoucher in Military Survey, Survey of India or other Photo-Litho establishment of repute (for ex-Servicemen only).
Pharmacist
  • Candidates who have passed their class 12th / Intermediate Examination with Science, Diploma in Pharmacy, 02 Years Experience.
  • Diploma in Pharmacy from a recognized institute.
  • Registered as Pharmacist under the Pharmacist Act, 1948 (8 of 1948).
  • Two years experience as Pharmacist in any recognized hospital or pharmacy after duly registered as Pharmacist under the Pharmacy Act, 1948 (8 of 1948); and
  • The candidate shall have working knowledge of computer.
Cameraman
  • Candidates who have passed their class 10th / High School Examination from any recognized Board in India.
  • Diploma or certificate in Printing Technology awarded after at least two years course or training from a recognized Institute.
  • 5 years’ experience of operating process cameras. Or
  • 10 years experience as a Cameraman or photographer in Military Survey, Survey of India or other photo-litho establishment of repute (for ex-Servicemen only).
Store Superintendent (Armament)
  • Degree in Science with Physics or Chemistry or Mathematics from a recognized University with basic computer knowledge on database management with one year experience in stores work in Government Department or Public Sector Undertaking or in a reputed firm. Or
  • 10+2 in Science or Commerce with five years experience in stores’ work in a Government Department or Public Sector Undertaking or in a reputed workshop.
Fire Engine Driver
  • Candidates who have passed their class 12th Examination from any recognized Board in India + Heavy Motor Vehicle License.
  • Physical Standards.
  • Must be physically fit and capable of performing strenuous duties and must have passed the test specified below:-
    • (i) Height without shoes- 165 centimeters provided that a concession 2.5 centimeters height shall be allowed for Scheduled Tribes.
    • (ii) Chest (normal)- 81.5 centimeters.
    • (iii) Chest (on expansion)- 85 centimeters.
    • (iv) Weight- 50 kilograms (minimum).
    • (v) Eye sight -6/6 without any correction (normal vision).
  • Endurance Test.
    • (i) Carrying a person (fireman lift of 63.5 kilograms to a distance of 183 meters within 96 seconds);
    • (ii) Clearing 2.7 meters wide ditch landing on both feet (long jump); and
    • (iii) Climbing 3 meters vertical rope using hand and feet.
Fireman
  • Candidates who have passed their class 12th Examination from any recognized Board in India.
  • Elementary or basic or auxiliary Fire Fighting course from a recognized institute.
  • Possession of heavy motor vehicle license.
  • Physical Standards:- The candidate shall be physically fit and capable of performing strenuous duties and must pass the test specified below:-
    • (i) Height without shoes- 165 centimeters provided that a concession 2.5 centimeters height shall be allowed for Scheduled Tribes.
    • (ii) Chest (normal)- 81 centimeters.
    • (iii) Chest (on expansion)- 85 centimeters.
    • (iv) Weight- 50 kilograms (minimum).
    • (v) Eye sight- 6/6 (normal Vision).
  • Endurance Test-
    • (i) Carrying a person (fireman lift of 63.5 kilograms to a distance of 183 meters within 96 seconds);
    • (ii) Clearing 2.7 meters wide ditch landing on both feet (long jump); and
    • (iii) Climbing 3 meters vertical rope using hand and feet.
Storekeeper/ Storekeeper (Armament) Candidates who have passed their class 12th / Intermediate Examination from any recognized Board in India with one-year experience in inventory related stores work in government department or Public undertaking or in a reputed firm.
Civilian Motor Driver Ordinary Grade
  • Candidates who have passed their class 10th / High School Examination from any recognized Board in India.
  • Must possess a driving license for Heavy Vehicles and Motor Cycles.
  • One year practical experience in Heavy Motor Vehicles (HMVs) driving.
Tradesman Mate Candidates who have passed their class 10th / High School Examination from any recognized Board in India + ITI in Related Field.
Pest Control Worker
  • Candidates who have passed their class 10th / High School Examination from any recognized Board in India.
  • Must have knowledge of Hindi or regional language.
Bhandari
  • Candidates who have passed their class 10th / High School Examination from any recognized Board in India.
  • Knowledge of swimming.
  • One year experience as Cook.
Lady Health Visitor Should be of Matriculation Standard and should have some special training after her basic Auxiliary Nurse Midwifery course unless she has done a course which has been specifically strengthen with regard to family planning and the total family planning health outlook.
Multi Tasking Staff (Mininsterial) Candidates who have passed their class 10th / High School + ITI Examination from any recognized Board in India.
Multi Tasking Staff (Non Industrial)/ Ward Sahaika Candidates who have passed their class 10th / High School + ITI Examination from any recognized Board in India.

Multi Tasking Staff (Non Industrial)/ Barber Candidates who have passed their class 10th / High School + ITI Examination from any recognized Board in India.
Draughtsman (Construction) Certificate in Draughtsman ship (Mechanical or Civil) from Industrial Training Institute, recognized by National Council of Vocational Training or Ex-Naval apprentices trained in Mechanical or Civil Engineering.
