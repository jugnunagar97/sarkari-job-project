IOCL Junior Engineer Recruitment 2025

Post Date: September 12, 2025

The Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) has released the detailed IOCL Junior Engineer Recruitment 2025 Notification for Junior Engineer posts. Eligible candidates with a 3-year full-time Diploma in Engineering in relevant disciplines and aged between 18 to 26 years can apply online from 12th September 2025 to 28th September 2025 via the official website, www.iocl.com. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Test (CBT), Group Discussion (GD), Group Task (GT), Personal Interview (PI), Document Verification, and Medical Examination. Selected candidates will receive a salary in the pay scale of ₹30,000–₹1,20,000 along with additional allowances.

IOCL Junior Engineer Recruitment 2025: Eligibility Criteria
Educational Qualification
  • Diploma Requirement: 3-year full-time regular Diploma in Engineering from an AICTE/UGC/State Board recognized Institute/University.
  • Minimum Marks:
    • General/EWS/OBC-NCL: 65%
    • SC/ST/PwBD: 55%
  • Eligible Disciplines:
    • Chemical: Chemical Engineering, Chemical Technology, Chemical Technology (Fertiliser), Petrochemical Engineering, Petrochemical Technology, Refinery & Petrochemical Engineering
    • Mechanical: Mechanical Engineering
    • Electrical: Electrical, Electrical & Electronics, Electrical Power System, Electrical Engineering (Industrial Control)
    • Instrumentation: Instrumentation, Instrumentation & Electronics, Instrumentation & Control, Applied Electronics & Instrumentation
Documents Required
  • Scanned copy of 3-year Diploma certificate and mark sheets.
  • Recent passport-size photograph (JPG format, size as per IOCL guidelines).
  • Scanned signature (JPG format, size as per IOCL guidelines).
  • Scanned thumb impression (JPG format, size as per IOCL guidelines).
  • Caste certificate (if applicable, for SC/ST/OBC-NCL).
  • PwBD certificate (if applicable).
  • Valid ID proof (Aadhar Card/PAN Card/Voter ID/Driving License).
Steps to Apply for IOCL Junior Engineer Recruitment 2025
  • Online Registration:
    • Visit the IOCL official website: www.iocl.com, and go to Latest Job Opening.
    • Click on the Apply Online link under Junior Engineer/Officer Recruitment 2025.
    • Read the instructions carefully and proceed to fill out the online application form with accurate details.
  • Filling the Application Form:
    • Enter personal, educational, and communication details carefully.
    • Upload scanned photograph, signature, and thumb impression in the prescribed format.
    • The portal will capture a LIVE photo using a webcam or mobile (must match actual appearance during GD/PI).
  • Payment of Application Fee:
    • Pay online through Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking/UPI/Wallets.
    • SC/ST/PwBD candidates are exempted from the fee.
    • After successful payment, an e-receipt will be generated.
  • Final Submission:
    • Verify all entries carefully before submission (changes not allowed after submission).
    • Submit the application form online.
    • Download and save the PDF copy of the filled application form for future reference.
