ISRO ICRB Scientist/Engineer Exam Date 2025

Post Date: September 12, 2025 4:10 PM

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has announced the revised exam date for the Scientist/Engineer ‘SC’ Recruitment 2025 under Advertisement No. ISRO:ICRB:02(EMC):2025. The computer-based test is scheduled for 26 October 2025 to fill 320 vacancies in Electronics, Mechanical, and Computer Science disciplines. The online application process closed on 16 June 2025. Candidates can check their exam details and download admit cards using their Registration Number and Date of Birth on the official ISRO portal. The exam was rescheduled from 14 September 2025 due to a clash with the UPSC CDS-II Exam 2025. Below are the details for accessing the exam schedule and other relevant information.
