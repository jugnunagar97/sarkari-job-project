ITBP MEDICAL OFFICER MO RESULT 2025

Post Date: July 24, 2025

The Indo-Tibetan Border Police (ITBP) has released the result for the Medical Officer (MO) Examination 2025. This recruitment is for 345 positions. The exam was conducted in February 2025. Candidates who appeared for the test can now check their results by logging into the official portal using their credentials. The direct link is provided below.

How To Check & Download ITBP Medical Officer MO Result 2025
  1. Visit the official ITBP recruitment website: recruitment.itbpolice.nic.in.
  2. On the homepage, navigate to the "Results" section.
  3. Find and click the link for “Result for Medical Officer Examination 2025”.
  4. You may be required to log in with your roll number/registration number and date of birth.
  5. The result or merit list will be displayed in PDF format.
  6. Download and save the file for your records.
