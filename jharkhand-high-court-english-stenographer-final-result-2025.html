JHARKHAND HIGH COURT ENGLISH STENOGRAPHER FINAL RESULT 2025

Post Date: September 18, 2025

The Jharkhand High Court (JHC) has released the Final Result for the English Stenographer post (Advt. No. 02/2024) on its official website, jharkhandhighcourt.nic.in. The recruitment covers 399 vacancies for English Stenographers in Civil Courts and the Judicial Academy, Jharkhand. The stenography skill test was conducted from June 18–22, 2024, and interviews were held from August 22 to September 3, 2024. The final result was declared on September 18, 2025. Candidates can check their result using their Roll Number in the PDF available on the JHC portal. Download the result PDF from the link below and stay updated at sarkaariresult.org for further updates.
