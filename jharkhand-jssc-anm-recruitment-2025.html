JHARKHAND JSSC ANM RECRUITMENT 2025 – LAST DATE TODAY

Post Date: September 10, 2025

The Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission (JSSC) is closing the application window for the Jharkhand Auxiliary Nurse Midwife Competitive Examination (JANMCE-2025) today. This recruitment drive is for a total of 3181 ANM positions. The online application process, which began on 11 August 2025, will end today, 10 September 2025. Interested and eligible candidates are urged to submit their applications immediately. Complete details regarding the application process, eligibility, and important links are provided below.

JHARKHAND JSSC ANM EXAMINATION 2025 : SHORT DETAILS

Important Dates

  • Online Apply Start Date: 11 August 2025
  • Online Apply Last Date: 10 September 2025
  • Last Date For Fee Payment: 10 September 2025
  • Exam Date: Notify Later
  • Admit Card: Before Exam

Application Fee

  • General/EBC/BC/EWS: ₹ 100/-
  • SC/ST of Jharkhand: ₹ 50/-
  • Payment Mode: Online via Debit Card, Credit Card, or Net Banking.
JHARKHAND JSSC ANM NOTIFICATION 2025 : AGE LIMITS AS ON 01 AUGUST 2025
Minimum Age 18 Years
Maximum Age (UR-Male) 40 Years
Maximum Age (EBC/BC-Male) 42 Years
Maximum Age (All Female) 43 Years
Maximum Age (SC/ST) 45 Years
Age relaxation is provided as per JSSC regulations.
JHARKHAND JSSC ANM RECRUITMENT 2025 : VACANCY DETAILS
Post Name Total Posts Eligibility Criteria
Jharkhand JSSC ANM 3181
  • Passed Matriculation (10th) with at least 45% marks.
  • Completed 18 months of A.N.M. training.
  • Registered with the Jharkhand State Nursing Council.
HOW TO FILL JHARKHAND JSSC ANM ONLINE FORM 2025

Interested candidates must submit their application online before the deadline on 10 September 2025 through the official JSSC website. It is highly recommended that all candidates read the official notification carefully before filling out the form to understand the requirements for the Last Date, Age Limit, and Education Qualification.
