JPSC ACF Pre Revised Answer Key 2025

This recruitment is for 248 positions. The JPSC Exam was Conducted in 13 July 2025. JPSC ACF & FRO Recruitment 2024 : Vacancy Details
Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Assistant Conservator Of Forest (ACF) Candidates Must Have A Bachelor’s Degree In Relevant Science Subjects Or An Engineering Degree In Civil, Mechanical, Or Chemical From A Recognized University. For Full Details, Refer To The Official Notification.
Forest Range Officer (FRO) Candidates Must Have A Bachelor’s Degree In Relevant Science Subjects Or An Engineering Degree In Civil, Mechanical, Or Chemical From A Recognized University. Refer To The Official Notification For Full Details.
How To Check & Download JPSC ACF Pre Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official JPSC website: Go to jpsc.gov.in .
  • Search the Admit Card link: Find the banner or notification titled “Download Admit Card for Jharkhand Assistant Conservator of Forest P.T. Examination, Advt. No. 04/2024”
  • Enter your credentials: Registration number, Date of Birth, CAPTCHA (if required).
  • Download & print your Admit Card: You’ll see your hall ticket with details like: exam centre, date & time, your name, photo, and instructions.
  • Check all details: Verify Name, Roll No., Exam City, Hall Number, Session timing.
