How to Fill KSSSCI Non-Teaching Online Form 2025
  • Visit official KSSSCI application portal at https://ksssci.register.smartexams.in/home.
  • Click on "Apply Online" or registration option for new users.
  • Register with valid email ID and mobile number providing personal information.
  • Receive One-Time Password (OTP) on registered email and mobile for verification.
  • Log in using your credentials to access KSSSCI non-teaching application form.
  • Select appropriate post category matching your qualifications.
  • Fill all mandatory personal details including name, date of birth, father's name, address.
  • Provide complete educational qualification details including degree name, board/university, passing year.
  • Mention relevant work experience if applicable for the position.
  • Upload recent passport-sized photograph (4.5cm x 3.5cm, JPG format, white background).
  • Upload signature in specified dimensions (3cm x 1.5cm, JPG format) using black/blue ink.
  • Upload educational certificates, caste certificate, identity proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID).
  • Upload domicile certificate for Rajasthan residence proof.
  • Upload income certificate for EWS category applicants if applicable.
  • Upload special category certificates (PH, Ex-servicemen) if applicable.
  • Review all entered information thoroughly before submission.
  • Deadline: 31 January 2025 – Apply before deadline to avoid rejection.

Non-Teaching Healthcare Roles and Professional Responsibilities

Understanding Diverse Healthcare and Administrative Positions

The KSSSCI non-teaching positions encompass diverse healthcare and administrative specializations. Pharmacists (15 positions) manage medication dispensing, inventory, quality control, ensuring patient medication safety and compliance. Junior Physiotherapists (4 posts) provide rehabilitation, patient mobility support, and therapeutic services. Dieticians (4 posts) manage nutritional planning, patient diet management supporting cancer treatment. Receptionists (10 posts) manage patient registration, appointment scheduling, front-office operations. Storekeepers (10 posts) manage medical supplies, equipment inventory, procurement. Medical Social Service Officers (8 posts) provide patient counseling, welfare support, insurance assistance.

KSSSCI Overview and Cancer Treatment Excellence

Understanding Premier Oncology Institute and Healthcare Mission

Kalyan Singh Super Specialty Cancer Institute (KSSSCI), established as Rajasthan's leading specialized cancer treatment facility, provides comprehensive oncology services including diagnosis, treatment, research, and rehabilitation. KSSSCI operates advanced cancer centers equipped with modern diagnostic equipment, radiation therapy facilities, surgical suites, and chemotherapy departments. The institute treats diverse cancer types including breast, lung, colorectal, head-neck, and hematologic malignancies serving patients across India. KSSSCI combines clinical excellence with research advancing cancer understanding and treatment innovation. The facility maintains international treatment standards ensuring patient safety, privacy, and compassionate care.

KSSSCI's Strategic Healthcare Services:
