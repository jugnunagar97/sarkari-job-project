LIC AAO / AE Pre Admit Card 2025

Post Date: September 26, 2025

The Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) has released the Pre Admit Card for AAO / AE Examination 2025 on its official website. The preliminary exam is scheduled for 03 October 2025, with applications closed on 08 September 2025. A total of 841 vacancies are available for Assistant Administrative Officer (AAO) and Assistant Engineer (AE) posts. Candidates can download their admit card using Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. Direct links are provided below.

LIC AAO / AE Examination 2025: Short Details

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: 16 August 2025
  • Last Date: 08 September 2025
  • Last Date For Fee Payment: 08 September 2025
  • Exam Date: 03 October 2025
  • Admit Card: 25 September 2025
  • Result Date: Will Be Updated Here Soon
  • Note: Candidates are advised to confirm from the LIC official website.

Application Fee

  • For General/ OBC/ EWS Candidates: ₹700/-
  • For SC/ ST/ PwBD Candidates: ₹85/-
  • Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, Internet Banking, IMPS, Cash Card / Mobile Wallet

LIC AAO / AE Notification 2025: Age Limits (As on 08 September 2025)

  • Minimum Age: 21 Years
  • Maximum Age: 32 Years
  • Candidates born not earlier than 02.08.1993 and not later than 01.08.2004 (both days inclusive).
  • Age Relaxation: As per LIC regulations

LIC AAO / AE Recruitment 2025: Vacancy Details
Post NameNo. Of PostEligibility Criteria
AAO (Generalist)341Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized Indian university or institution. The age limit is 21 to 30 years, i.e., candidates should be born between 02 August 1995 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).
AAO (Generalist) Backlog09Same as AAO (Generalist).
Assistant Engineer (AE) Civil50Candidates must have a B.Tech/B.E. in Civil Engineering from an AICTE-recognized university. They should also have at least 3 years of post-qualification experience in planning and executing multi-storied building projects. The age limit is 21 to 30 years, i.e., candidates must be born between 02 August 1995 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).
Assistant Engineer (AE) Electrical31Candidates must have a B.Tech/B.E. in Electrical Engineering from an AICTE-recognized university. They should also have at least 3 years of post-qualification experience in planning and executing multi-storied building projects. The age limit is 21 to 30 years, i.e., candidates must be born between 02 August 1995 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).
AAO (CA)30Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree and must have passed the Final Examination of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). They should also be an Associate Member of ICAI. The age limit is 21 to 30 years, i.e., candidates must be born between 02 August 1995 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).
AAO (CS)10Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree and be a qualified member of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI). The age limit is 21 to 32 years, i.e., candidates must be born between 02 August 1993 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).
AAO (Actuarial)30Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized Indian university and must have passed at least 6 papers of the examination conducted by the Institute of Actuaries of India or the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries, UK. They must also provide their membership number for verification. The age limit is 21 to 30 years, i.e., candidates must be born between 02 August 1995 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).
AAO (Insurance Specialist)310Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline along with a professional qualification in Life Insurance (Fellowship of Insurance Institute of India). They should also have at least 5 years of experience working in IRDAI-regulated Life Insurance Companies. The age limit is 21 to 30 years, i.e., candidates must be born between 02 August 1995 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).
AAO (Legal)30Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Law with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST and PwBD). They should be enrolled with the Bar Council as an Advocate and must have at least 2 years of experience either as an Advocate or as a Law Officer in the Legal Department of a bank, financial institution, statutory corporation, company, or State/Central Government. The age limit is 21 to 32 years, i.e., candidates must be born between 02 August 1993 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).
Documents Required
  • Scanned copies of educational certificates (Bachelor’s degree, B.Tech/B.E., or professional qualifications like CA, CS, Actuarial, etc.).
  • Recent passport-size photograph (JPG format, as per LIC guidelines).
  • Scanned signature (JPG format, as per LIC guidelines).
  • Caste certificate (if applicable, for SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD).
  • Valid ID proof (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving License).
  • Experience certificates (for AE, Insurance Specialist, and Legal posts, if applicable).
  • Membership certificates (for CA, CS, Actuarial, or Insurance Specialist posts).
How to Check & Download LIC AAO / AE Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the LIC official website at www.licindia.in/.
  • Go to the Careers / Recruitment section.
  • Find the link for “LIC AAO / AE Admit Card 2025”.
  • Click on that link to open the login page.
  • Enter your Registration Number / Roll Number and Password / Date of Birth.
  • Submit the details to view your admit card.
  • Download the PDF admit card.
  • Take a clear printout to carry to the exam centre.
