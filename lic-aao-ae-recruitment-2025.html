LIC AAO / AE Online Form 2025

Post Date: August 16, 2025 11:23 am

Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) has released a notification on official website for the recruitment of Assistant Administrative Officer AAO Generalist, Assistant Engineer AE, AAO Specialist Post. This recruitment is for 841 positions. LIC Application Form has started from 16 August 2025 & the candidates can apply till the 08 September 2025.

Minimum age required is 21 & The Maximum Age Is 32 Years (Candidates born not earlier than 02.08.1995 and not later than 01.08.2004 both days inclusive only are eligible). Candidates must check the complete details for LIC AAO / AE Recruitment 2025 below.

LIC AAO / AE Recruitment 2025: Vacancy Details
Post Name No. Of Post Eligibility Criteria
AAO (Generalist) 341 Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized Indian university or institution. The age limit is 21 to 30 years, i.e., candidates should be born between 02 August 1995 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).
AAO (Generalist) Backlog 09 -
Assistant Engineer AE (Civil) 50 Candidates must have a B.Tech/B.E. in Civil Engineering from an AICTE-recognized university. They should also have at least 3 years of post-qualification experience in planning and executing multi-storied building projects. The age limit is 21 to 30 years.
Assistant Engineer AE (Electrical) 31 Candidates must have a B.Tech/B.E. in Electrical Engineering from an AICTE-recognized university. They should also have at least 3 years of post-qualification experience in planning and executing multi-storied building projects. The age limit is 21 to 30 years.
AAO (CA) 30 Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree and must have passed the Final Examination of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). They should also be an Associate Member of ICAI. The age limit is 21 to 30 years.
AAO (CS) 10 Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree and be a qualified member of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI). The age limit is 21 to 32 years.
AAO (Actuarial) 30 Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree in any discipline and must have passed at least 6 papers of the examination conducted by the Institute of Actuaries of India or the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries, UK. The age limit is 21 to 30 years.
AAO (Insurance Specialist) 310 Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree in any discipline along with a professional qualification in Life Insurance (Fellowship of Insurance Institute of India). They should also have at least 5 years of experience working in IRDAI-regulated Life Insurance Companies. The age limit is 21 to 30 years.
AAO (Legal) 30 Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree in Law with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST and PwBD). They should be enrolled with the Bar Council as an Advocate and must have at least 2 years of experience. The age limit is 21 to 32 years.
