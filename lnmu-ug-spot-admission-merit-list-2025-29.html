LNMU UG SPOT ADMISSION MERIT LIST 2025-29

Post Date: September 7, 2025

The Lalit Narayan Mithila University (LNMU) has released the Spot Admission Merit List for its Undergraduate (UG) courses, including B.A, B.Sc, and B.Com, for the 2025 academic session. Online applications for admission were accepted from May 1, 2025, to May 24, 2025. Candidates who applied for the spot admission can now check the detailed merit list provided below on the official website.

UG Arts

LNMU UG Admission 2025: FAQs

Question: When will the registration process for LNMU UG Spot Admission 2025 begin?

Answer: Lalit Narayan Mithila University (LNMU) will open online registration for vacant seats on September 1, 2025. The window to register and select a college will be available on September 1 and 2, 2025.

Question: Who is eligible to apply for the spot admission round?

Answer: All applicants who were not enrolled based on the third selection list for the four-year undergraduate session (2025-29) are eligible to apply for the vacant seats through the online spot admission process.

Question: Where can I find the list of vacant seats?

Answer: The university has published a subject-wise list of vacant seats by college, which is available on the official LNMU website.

Question: How do I register for the LNMU UG spot admission?

Answer: To apply online for the admission round, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official LNMU website at lnmu.ac.in.
  2. Click on the login link and enter your application ID, date of birth, and password.
  3. Once logged in, you can select your preferred college and fill out the application form.
  4. Proceed to make the payment for the application fee.
  5. Download a copy of the completed form and keep a hard copy for your records.

Question: When will the selection list for spot admission be released?

Answer: The selection list for candidates who have applied for the online spot admission will be released on the university website on September 4, 2025.

Question: What are the admission dates after being selected in the spot round?

Answer: Candidates who are selected can download their selection letter and apply for admission to their allotted college from September 8 to September 10, 2025.
