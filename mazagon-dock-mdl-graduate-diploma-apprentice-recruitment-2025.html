How to Fill MDL Graduate/Diploma Apprentice Online Form 2025
  • Visit official Mazagon Dock MDL apprentice portal at https://mazagondock.in/app/mdlapprentice/Login.aspx.
  • Click on "Apply Online" or "Register" option for new applicants.
  • Create account with valid email ID and mobile number providing personal information.
  • Receive One-Time Password (OTP) on registered email and mobile for verification.
  • Log in using your credentials to access MDL apprentice application form.
  • Select apprentice category (Graduate Apprentice or Diploma Apprentice).
  • Fill all mandatory personal details including name, date of birth, father's name, address carefully.
  • Provide complete educational qualification details including degree/diploma name, specialization, board/university, passing year, marks/percentage.
  • Mention final semester completion date (must be on/after 01 April 2020).
  • Upload recent passport-sized photograph (4.5cm x 3.5cm, JPG format, white background) ensuring clarity and quality.
  • Upload signature in specified dimensions (3cm x 1.5cm, JPG format) using black/blue ink on white paper.
  • Upload degree/diploma certificate copy and final mark sheet for verification.
  • Select preferred MDL facility/workshop from available locations at Mumbai.
  • Review all entered information thoroughly checking against original documents before submission.

Shipbuilding Engineering and Apprenticeship Training Programs

Understanding Naval Vessel Manufacturing and Specialized Technical Training

The MDL apprenticeship encompasses diverse engineering specializations reflecting comprehensive shipbuilding requirements. Civil Engineering apprentices engage in shipyard infrastructure, dock construction, and support facilities. Electrical Engineering apprentices work on ship power systems, propulsion, and onboard electrical networks. Mechanical Engineering apprentices handle machinery, engines, pumps, and mechanical systems installation. Electronics and Telecommunication specialists manage advanced navigation, communication, and warfare systems. Shipbuilding Technology graduates focus directly on hull construction, naval architecture applications, and advanced vessel design. Apprentices gain hands-on experience in advanced manufacturing techniques, precision engineering, quality systems, and naval specifications compliance.

Shipbuilding Specialization Areas:

  • Hull Construction: Ship hull fabrication, welding, structural assembly using advanced techniques
  • Propulsion Systems: Marine engine installation, propeller systems, power generation
  • Navigation & Combat Systems: Advanced radar, sonar, fire control, communication systems integration
  • Electrical Infrastructure: Ship power distribution, lighting, auxiliary systems, battery systems
  • Mechanical Systems: Pumps, compressors, valves, hydraulic systems, steam systems
  • Outfitting & Integration: Equipment installation, system integration, performance testing

Comprehensive Training Framework: Apprentices combine classroom instruction in shipbuilding principles, naval architecture, standards compliance with intensive shipyard assignments. Training at MDL's Mumbai facility provides exposure to active warship and submarine construction programs. Trainees work under experienced technical supervisors gaining practical expertise in modern shipbuilding technologies and manufacturing excellence. Post-apprenticeship successful candidates transition to permanent technical positions in ship design, construction, outfitting, or quality departments. Career progression enables advancement toward senior engineer and management positions overseeing major ship construction projects. Throughout MDL careers, professionals contribute meaningfully to India's maritime defense capabilities and shipbuilding sector development.

MDL History, Shipbuilding Excellence, and Defense Contribution

Understanding India's Premier Naval Shipbuilder and Maritime Legacy

Mazagon Dock Limited (MDL), established as India's leading defense shipbuilding company, specializes in constructing sophisticated naval warships, submarines, and offshore vessels. MDL has delivered 805 vessels since 1960 including 30 advanced warships and 8 submarines establishing extraordinary manufacturing record. The company manufactures state-of-the-art destroyers, frigates, missile boats, submarines, offshore patrol vessels, and specialized maritime equipment for Indian Navy and international clients. MDL maintains highest defense and quality standards ensuring naval platforms meet stringent maritime and combat requirements. The shipbuilder operates advanced manufacturing facilities equipped with modern technologies enabling precision construction and complex system integration. MDL vessels operate worldwide serving India's maritime interests and international naval partnerships.

MDL's Strategic Shipbuilding Programs:

  • Advanced Warships: State-of-the-art destroyers and frigates defending India's maritime interests
  • Submarine Construction: Advanced diesel-electric and future nuclear submarines
  • Offshore Vessels: Patrol, supply, and specialized maritime operation vessels

  • Technology Transfer: Collaboration with international shipbuilders enhancing Indian capabilities
  • Modernization Programs: Continuous upgrades incorporating advanced technologies and systems
