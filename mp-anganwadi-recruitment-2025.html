WCD MP Anganwadi Helper

Candidates who have passed 5th class or an equivalent exam from any recognized board or institution in India are eligible. For complete details, refer to the WCD MP Anganwadi Worker/Helper Recruitment Notification 2025.
WCD MP Anganwadi Worker

Candidates must have passed the 12th class examination or an equivalent qualification from any recognized board or institution in India. This means students who have completed their higher secondary education or any similar level of education from an approved board are eligible to apply.
How To Fill MP Anganwadi Online Form 2025

Visit the official portal

  • Go to chayan.mponline.gov.in (MP Online «Chayan»)

Choose the relevant recruitment link

  • On the homepage, click “Women and Child Development” → select “WCD MP Anganwadi Worker & Assistant 2025”, then hit “Apply Online”

Register or log in

  • New users: Register by entering your mobile number, email, and creating a password.
  • Existing users: Log in using your credentials.

Fill in the application form

  • Provide your personal details (name, DOB, category, etc.)
  • Fill in educational info (5th for Assistant, 12th for Worker)
  • Enter address, district, and bank/payment info

Upload required documents

  • In prescribed formats (PDF/image), upload:
  • Scanned passport-size photo
  • Signature
  • Educational certificate (5th/12th pass)
  • Domicile/residence proof
  • Caste certificate, if applicable

Pay the application fee

  • ₹100 + 18% GST via online methods (debit/credit/UPI/net banking)
  • SC/ST/PwD candidates are exempted

Review & submit the form

  • Double-check all details
  • Submit the form

Download or print the confirmation/receipt for your records
