MP ESB Middle and Primary Teacher Result 2025

Post Date: September 26, 2025 | 11:53 AM IST

The Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has officially released the **Result for the Middle School and Primary School Teacher Examination 2025** on its official website https://esb.mp.gov.in/. This recruitment drive aims to fill a total of 10,758 positions. The exam was conducted on 20 April 2025. Candidates can check their result by logging into the portal using their Enrollment Number / Registration Number / Date of Birth. The direct link to check the result is provided below.

How to Check & Download MPESB School Teacher Result 2025

Follow these steps to successfully check and download your result:

  • Visit the Official Website: Go to the MPESB official portal at https://esb.mp.gov.in.
  • Access the Result Section: On the homepage, look for the 'Results' tab or the link titled **“MP TET Varg 2 Result 2025”** under the latest updates/notifications section.
  • Enter Login Details: Click on the result link and enter your **Enrollment Number / Registration Number** and **Date of Birth** to log in.
  • View and Download: Submit the details to view your scorecard.
  • Printout: Download the result PDF and take a clear printout for future reference.

Why the MPESB Teacher Result 2025 Matters

Importance of MPESB Teacher Result 2025

The release of the MPESB Middle and Primary Teacher Result 2025 is the final stage for over 10,758 posts, including Secondary Teacher, Primary Teacher, and various specialist roles. Candidates who pass the written examination held on 20 April 2025 will proceed to the final merit list and subsequent document verification. This result is crucial for securing a prestigious government teaching position in Madhya Pradesh.

Career Growth in MPESB Teaching Roles

Securing one of the 10,758 vacancies as an MPESB Middle or Primary Teacher provides a stable and respected career in the Madhya Pradesh state education system. Teachers in these roles are responsible for foundational education, offering strong growth prospects and benefits. For details on eligibility and selection, refer to the official MPESB notification.
