MPESB Group 2 Sub Group 3 Recruitment 2025 – Apply Online Now

Post Date: October 29, 2025 | 6:27 PM IST

The Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board (MPESB) has officially released the notification for Group 2 Sub Group 3 Recruitment 2025 on its official website. This prestigious state recruitment offers 454 positions across diverse technical and professional categories including Junior Engineer (Civil, Electrical, Mechanical), Laboratory Technician, Field Officer, Occupational Therapist, Inspector (Supply, Weights & Measures), Assistant Engineer, and Biomedical Engineer. The online application process started on 29 October 2025 and will continue until 12 November 2025. Candidates aged 18-40 years (UR/EWS) and 18-45 years (OBC/SC/ST/PH/Female) as on 01 January 2025 possessing relevant diploma or degree qualifications are eligible to apply. Application fee is ₹560 for General/Others and ₹310 for OBC/SC/ST candidates with multiple online payment options available.

How to Fill MPESB Group 2 Sub Group 3 Online Form 2025
  • Visit the official MPESB online portal at https://esb.mponline.gov.in/Portal/.
  • Click on "Group 2 Sub Group 3" or "Apply Online" option on the examination portal.
  • Register with valid email ID and mobile number if applying for first time.
  • Receive One-Time Password (OTP) on registered email and mobile for verification.
  • Log in using your credentials to access the online application form.
  • Fill all mandatory personal details including name, date of birth, father's name, address, and category carefully.
  • Select the position category (Junior Engineer, Lab Technician, Field Officer, etc.) matching your qualifications.
  • Provide complete educational qualification details including degree/diploma names, institution names, passing years, and marks obtained.
  • Upload recent passport-sized photograph (4.5cm x 3.5cm, JPG format, white background) ensuring clarity.
  • Upload signature in specified dimensions (3cm x 1.5cm, JPG format) using black/blue ink on white paper.
  • Select your preferred examination center from available options across Madhya Pradesh.
  • Review all entered information thoroughly comparing against original documents.
  • Proceed to payment section and pay applicable fee through available online methods.
  • After successful payment, save and print the confirmation document containing registration number.
  • Deadline: 12 November 2025 – Apply immediately to avoid last-minute delays or technical issues.
