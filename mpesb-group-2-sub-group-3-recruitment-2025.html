MPESB Group 2 Sub Group 3 Recruitment 2025

Post Date: September 10, 2025

The Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board (MPESB) has released a notification for the recruitment of Junior Engineer (Civil, Electrical, Mechanical), Laboratory Technician, Field Officer, Occupational Therapist, Inspector (Supply, Weights & Measures), Assistant Engineer, and Biomedical Engineer under Group 2 Sub Group 3. This recruitment offers 339 positions. The online application process began on September 9, 2025 and will close on September 23, 2025. The minimum age is 18 years, with a maximum age of 40 years for UR/EWS and 45 years for OBC/SC/ST/PH/Female as of January 1, 2025, with relaxations as per MPESB rules. Candidates should apply via the official website esb.mp.gov.in and verify all details.

MPESB Group 2 Sub Group 3 Online Form 2025: Eligibility Criteria
  • Junior Engineer (Civil, Electrical, Mechanical): Candidates must have a Diploma or a B.E./B.Tech degree in the relevant engineering field (Civil, Electrical, or Mechanical).
  • Laboratory Technician: Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Science, often with a specialization in Chemistry, Microbiology, or Biology, or a Diploma/Degree in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT/BMLT).
  • Field Officer: Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in a relevant subject, e.g., B.Sc. in Agriculture or Horticulture.
  • Occupational Therapist: Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Occupational Therapy (BOT) from a recognized institution.
  • Inspector (Supply, Weights & Measures): Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline, with additional knowledge of relevant regulations or computer skills possibly required.
  • Assistant Engineer: Candidates must have a B.E./B.Tech in the specific engineering discipline (e.g., Civil, Electrical, Mechanical), with some posts accepting a Master’s degree.
  • Biomedical Engineer: Candidates must have a B.E./B.Tech in Biomedical Engineering or a related field.
