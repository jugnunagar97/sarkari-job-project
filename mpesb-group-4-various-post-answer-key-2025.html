MPESB GROUP 4 VARIOUS POST ANSWER KEY 2025

Post Date: May 14, 2025

The Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has released the Answer Key for the Group-4, Assistant Grade-3, Stenotypist, Stenographer & Other Post Combined Recruitment Test 2025 on its official website. Candidates can check their Answer Key using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the portal. Objections to the provisional answer key could be raised until May 16, 2025. All essential details and direct links are provided below.

HOW TO CHECK & DOWNLOAD MPESB GROUP 4 VARIOUS POST ANSWER KEY 2025
  1. Visit the official MPESB website: esb.mp.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, locate and click the link titled “Online Question/Answer Objection – Group-4, Asstt. Grade-3, Stenotypist, Stenographer & Other Post Combined Recruitment Test – 2024”.
  3. Click on the “Objection Tracker Login” option.
  4. Enter your Application Number and Date of Birth, then fill in the captcha code and click “Sign In”.
  5. Download the MPESB Group 4 Answer Key 2025 in PDF format and compare your responses to estimate your score.
HOW TO RAISE OBJECTIONS TO MPESB GROUP 4 ANSWER KEY 2025
  1. Log in to the Objection Tracker on the MPESB website using your credentials.
  2. Select the question(s) you wish to challenge.
  3. Provide a clear justification with supporting evidence or references.
  4. Upload necessary documents to support your objection.
  5. Pay the objection fee (if applicable) online.
  6. Submit the objection and keep a record for reference.
  7. Check the final answer key on the MPESB website after objections are reviewed.
