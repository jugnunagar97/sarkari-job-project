MPESB Group I Sub Group 3 Answer Key 2025

Post Date: May 28, 2025

Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has released the provisional Answer Key for the Group-1 Sub Group-3 Combined Recruitment Examination 2025. The exam, initially scheduled for May 15, 2025, was conducted on May 23, 2025, due to technical issues. A total of 10,704 candidates appeared for the examination. Candidates can check their Answer Key by logging into the portal (link provided below) using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number. As of today, September 12, 2025, at 1:20 PM IST, candidates are encouraged to review the answer key for accuracy.
