MPESB PBBSC MSC NURSING RESULT 2025

Post Date: August 1, 2025

The Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board (MPESB) has released the result for the BSc Nursing/ MSc Nursing entrance examination. The exam was conducted on 01 July 2025. Candidates who appeared for the test can now check their results by logging into the official portal using their roll number and date of birth. The direct link is provided below.

Important Dates

Online Apply Start Date: 23 May 2025

Last Date for Apply Online: 06 June 2025

Exam Date: 01 July 2025

Admit Card Available: 25 June 2025

Answer Key: 03 July 2025

Result Declared: 31 July 2025

Application Fee

For General, Other State: ₹ 560/-

For MP Reserve Category: ₹ 310/-

Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, etc.

MPESB Nursing Admission 2025: Age Limits as on 31/12/2025

  • Minimum Age: 17 Years
  • Maximum Age: N/A
  • For more details, refer to the official notification.

NA
MPESB Nursing Admission 2025: Course & Eligibility Details
Course Name Eligibility Criteria
Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing (PBBSc)
  • Must have completed a 3-year GNM (General Nursing and Midwifery) course with a minimum of 45% marks.
  • Read the official notification for complete details.
Master of Science Nursing (M.Sc. Nursing)
  • Must have completed B.Sc Nursing or Post Basic B.Sc Nursing with a minimum of 45% marks.
  • Refer to the official notification for detailed criteria.
How To Check & Download MPESB Nursing Result 2025
  1. Visit the official MPESB website: esb.mp.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the “Results” section.
  3. Find and click the link for “MPESB BSc Nursing / MSc Nursing Result 2025”.
  4. Enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth.
  5. Click on “Search” or “Submit” to view your result.
  6. Download or print the result for future reference.

MPESB Nursing Admission 2025: Important Questions

Question: When did the online application for MPESB Nursing Admission 2025 start?

Answer: The online application started on 23 May 2025.

Question: What was the last date to apply for this admission?

Answer: The last date to apply online was 06 June 2025.

Question: What is the age limit for MPESB Nursing Admission?

Answer: The minimum age is 17 years. There is no maximum age limit. Please refer to the official notification for full details.
