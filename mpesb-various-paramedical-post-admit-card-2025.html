MPESB Various Paramedical Post Eligibility Criteria
Post NameEligibility Criteria
Physiotherapist
  • Bachelor’s degree in Physical Therapy (BPT) with registration under the MP Paramedical Council is required.
  • Pay Scale: ₹36,200 – ₹1,14,800 (Level-9).
  • For full details, refer to the official notification.
Counselor
  • Master’s degree in Social Work (MSW) with a Post Graduate Diploma in Counseling and Family Therapy (PGDCFT) is required.
  • Pay Scale: ₹25,300 – ₹80,500 (Level-6).
Pharmacist Grade II
  • 10+2 (Intermediate) with PCB subjects, along with a Degree or Diploma in Pharmacy and registration with the MP Pharmacy Council is required.
  • Pay Scale: ₹25,300 – ₹80,500 (Level-6).
Ophthalmic Assistant
  • 10+2 (Intermediate) with PCB subjects and a Diploma in Ophthalmic Assistant is required, along with registration under the MP Paramedical Council.
  • Pay Scale: ₹28,700 – ₹91,300 (Level-7).
O.T. Technician
  • 10+2 (Intermediate) with PCB subjects and a 1-year course in Operation Theatre Technician is required, along with registration under the MP Paramedical Council.
  • Pay Scale: ₹25,300 – ₹80,500 (Level-6).
How to Check & Download MPESB Various Paramedical Post Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official website at esb.mp.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the “Admit Card” tab or Latest Updates/Notifications section.
  • Look for the link titled “Various Paramedical Post Admit Card 2025.”
  • Enter your Application Number and Date of Birth (or other required details).
  • Your admit card will be displayed on the screen with exam details.
  • Download and save the PDF copy of the admit card to your device.
  • Take a clear printout of the admit card to carry to the examination center.
