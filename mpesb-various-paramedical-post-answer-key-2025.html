How To Check & Download MPESB Paramedical Answer Key 2025
  • Visit the official website of MPESB at esb.mp.gov.in.

  • If you find discrepancy, submit objections during objection window (29 Sept - 3 Oct 2025) with valid supporting evidence.

Paramedical Careers: MP Healthcare System's Backbone

Filling 752 Paramedical Positions Serving Madhya Pradesh's Public Health Infrastructure

The MPESB Paramedical Recruitment 2025 fills 752 Group 5 positions across five specialized categories serving Madhya Pradesh's healthcare system. Paramedical professionals including Pharmacists (313), O.T. Technicians (288), Counselors (100), Ophthalmic Assistants (100), and Physiotherapists (41) provide crucial healthcare support services including medication management, surgical assistance, mental health counseling, eye care, and rehabilitation therapy serving state's 8 crore+ population across 52 districts ensuring quality healthcare delivery in government hospitals, primary health centers, community health centers contributing India's public health mission.

Post-Wise Job Responsibilities:

  • Physiotherapist (41 posts): Physical rehabilitation, therapeutic exercises, pain management, mobility restoration, post-surgical recovery

  • Pharmacist/OT Tech/Counselor (Level-6): ₹25,300-₹80,500/month with in-hand ₹35,000-₹45,000 initially
  • Annual increments: Regular salary progression with yearly increments ensuring income growth

MP Government paramedical staff enjoy comprehensive state benefits including DA (50%), HRA, medical coverage, GPF, pension, LTC, transfer allowances, children education benefits, promotion opportunities to senior positions ensuring rewarding careers serving state's healthcare infrastructure.

Answer Key Analysis: Exam Pattern and Expected Cutoff Marks

Provisional Answer Key Released on 29 September with Objection Window Till 3 October 2025

The MPESB Paramedical Answer Key 2025 enables thousands of candidates calculating expected scores comparing their responses with official answers before result declaration. The answer key released for Computer-Based Test conducted 27 September covering general awareness, reasoning ability, subject-specific knowledge allowing candidates verifying their marked answers identifying discrepancies filing evidence-based objections during five-day window ensuring transparent fair evaluation process with expert committee reviewing challenges before preparing final answer key determining selection merit lists.

Exam Pattern Overview:

  • Total questions: 100-150 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) depending on post category
  • Total marks: 100-150 marks (each question carries 1 mark)
  • Duration: 2-3 hours depending on post requirements and exam complexity
  • General Awareness: Current affairs, MP GK, Indian history, geography, polity, healthcare developments
  • General Intelligence: Reasoning, logical thinking, analytical ability, problem-solving, mental aptitude
  • Subject-Specific Knowledge: Professional competence testing post-specific technical skills, procedures, protocols
  • For Pharmacist: Pharmaceutical chemistry, pharmacology, dispensing, drug interactions, hospital pharmacy
  • For Physiotherapist: Anatomy, physiology, therapeutic exercises, rehabilitation techniques, physical therapy
  • For OT Technician: Surgical instruments, sterilization, aseptic techniques, operation theatre protocols
  • Negative marking: 0.25 marks (1/4th mark) deducted for each wrong answer
  • Exam mode: Computer-based test conducted at authorized centers across Madhya Pradesh

Marks Calculation Method:

  • Formula: Total Score = (Correct Answers × 1) - (Wrong Answers × 0.25)
  • Example: 110 correct + 25 wrong + 15 unattempted (for 150Q exam) = (110 × 1) - (25 × 0.25) = 110 - 6.25 = 103.75 marks
  • Percentage calculation: (Total Score / Total Marks) × 100 = Percentage achieved
  • Category-wise qualification: Different cutoff marks for UR, OBC, SC, ST, PH categories

Expected Cutoff Marks (Post-Wise):

  • Physiotherapist UR: 95-110/150 (63-73% marks) due to higher qualification requirements
  • Pharmacist UR: 85-100/150 (57-67% marks) considering moderate competition
  • OT Technician UR: 82-97/150 (55-65% marks) with technical skill emphasis
  • Counselor UR: 88-103/150 (59-69% marks) testing psychological knowledge
  • Ophthalmic Assistant UR: 80-95/150 (53-63% marks) requiring specialized eye care knowledge
  • OBC category: 7-10 marks relaxation from UR cutoff typically
  • SC category: 15-20 marks relaxation from UR cutoff
  • ST category: 20-25 marks relaxation from UR cutoff
  • Cutoff varies: Post-wise, category-wise cutoffs differ based on vacancies, difficulty level, candidate performance

Objection Filing Process:

  • Objection window: 29 September - 3 October 2025 (5-day period for raising challenges)
  • Access portal: Visit challenge portal (paramedicalcrt2025questionchallenge.cbtexam.in) using login credentials
  • Question selection: Choose specific question number to challenge from displayed answer key
  • Evidence submission: Upload clear supporting documents from standard textbooks, pharmaceutical references, medical literature
  • Valid references: Only objections with proper citations from recognized medical/pharmaceutical books, government guidelines accepted
  • Objection fee: Pay prescribed fee per question through online payment gateway
  • Expert committee review: Medical and pharmaceutical subject experts evaluate each objection considering merit, evidence
  • Fee refund: If objection accepted and answer key modified, full fee refunded within 4-6 weeks
  • Final answer key: Released after objection evaluation used for result preparation, no further appeals

Candidates scoring above expected cutoffs should prepare for interview/viva-voce stage, document verification including registration certificates, and medical examination ensuring readiness for joining Madhya Pradesh health services serving state's medical infrastructure.

Paramedical Career Growth: Progression to Senior Healthcare Positions

₹35,000-₹60,000 Starting Salary with Promotions to Supervisory and Administrative Roles

The MP Paramedical careers offer structured progression path advancing from entry-level positions to senior supervisory and administrative roles. Paramedical professionals gain valuable healthcare experience, develop clinical skills, master department procedures, and qualify for promotions to senior positions including Chief Pharmacist, Senior Physiotherapist, OT Supervisor managing healthcare units serving Madhya Pradesh's expanding medical infrastructure ensuring quality patient care contributing state's healthcare excellence serving crores of patients annually.

Career Advancement Path:

  • Paramedical Staff (0-5 years): Entry level providing direct patient care, technical support, clinical assistance under supervision
  • Senior Paramedical Staff (5-10 years): Increased responsibilities, mentoring juniors, quality assurance, protocol implementation
  • Supervisor (10-15 years): Department supervision, staff management, inventory control, quality monitoring
  • Chief/Senior positions (15-20 years): Heading departments, strategic planning, policy implementation, training coordination
  • Administrative roles (20-25 years): Hospital administration, district-level coordination, state health programs management

Work Responsibilities and Environment:

  • Clinical duties: Direct patient care, technical procedures, diagnostic assistance, therapeutic interventions
  • Documentation: Maintaining patient records, treatment documentation, inventory registers, quality reports
  • Quality assurance: Following protocols, infection control, safety standards, accreditation compliance
  • Patient education: Health awareness, medication counseling, exercise guidance, preventive care education
  • Team coordination: Working with doctors, nurses, other paramedical staff ensuring comprehensive patient care
  • Continuing education: Attending workshops, conferences, training programs maintaining professional competence

Comprehensive Benefits Package:

  • Basic salary: Pay Level 6-9 with regular annual increments ensuring steady income growth
  • Dearness Allowance: 50% of basic salary (revised biannually based on inflation index)
  • House Rent Allowance: 8-24% of basic depending on posting location (X, Y, Z class cities)
  • Transport Allowance: ₹1,600-₹3,600/month for commuting expenses
  • Medical facilities: Government hospital treatment + CGHS coverage + medical reimbursement for family
  • GPF accumulation: General Provident Fund with interest building retirement corpus
  • Pension: Monthly pension after retirement ensuring post-retirement financial security
  • Transfer allowances: Relocation assistance for inter-district transfers
  • Children education: Education allowance for children's schooling expenses
  • LTC: Leave Travel Concession for family travel covering transportation costs

Work Culture and Environment:

  • Working hours: Shift-based duties (8-hour shifts) with rotating schedule ensuring 24x7 healthcare coverage
  • Posting locations: District hospitals, medical colleges, CHCs, PHCs across 52 districts of Madhya Pradesh
  • Infrastructure: Modern equipment, updated facilities, continuous infrastructure development in government hospitals
  • Professional development: Regular training programs, skill upgradation workshops, department seminars
  • Transfer policy: Transfers every 3-5 years ensuring diverse experience across different healthcare settings

MP Paramedical positions provide rewarding careers combining job satisfaction of patient care, stable government employment, comprehensive benefits, professional growth opportunities, social respect making paramedical service attractive profession for diploma and graduate candidates aspiring healthcare careers serving state's medical infrastructure.
