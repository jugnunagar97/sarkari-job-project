NFL NON EXECUTIVES VARIOUS POST 2024 RESULT/ SCORE CARD

Post Date: July 10, 2025

National Fertilizers Limited (NFL) has released the result and score card for the Non-Executive Various Posts Recruitment 2024. The exams for 336 vacancies were held in December 2024. Candidates who appeared for the test can now check their results by logging into the official portal using their registration number and date of birth. The direct link is provided below.

Important Dates

Application Start Date: 09 October 2024

Last Date to Apply: 08 November 2024

Exam Date: 7, 8, 14, 15 & 21 December 2024

Admit Card Available: 04 December 2024

Result Date: 10 July 2025

Application Fee

General, EWS, OBC: ₹ 200/-

SC, ST, PWD: ₹ 0/-

Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, etc.

NFL Recruitment 2024: Age Limits as on 30/09/2024

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years
  • Maximum Age: 30 Years
  • Age relaxation is provided as per NFL regulations.

Total Post

336
NFL Non-Executive Recruitment 2024: Eligibility Details
Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Non-Executive (Various Posts)
  • Matriculation (10th Pass) with a regular ITI certificate in the relevant trade (e.g., Welder, Electrician).
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved categories).
  • Please refer to the official notification for detailed post-wise eligibility.
How To Download NFL Non-Executive Result 2024
  1. Visit the official NFL website: www.nationalfertilizers.com.
  2. On the homepage, click on the ‘Careers’ tab.
  3. Find the link for “Recruitment of Non-Executives in NFL-2024”.
  4. Click on the ‘Result/Score Card Download Link’.
  5. Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth to log in and view your result.
  6. Download and print the result for future reference.
