NIACL AO Scale I Examination 2025: Eligibility Criteria
  • Accounts: Chartered Accountant (ICAI) or Cost and Management Accountant (from The Institute of Cost Accountants of India, formerly ICWAI), AND Graduate/Postgraduate in any discipline with a minimum of 60% marks (or 55% for SC/ST/PwBD categories), OR MBA (Finance) / PGDM (Finance) / M.Com with a minimum of 60% marks (or 55% for SC/ST/PwBD candidates).
  • Generalists: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in any discipline from a recognized university, or an equivalent qualification recognized by the Central Government, with a minimum of 60% marks for General category candidates and 55% marks for SC/ST/PwBD candidates in either degree examination.
