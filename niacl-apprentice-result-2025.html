NIACL APPRENTICE RESULT 2025

Post Date: August 11, 2025

New India Assurance Company Ltd. (NIACL) has released the result for the Apprentices Recruitment Examination 2025. This recruitment is for 500 positions. The exam was held on 26 June 2025. Candidates who participated can now check their results using their credentials on the official portal. The direct link to the result is provided below.

Important Dates

Online Apply Start Date: 06 June 2025

Online Apply Last Date: 20 June 2025

Last Date For Fee Payment: 20 June 2025

Exam Date: 26 June 2025

Result Date: 11 August 2025

Application Fee

For General/ OBC/ EWS: ₹ 944/-

For SC/ ST/ Female (Bihar): ₹ 708/-

For PH Candidates: ₹ 472/-

Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, etc.

NIACL Apprentices Notification 2025: Age Limits as on 01/08/2025

  • Minimum Age: 21 Years
  • Maximum Age: 30 Years
  • Age relaxation is provided as per NIACL regulations.

Total Post

500
NIACL Apprentices Recruitment 2025: Vacancy & Eligibility
Post Name No. of Posts Eligibility Criteria
Apprentices 500
  • Candidates must have a Graduation Degree in any stream from a recognized university.
How To Check & Download NIACL Apprentices Result 2025
  1. Visit the official NIACL website.
  2. Navigate to the “Recruitment” or “Career” section.
  3. Look for the notification titled “NIACL Apprentice Result 2025”.
  4. Click on the link to open the result PDF.
  5. Download and save the PDF to your device.
  6. Use the search function (Ctrl+F) to find your roll number and confirm your selection status.
