NIACL Assistant Eligibility (As of 1/12/2024):
Age Limit Qualification

Candidates must be 21-30 years old (born between 02/12/1994 and 01/12/2003, both inclusive). Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per official notification.
  • A Graduation degree in any stream from a recognized university.
  • Candidates must also possess proficiency in the language of the State/UT they are applying for.
Steps to Apply for the NIACL Assistant 2024

Candidates have to follow the steps provided below to fill out their application form for NIACL Assistant 2024.

  • Step 1: Visit the official website of New India Assurance Company Limited by going to www.newindia.co.in.
  • Step 2: Once on the homepage, navigate to the “Recruitment” tab located at the top of the page. From there, locate the details for NIACL Assistant Recruitment 2024 and follow the provided link to apply online.
  • Step 3: Select the “Click Here for New Registration” option and input your correct details, including your name, contact information, and email address.
  • Step 4: After providing the required information, a unique registration number and password will be generated. Ensure to securely save this information by jotting it down, taking a photo, or capturing a screenshot.
  • Step 5: Proceed to complete the NIACL Assistant 2024 application form by supplying all necessary details, and then click on ‘Save and Next.’
  • Step 6: Carefully review all entered information for accuracy before hitting the ‘Submit’ button.
  • Step 7: Select your preferred payment option and proceed to make the necessary payment for the application fee.
Documents Required for NIACL Assistant Application Form 2024

The list given below mentions the required documents and their appropriate sizes. Without it, the application form is incomplete.

  • The photograph needs to have a light-coloured background (White Wall).
  • Signatures must be made using a black ink pen on a white sheet of paper.
  • Handwritten declarations should be written in English and not in uppercase letters; other formats will not be accepted.
  • Left-hand thumb impressions should be clear and not blurry. Candidates without a left thumb can use their right-hand thumb impression instead.
