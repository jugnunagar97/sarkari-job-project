NTA CUET UG RESULT 2025

Post Date: July 4, 2025

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has released the result for the Common University Entrance Test (CUET) UG 2025. The exams were conducted from 08 May to 01 June 2025. Candidates who appeared for the test can now check their results and download their score card by logging into the official portal using their application number and date of birth. The direct link is provided below.

Important Dates

Apply Online Start Date: 01 March 2025

Last Date for Apply Online: 24 March 2025

Correction Date: 26-27 March 2025

Exam Date: 08 May – 01 June 2025

Answer Key: 17 June 2025

Result Declared: 04 July 2025

Application Fee

General: ₹ 1000/-

EWS, OBC: ₹ 900/-

SC, ST, PH: ₹ 800/-

Additional Subject Charge Applicable.

NTA CUET UG 2025: Age Limit

  • There is no age limit for appearing in the CUET UG 2025 Examination.

Total Seats

NA
NTA CUET UG 2025: Eligibility Criteria
Exam Name Eligibility Criteria
CUET UG 2025 Examination
  • Candidates who have passed or are appearing for the 10+2 (Intermediate) exam from any recognized board in India are eligible.
How To Check & Download NTA CUET UG Result 2025
  1. Visit the official CUET UG portal: exams.nta.ac.in/CUET-UG.
  2. On the homepage, find and click the link for “CUET UG 2025 Result / Score Card”.
  3. Log in using your Application Number and Date of Birth.
  4. Your scorecard will be displayed on the screen.
  5. Download the result PDF and save it for future reference and counselling processes.
