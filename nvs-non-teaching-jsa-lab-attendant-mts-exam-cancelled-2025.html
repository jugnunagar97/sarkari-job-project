NVS Non-Teaching JSA, Lab Attendant & MTS Exam Cancelled 2025

Post Date: September 12, 2025 2:02 PM

The Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) has released an exam cancelled notice for the recruitment of various non-teaching posts including Staff Nurse, ASO, Translator, Assistant, Stenographer, MTS, Supervisor, Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), and Electrician. The application process began on 22 March 2024 and ended on 14 May 2024, with the exam originally held from 14-19 May 2025. Candidates can check and download their results using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number via the portal link provided below.

How To Check & Download NVS Non Teaching Various Post Result 2025
  • Visit the Official NVS Website: Open the Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti’s official website and go to the “Recruitment” or “Results” section.
  • Locate the Result Notification: Look for the entry titled “Result – Non-Teaching Various Posts 2025” under the current recruitment updates.
  • Open the Result PDF: Click on that entry to view a PDF containing roll numbers or names of candidates who have qualified or are shortlisted.
  • Find Your Roll Number: Use the search function (Ctrl + F) within the PDF to quickly locate your roll number or name.
  • Save & Print the Document: Download a copy of the PDF and keep it saved on your device. Print a clear copy for your records and any further recruitment stages (like document verification).
