NVS NON TEACHING VARIOUS POST RESULT 2025

Post Date: July 15, 2025

Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) has released the result for the recruitment of Non-Teaching Various Posts including Staff Nurse, ASO, JSA, MTS, and others. This recruitment is for 1377 positions. The exam was conducted from 14-19 May 2025. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now check their results by logging into the official portal with their credentials.

Important Dates

Application Start: 22 March 2024

Last Date to Apply: 14 May 2024

Correction Date: 16-18 May 2024

Exam Date: 14-19 May 2025

Answer Key: 10 June 2025

Result Declared: 15 July 2025

Application Fee

Female Staff Nurse: ₹ 1500/-

All Other Posts: ₹ 1000/-

SC / ST / PH: ₹ 500/-

Payment Mode (Online)

NVS Recruitment 2024: Age Limits

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years
  • Maximum Age: Varies (27 to 40 Years)
  • Age relaxation is provided as per NVS regulations.

Total Post

1377
NVS Non-Teaching Recruitment 2024: Vacancy & Eligibility
Post Name Total Posts Eligibility Criteria
Female Staff Nurse 121 Bachelor’s degree in Nursing and registered with a State Nursing Council.
Assistant Section Officer (ASO) 05 Bachelor’s degree with 3 years of experience.
Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA) 381 10+2 (Intermediate) with a typing speed of 30 WPM (English) or 25 WPM (Hindi).
Lab Attendant 161 10th Pass with a Diploma in Laboratory Technique OR 10+2 with Science Stream.
Mess Helper 442 10th Pass with 5 years of experience.
Multi Tasking Staff (MTS) 19 Class 10th (High School) pass.
How To Check & Download NVS Non-Teaching Post Result 2025
  1. Visit the official Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti website.
  2. Go to the “Recruitment” or “Results” section.
  3. Find the link titled “Result – Non‑Teaching Various Posts 2025”.
  4. Click the link to open the result PDF.
  5. Use the search function (Ctrl+F) to find your name or roll number.
  6. Download and save the PDF for future reference.
