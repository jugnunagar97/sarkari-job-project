OFSS BIHAR 11TH INTERMEDIATE SPOT ADMISSION 2025-27

Post Date: September 9, 2025

The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has released a notification regarding Spot Admission for the Intermediate (11th Class) session of 2025-27 through its Online Facilitation System for Students (OFSS) portal. The application window for this spot admission round has been reopened and will be available from 09 September 2025 to 12 September 2025. Students who are seeking admission into colleges and schools in Bihar should review the detailed information provided below to complete the process.

OFSS BIHAR 11TH ADMISSION 2025 : SHORT DETAILS
Important Dates Application Fee

Initial Application: 24 April - 20 May 2025

Re-Open Application: 04 - 06 July 2025

1st Merit List Admission: 04-10 June 2025

2nd Merit List Admission: 15-19 July 2025

3rd Merit List Admission: 28-31 July 2025

Spot Admission: 04-18 August 2025

Spot Admission Re-Open: 09 - 12 September 2025

For All Categories: ₹ 350/-

Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, or Internet Banking.

OFSS BIHAR 11TH ADMISSIONS NOTIFICATION 2025 : AGE LIMITS AS ON 03 MAY 2025

  • For detailed information regarding age limits, please refer to the official notification on the OFSS Bihar website.

Total Seats

Not Applicable
OFSS BIHAR 11TH ADMISSIONS 2025 ONLINE FORM : ADMISSION DETAILS
Course Name Eligibility Criteria
Class 11th Intermediate (Arts, Science, Commerce, Agriculture, Vocational)
  • Candidates must have passed the Class 10 (Matriculation/High School) examination.
  • Applicable for students from BSEB, CBSE, ICSE, and other recognized state boards.
How To Fill OFSS Bihar 11th Intermediate Spot Admission 2025-27
  • Go to the official OFSS portal during the spot admission period.
  • Select the "Spot Admission" option and read the provided instructions carefully.
  • Complete the Common Application Form (CAF) with all required personal and academic information.
  • Upload a recent passport-sized photograph and complete mobile verification with an OTP.
  • Submit the application fee of ₹350 through the available online payment methods.
  • After successful payment, download and print the final acknowledgment receipt.
OFSS Bihar 11th Admissions Online Form 2025 : Mode Of Selection
  • Selection will be based on the Merit List prepared from the student's Class 10 marks.
