ONGC Apprentice Recruitment 2025 – Apply Online Now

Post Date: October 17, 2025 | 5:41 PM IST

The Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited (ONGC) has officially released the notification for Apprentice Recruitment 2025 on its official website. This massive recruitment offers 2,623 positions for Trade, Diploma, and Graduate Apprentices across India. The online application process started on 16 October 2025 and will continue until 06 November 2025. Candidates aged 18-24 years as on 06 November 2025 (born between 06/11/2001 and 06/11/2007) possessing 10th, ITI, Diploma, or Graduation in relevant fields are eligible to apply. Application fee is completely FREE for all candidates. Selection is based on merit list, document verification, and medical examination. Merit list will be published on 26 November 2025. This represents an exceptional opportunity to launch careers in India's premier energy sector (Advt. No. : ONGC/APPR/1/2025).

How to Fill ONGC Apprentice Online Form 2025
  • Visit official apprenticeship portals for different apprentice categories on official ONGC website.
  • For Trade Apprentice: Visit https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/.
  • For Graduate/Technical Apprentice: Visit https://nats.education.gov.in/.
  • Click on "Apply Online" or "Register" option on the respective portal.
  • Create account with valid email ID and mobile number if you are a new user.
  • Verify email and mobile through OTP sent to registered contacts.
  • Log in using your credentials to access ONGC apprentice application form.
  • Fill all mandatory personal details including name, date of birth, father's name, address carefully.
  • Provide complete educational qualification details matching your apprentice category (Trade/Diploma/Graduate).
  • Mention relevant skills and certifications for the applied apprentice position.
  • Upload recent passport-sized photograph (4.5cm x 3.5cm, JPG format, white background) ensuring clarity.
  • Upload signature in specified dimensions (3cm x 1.5cm, JPG format) using black/blue ink on white paper.
  • Select preferred ONGC location/facility from available options across India.
  • Review all entered information thoroughly before final submission.
  • Submit application form after verification.
  • Deadline: 06 November 2025 – Apply immediately to avoid last-minute delays.

Apprenticeship Categories, Trade Specializations, and Technical Training

Understanding Trade, Diploma, and Graduate Apprentice Categories

The ONGC Apprentice Recruitment 2025 encompasses three comprehensive apprenticeship categories accommodating diverse educational qualifications. Trade Apprentices require 10th pass with ITI certification in relevant trades including Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Instrumentation, Welding, and Fitter specializations providing hands-on skill development. Diploma Apprentices require 2-3 year Diploma degrees in engineering disciplines ensuring theoretical and practical technical expertise. Graduate Apprentices require Bachelor's degrees in engineering, technology, or related fields enabling advanced technical roles. Each category offers specialized training aligned with ONGC operations including petroleum exploration, drilling, production, refining, transportation, and safety management. Apprentices receive structured technical training combining classroom education with field assignments gaining practical experience on active oil/gas facilities.

Major Apprentice Trade Specializations Available:

  • Mechanical Trade: Equipment operation, maintenance, assembly, mechanical system troubleshooting in petroleum operations
  • Electrical Trade: Power systems, electrical equipment maintenance, grid operation, safety management in energy installations
  • Electronics/Instrumentation: Control systems, monitoring equipment, advanced instrumentation, automation technologies
  • Welding/Fabrication: Precision welding, metal fabrication, pipeline construction, structural assembly
  • Civil/Construction: Infrastructure development, site construction, facility management at oil/gas locations
  • Geology/Petroleum Technology: Subsurface analysis, drilling technology, production engineering, reservoir management

Training Framework Benefits: ONGC apprentices receive training from industry experts in state-of-the-art facilities with internationally recognized standards. Apprentices combine theoretical learning with practical fieldwork at operating facilities gaining real-world experience managing complex petroleum operations. Apprenticeship durations vary from 1-4 years based on specialization ensuring comprehensive skill development. Post-apprenticeship, performers receive opportunity for permanent employment in ONGC contributing meaningfully to India's energy independence throughout their professional careers in the dynamic petroleum sector.

Understanding ONGC Functions and Distinguished Apprenticeship Career Pathways

ONGC apprenticeship programs provide young professionals entry into prestigious energy sector career with opportunities for advancement toward supervisory, management, and specialized technical positions throughout their distinguished careers in India's petroleum industry.

Lifetime Career Impact: ONGC apprentices launch meaningful careers contributing India's petroleum sector development while advancing professional expertise through diverse technical assignments across exploration, production, refining, and distribution operations.
