What is the Pan Card

The Permanent Account Number (PAN) is a crucial identification document for taxpayers in India. It is essential to ensure that the information on your PAN card is accurate and up to date. This article provides a clear guide on how to correct or update your PAN details through both online and offline methods. It also outlines the required forms, documents, and applicable fees to help you complete the process smoothly and efficiently.

How to Change PAN Card Details

There may be errors in your name, your parent’s name, or your date of birth when your PAN card is issued. Additionally, your name or address may change after the card has been issued. In such cases, it is important to update the PAN card with the correct information, including your name, parent’s name, address, or date of birth. You can update these details either online or offline.

Document Required for Pan Card

  • Aadhar Card
  • Voter Identify Card (Voter ID)
  • Arms License
  • Passport
  • Driving License (DL)
  • Pension Card with Photograph
  • Ration Card
  • Photo ID Card Issued By Any Central / State Government Authority
  • Any Other Related Supported Document
How to Apply For New PAN Card

Click on ” New PAN Card Registration” Tab to fill online Application form for New PAN card.

  • Now Fill your PAN Card application form online
  • Select your Required Documents which you have
  • Click on Submit
  • Verify your details and click on Proceed to Payment Button.
  • Make payment This is one time fee. No Hidden or extra cost.
  • After successful Payment, download and print your pre-filled application form (along with Instruction sheet and Blank Application Form).
  • Paste photo, sign, attach documents and post to address (You will get the postal address in instruction sheet.)
  • Now you will get your pan card to your address within 30 Days.
