How To Check & Download Patna High Court Mazdoor Answer Key 2025
  • Visit the official Patna High Court website: patnahighcourt.gov.in
  • Navigate to the “Recruitments” or “Latest Notices” section.
  • Look for a notice titled “Notice along with Model Answer regarding Regular Mazdoor Recruitment Examination, 2025”.
  • Click to download the PDF. This will open the answer key.
  • Compare your responses with the model answers to estimate your score.
  • Raise objections if applicable within the specified window.
  • Await the Final Key, which will be used to prepare the results.
