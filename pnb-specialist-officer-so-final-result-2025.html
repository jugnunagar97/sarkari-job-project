PNB Specialist Officer SO Final Result 2025

Post Date: September 5, 2025

Punjab National Bank (PNB) has officially published the final results for the PNB Specialist Officer (SO) Recruitment Examination 2025. The application window for this recruitment closed on March 24, 2025, with the examination taking place in April 2025. Candidates who participated in the selection process can now view their final results on the PNB official website. To access the results, applicants will need to log in using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. Direct links are provided in the section below for convenience.

HOW TO CHECK & DOWNLOAD PNB SPECIALIST OFFICER SO FINAL RESULT 2025
  1. Navigate to the Official PNB Website: Open your browser and go to https://www.pnbindia.in.
  2. Find the Recruitment Section: On the homepage, locate and click on the "Recruitment" tab.
  3. Select the Relevant Notification: Look for the link related to "Recruitment for Specialist Officers under HRP 2025-26".
  4. Open the Result Link: Click on the link that says "Click Here to Download the Result".
  5. Enter Your Credentials: Input your Registration Number/Roll Number and Password/Date of Birth as required.
  6. Complete Verification: Enter the security CAPTCHA code shown on the screen.
  7. View and Download: Your PNB SO Final Result will be displayed. Download it and take a printout for your records.
