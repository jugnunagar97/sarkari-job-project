Railway RPF SI Final Result 2025

Post Date: August 27, 2025 3:51 PM

Railway Protection Force RPF, has declared the Final Result. Candidates can check their exam date by using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the official portal (link provided below). The RPF Sub Inspector (SI) PET / PMT Exam was conducted in June 22 to July 02, 2025, and the Final Result has been released.

Railway RPF Sub Inspector SI Examination 2025 : Short Details
Important Dates Application Fee
  • Application Begin : 15 April 2024
  • Last Date for Apply Online : 14 May 2024
  • Last Date Pay Exam Fee : 14 May 2024
  • Correction / Modified Form : 15 – 24 May 2024
  • Re Upload Photo / Signature : 15 – 17 June 2024
  • Exam Date : 02, 03, 09, 12 & 13 December 2024
  • Exam City Available : 20 February 2025
  • Admit Card Available : 26 February 2025
  • Result Declared Date : 03 March 2025
  • Score Card Released Date : 07 March 2025
  • PET / PMT Exam Date : 22 June – 02 July 2025
  • General, OBC, EWS : ₹ 500/-
  • SC, ST : ₹ 250/-
  • PH : ₹ 250/-
  • Female (All Category) : ₹ 250/-
  • Application Edit / Modification Charge : ₹ 250/-
  • Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, Internet Banking, IMPS, Cash Card / Mobile Wallet

Age Limits As On 01 July 2024

  • Constable : 18 – 28 Years
  • Sub Inspector : 20 – 28 Years
  • Railway provides age relaxation for the RPF Sub Inspector, Constable position as per their regulations.

Steps To Check & Download RPF Sub Inspector SI Final Result 2025
  1. Visit the official RPF website: rpf.indianrailways.gov.in.
  2. Go to the “Recruitment” or “Careers” section.
  3. Click on the link titled “RPF SI Final Result 2025”.
  4. Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth.
  5. Submit the details to access your result.
  6. Download and print the result for future reference.
