Railway RRB ALP Bharti 2024 : Education Qualification
  • All candidates who have passed the Class 10th High School Examination from any recognized board in India and possess an ITI certificate are eligible to apply for this recruitment. Or
  • All candidates who have completed Class 10th from any recognized board in India along with a diploma in Mechanical/Electrical/Electronics/Automobile Engineering are eligible to apply for this recruitment. Or
  • The candidate must have completed a BE/B.Tech degree in Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, or Automobile Engineering from a recognized university.

Steps To Check & Download the Railway RRB ALP CBAT Re-Exam City Details 2025
  • Visit the official Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) portal of your region.
  • Find the ALP CBAT Re-Exam 2025 City Intimation link on the homepage.
  • Click on the link and enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth/Password.
  • Submit the details to log in.
  • Your Exam City, Date, and Shift details will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout for reference.
