RAILWAY RRB ALP CBT-2 RESULT 2025

Post Date: July 3, 2025

The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has declared the ALP CBT-2 Result. The Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) CBT-II exam was held from May 02 to 06, 2025. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now check their results by logging into the official portal using their credentials. The direct link is provided below.

Important Dates

Application Start: 20 January 2024

Last Date to Apply: 19 February 2024

CBT-1 Exam: 25-29 November 2024

CBT-1 Result: 26 February 2025

CBT-2 Exam Date: 02 – 06 May 2025

CBT-2 Result: 01 July 2025

CBAT Exam Date: 15 July 2025

Application Fee

General, OBC, EWS: ₹ 500/-

SC, ST, PH, All Female: ₹ 250/-

Fee Refund: ₹400 for General & ₹250 for others after appearing in Stage I Exam.

Railway RRB ALP 2024: Age Limits as on 01/07/2024

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years
  • Maximum Age: 33 Years
  • Age relaxation is provided as per RRB regulations.

Total Post

18,799
Railway RRB ALP Recruitment 2024: Eligibility Details
Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP)
  • Class 10th with ITI certificate in the relevant trade, OR
  • Class 10th with a diploma in Mechanical/Electrical/Electronics/Automobile Engineering, OR
  • BE/B.Tech degree in Mechanical/Electrical/Electronics/Automobile Engineering.
How To Check & Download RRB ALP CBT-2 Result 2025
  1. Visit the official website of your respective RRB region (e.g., rrbcdg.gov.in).
  2. On the homepage, look for the link “CEN‑01/2024 Result of 2nd Stage CBT for ALP”.
  3. Click the link to download the zone-wise result PDF.
  4. Open the PDF and use the search function (Ctrl+F) to find your Roll Number.
  5. If your roll number is on the list, you have qualified for the next stage.
  6. Download and save the PDF for future reference.
