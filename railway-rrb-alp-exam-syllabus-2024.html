How to Apply

जो भी इच्छुक उम्मीदवार Railway RRB ALP पद के लिए आवेदन करना चाहते हैं वो 19 फरवरी 2024 से पहले नीचे दिए गए लिंक से आवेदन कर सकते हैं या RRB की Official Website से Online माध्यम से Apply कर सकते हैं।

Mode of Selection
  • CBT Stage-I and Stage-II Exam (सीबीटी स्टेज- I और स्टेज- II परीक्षा)
  • Document Verification (दस्तावेज़ सत्यापन)
  • Medical Examination (चिकित्सा परीक्षण)
RRB ALP Exam Pattern Overview
  • CBT 1: 75 questions, 75 marks, 60 minutes. Qualifying in nature (no sectional cutoff, but overall qualifying marks apply). Negative Marking: 1/3 mark deducted for wrong answers.
  • CBT 2 Part A: 100 questions, 100 marks, 90 minutes. Qualifying in nature. Negative Marking: 1/3 mark deducted.
  • CBT 2 Part B: 75 questions, 75 marks, 60 minutes. Qualifying in nature (trade-specific).
  • CBAT: Qualifying aptitude test for shortlisted candidates from CBT 2 (minimum 42 marks in Part A required to qualify for CBAT).
  • Document Verification & Medical Exam: Final stage for selected candidates.

The exam is conducted online (computer-based). Total marks for selection are based on CBT 2, with CBAT qualifying.

Preparation Tips for CBT 1: Focus on 10th-grade NCERT books for General Science and Mathematics. Practice previous year papers and mock tests to improve speed (60 minutes for 75 questions). Qualifying marks: UR/EWS: 40%, OBC/SC: 30%, ST: 25%.

RRB ALP Syllabus for CBT 2

Part A (Qualifying)

  • Duration: 90 minutes
  • Questions/Marks: 100 MCQs, 100 marks
  • Negative Marking: 1/3 mark deducted
SectionNo. of QuestionsMarksTopics Covered
Mathematics3030Same as CBT 1, but at a higher difficulty level (up to 12th standard). Includes advanced topics like Probability, Data Interpretation.
General Intelligence & Reasoning2525Same as CBT 1, with more complex puzzles, logical reasoning, and analytical questions.
Basic Science & Engineering4040Engineering Drawing (Projections, Views, Lines), Units, Measurements, Mass, Weight, Density, Work, Power, Energy, Speed & Velocity, Heat & Temperature, Basic Electricity, Levers & Simple Machines, Occupational Safety & Health, Environment Education, IT Literacy.
General Awareness & Current Affairs55Same as CBT 1, focusing on recent events.

Preparation Tips for Part A: Emphasize engineering basics from ITI-level books. Solve RRB-specific mock tests to handle the 90-minute duration.

Part B (Qualifying, Trade-Specific)

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Questions/Marks: 75 MCQs, 75 marks
  • Negative Marking: 1/3 mark deducted
  • Syllabus: Based on the trade (e.g., Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Automobile) as per the Director General of Employment & Training (DGET). Covers 10th/ITI-level topics in the candidate's chosen trade.
    • Mechanical/Trade Fitter: Tools, Units, Mass, Speed, Work/Power/Energy, Levers, Safety, Environment.
    • Electrical: Basic Electricity, Magnetism, Wiring, Safety.
    • Electronics: Semiconductors, Diodes, Transistors, Digital Electronics.
    • Automobile: Engine Components, Transmission, Brakes, Electrical Systems.

Preparation Tips for Part B: Refer to NCVT/SCVT ITI books for the specific trade. Qualifying marks: 35% (minimum 26 marks out of 75).

RRB ALP CBAT Syllabus
  • Qualifying Nature: Yes (minimum 42 marks in Part A of CBT 2 required to appear).
  • Tests: Includes Intelligence Test, Selective Attention Test, Spatial Scanning Test, Information Ordering, Personality Test.
  • Syllabus: Aptitude-based, no fixed topics. Focuses on psychomotor abilities, decision-making, and personality traits suitable for loco pilots.
  • Duration: Varies (approximately 45-60 minutes).

Preparation Tips: Practice aptitude tests from RRB previous papers. No negative marking.

Additional Notes
  • Negative Marking: Applies to all CBT stages (1/3 mark per wrong answer).
  • Language: Bilingual (Hindi & English), except language-specific questions.
  • Syllabus PDF: Download the official RRB ALP Syllabus PDF from the RRB website for detailed topics.
  • Preparation Resources: Use NCERT books (Class 9-12) for basics, Lucent GK for awareness, RS Aggarwal for Maths/Reasoning, and trade-specific ITI manuals.
