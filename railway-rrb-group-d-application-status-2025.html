Railway RRB Group D Application Status 2025

Post Date: September 24, 2025 | Updated on: September 27, 2025 at 02:30 PM IST

The Railway Recruitment Boards (RRB) has released the Application Status for the Recruitment of RRB Group-D Exam 2025 on its official website. The exam will be conducted soon, but following a recent court hearing in the High Court, a stay has been placed on the Group D exam date until the final hearing in CAT is completed. The last date to submit the application form was 01 March 2025. A total of 32438 vacancies are available for various Level 1 posts. Candidates can check their Application Status using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the portal. The direct link is provided below.
