RAILWAY RRB JE CBT-2 RESULT 2025

Post Date: July 21, 2025

The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the CBT-2 Result for the recruitment of Junior Engineer (JE) posts under CEN 03/2024. The exam was conducted on 04 June 2025. Candidates who appeared for the second stage CBT can now check their results and scorecard using their login credentials on the official regional RRB websites. The direct links are provided below.

Important Dates

Application Start Date: 30 July 2024

Last Date to Apply: 29 August 2024

CBT-1 Result: 06 March 2025

CBT-2 Exam Date: 04 June 2025

Answer Key: 07-12 June 2025

CBT-2 Result Declared: 02 July 2025

Application Fee

General, OBC, EWS: ₹ 500/-

SC, ST, PH, All Female: ₹ 250/-

Fee Refund: ₹400 for General & ₹250 for others after appearing in Stage I Exam.

Railway RRB JE 2024: Age Limits as on 01/01/2025

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years
  • Maximum Age: 36 Years
  • Age relaxation is provided as per RRB regulations.

Total Post

7951
Railway RRB Junior Engineer (JE) Recruitment 2024: Eligibility
Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Junior Engineer (JE), Depot Material Superintendent, Chemical & Metallurgical Asst.
  • Candidates must have an Engineering Diploma or Degree in the relevant trade.
  • Specific qualifications are required for JE (IT) and Chemical & Metallurgical Assistant posts.
  • Please read the official notification for detailed post-wise eligibility.
How To Check & Download RRB JE CBT-2 Result 2025
  1. Visit the official website of the RRB region you applied for.
  2. On the homepage, look for the link related to “CEN‑03/2024 Result of 2nd Stage CBT for JE posts”.
  3. Click on the link to download the zone‑wise merit list PDF.
  4. Open the PDF and use the search function (Ctrl+F) to find your Roll Number.
  5. If your roll number is in the list, you have qualified for the next stage.
  6. Download and save the PDF for future reference.
