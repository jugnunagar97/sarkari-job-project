Railway RRB Ministerial & Isolated CEN 07/2024 Admit Card 2025

Post Date: September 8, 2025

The Railway Recruitment Boards (RRB) has released the Exam City Intimation and Admit Card for the Ministerial & Isolated CEN 07/2024 Examination 2025 on its official website. The examination is scheduled for September 10 to 12, 2025, with the application window having closed on February 28, 2025. Candidates can download their Exam City Intimation and Admit Card using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the respective RRB portal. The admit cards were made available starting September 7, 2025. Candidates are advised to verify details on the official RRB website.
