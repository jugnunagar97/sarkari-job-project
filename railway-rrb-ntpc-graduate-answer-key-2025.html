How to Check & Download RRB NTPC Graduate Level Answer Key 2025
  • Visit the Official Website: Go to the official RRB portal: rrb.digialm.com or your respective regional RRB website.
  • Access the Answer Key Link: On the homepage, click on the link titled “RRB NTPC Answer Key 2025”.
  • Login with Details: Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth or Password as required.
  • View Answer Key: After logging in, your exam Answer Key will be displayed.
  • Download and Print: Download the slip and take a printout for future reference.
