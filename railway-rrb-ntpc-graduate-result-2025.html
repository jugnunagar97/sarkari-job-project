Railway RRB NTPC Graduate Result 2025

Post Date: September 20, 2025 1:26 PM

The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has announced the Result for the Non-Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) Graduate Level Posts. A total of 8113 vacancies were announced for this recruitment between September and October 2025. The Exam was conducted from 05 June – 24 June 2025. Candidates who applied can check their Result using their Enrollment number / Registration number / Date of birth by logging into the official portal. (Advt No. : CEN 05/2024)

Steps To Check & Download Railway RRB NTPC Graduate Level Result 2025

  • Step 1:

    Visit the Official RRB Website. Go to the Railway Recruitment Board’s regional website where you applied (e.g., RRB Kolkata, RRB Allahabad, RRB Chennai, etc.).

  • Step 2:

    Open the Results Section. On the homepage, find the tab/section labeled “Results”, “Recruitment”, or “Latest Notifications”.

  • Step 3:

    Look for Graduate Level Result Notification. Search for the notification titled “RRB NTPC Graduate Level Result 2025” or “CEN NTPC Graduate Posts Result”.

  • Step 4:

    Click the Result Link. The result may be published as: A PDF file with the roll numbers/names of shortlisted candidates, or A login-based scorecard for individual marks.

  • Step 5:

    Login (if required). Enter your Registration Number/Application ID. Enter your Date of Birth/Password. Fill in the captcha/security code if shown. Click on Submit/Login.

  • Step 6:

    View the Result. If it’s a PDF, use the search option (Ctrl+F) to find your roll number. If it’s a scorecard, check your marks, cut-off details, and qualifying status.

  • Step 7:

    Download & Save. Save the PDF/scorecard to your device. Take a screenshot as a backup. Print a Hard Copy. Keep a printed copy of your result/scorecard for Document Verification, Skill Tests, or future reference.
