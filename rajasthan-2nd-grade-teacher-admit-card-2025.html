RPSC 2nd Grade Teacher Admit Card 2025 Released

Published: September 4, 2025

RPSC 2nd Grade Teacher Admit Card 2025: The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) is releasing the admit cards for the Senior Teacher recruitment exam today, September 4th. Candidates who are scheduled to appear for the exam on September 7th can download their hall tickets from the official RPSC websites: rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in or recruitment.rajasthan.gov.in.

The download link for the admit card is being activated. Once live, you can enter your roll number and password to view and download your admit card online.

How to Download the RPSC Senior Teacher Admit Card 2025

You can download your admit card by following these simple steps:

  • First, go to the official RPSC website at rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in or recruitment.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • Look for the "News and Events" or "Admit Card" section on the homepage.
  • Find and click on the link for "RPSC 2nd Grade Teacher Admit Card 2025".
  • Enter the required details, such as your application number and date of birth.
  • Click "Submit" and your admit card will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of the admit card for the exam.

Exam Schedule for September 7th

The exam for **Group A (General Knowledge)** will be held on September 7th from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM. The paper for **Social Science** will be conducted on the same day from 3:00 PM to 5:30 PM. Admit cards for these two exams are available for download now.
