Rajasthan HC Civil Judge Pre Result 2025

Post Date: August 19, 2025 2:46 PM

Rajasthan High Court (RHC), has released the Pre Result for the recruitment of High Court Civil Judge Examination 2025 on its official website. The Rajasthan application process started on 01 March 2025, and candidates were able to submit their applications until 30 March 2025. The Pre exam was conducted in 27 July 2025. Candidates can check and download their Result by logging into the portal (link provided below) using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number.

Rajasthan High Court Civil Judge Examination 2025 : Short Details
Important Dates Application Fee
  • Online Apply Start Date : 01 March 2025
  • Online Apply Last Date : 30 March 2025
  • Last Date For Fee Payment : 31 March 2025
  • Exam Date : 27 July 2025
  • Admit Card : 09 July 2025
  • Result Date : 19 August 2025

Rajasthan High Court Civil Judge Notification 2025 : Age Limit As On 01 January 2026

  • Minimum Age : 21 Years.
  • Maximum Age : 40 Years.
  • Age Relaxation as per Rajasthan High Court Civil Judge Recruitment Rules.

How To Check & Download Rajasthan High Court Civil Judge Pre Result 2025
  • Visit the Rajasthan High Court Website: Open the official High Court portal and locate the “Recruitment”, “Result”, or “Press Notes” section.
  • Look for the Civil Judge Prelims Result Notice: Find the notification for Civil Judge (Preliminary) Result 2025.
  • Click the Result Link: Once the notice is visible, click the provided link to open the result download page.
  • Log In with Your Details: Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth, plus any captcha if prompted.
  • Download Your Result: Your result will appear. Download and print 2–3 copies for future use.
