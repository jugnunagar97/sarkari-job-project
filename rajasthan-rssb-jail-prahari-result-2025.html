How To Check & Download Rajasthan RSSB Jail Prahari Result 2025
  1. Visit the official RSSB website: rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in.
  2. Navigate to the "Candidate Corner" or "Result" section on the homepage.
  3. Find and click the link for “Jail Prahari Result 2025”.
  4. The result PDF will open. Use the search function (Ctrl+F) to find your roll number.
  5. Download and save the PDF for future reference.
