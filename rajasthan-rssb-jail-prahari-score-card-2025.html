RAJASTHAN RSSB JAIL PRAHARI SCORE CARD 2025

The score card for the Jail Prahari Examination 2025 has been officially released by the Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB). HOW TO CHECK & DOWNLOAD RAJASTHAN RSSB JAIL PRAHARI SCORE CARD 2025
  1. Visit the Official Website: Navigate to the Rajasthan Staff Selection Board’s official portal: https://rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in
  2. Find the Score Card Section: On the homepage, locate and click on the "Candidate Corner" or a similar link for results/score cards.
  3. Select the Relevant Link: Look for the specific link titled “Jail Prahari Score Card 2025” and click on it.
  4. Enter Login Details: You will be prompted to enter your credentials, such as Registration Number and Date of Birth.
  5. Download Score Card: Your score card will be displayed on the screen. Download and print a copy for your records.
