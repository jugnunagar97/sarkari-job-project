RPSC School Lecturer: Eligibility Criteria
SubjectEligibility Criteria
Hindi, English, History, Political Science, Geography, etc. Post Graduate Degree in the relevant subject with a Degree or Diploma in Education (B.Ed./Shiksha Shastri).
Biology Post Graduate Degree in Zoology/Botany/Micro-Biology/Bio-Technology with Botany and Zoology at Graduation level, plus a Degree/Diploma in Education.
Commerce Post Graduate Degree in Commerce with B.Com OR PG in Commerce with at least two teaching subjects for Higher Secondary classes, plus a Degree/Diploma in Education.
Drawing Post Graduate Degree in Drawing or a four/five-year Diploma in Arts from a recognized institution.
Physical Education Graduate with a Post Graduate degree in Physical Education/M.P.Ed. (2 years).
Coach (Various Sports) Graduate with a Degree or Diploma in Physical Education and a full-time National Institute of Sports (NIS) Certificate.
