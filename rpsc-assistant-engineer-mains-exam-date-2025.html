RPSC ASSISTANT ENGINEER MAINS EXAM DATE 2025

Post Date: June 13, 2025

The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has announced the Mains Exam Date for the Assistant Engineer (AE) Examination 2024. The application process for this recruitment concluded on September 15, 2024. The Mains examination is scheduled to be held from March 15-18, 2026. Candidates who have qualified the preliminary exam are advised that the admit card for the mains will be released on the official portal closer to the exam date.

RPSC ASSISTANT ENGINEER AE EXAMINATION 2024 : SHORT DETAILS
Important Dates Application Fee

Application Start: 14 August 2024

Application Last Date: 15 September 2024

Pre Exam Date: 28 September 2025

Mains Exam Date: 15-18 March 2026

Admit Card: Before Exam

General / Other State: ₹ 600/-

OBC / MBC / SC / ST: ₹ 400/-

Payment Mode: Online

RPSC ASSISTANT ENGINEER AE NOTIFICATION 2024 : AGE LIMITS AS ON 01 JANUARY 2025

  • Minimum Age: 21 Years
  • Maximum Age: 40 Years
  • Age relaxation is provided as per RPSC rules.
