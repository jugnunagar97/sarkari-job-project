RPSC Assistant Engineer Pre Admit Card 2025

Post Date: September 25, 2025 | Updated on: September 27, 2025 at 02:16 PM IST

The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the Pre Admit Card for Assistant Engineer (AE) Examination 2025 on its official website. The preliminary exam is scheduled for 28 September 2025, with the mains exam set for 15–18 March 2026. Applications closed on 15 September 2024. A total of 1014 vacancies are available for Assistant Engineer posts. Candidates can download their admit card using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. Direct links are provided below.

RPSC Assistant Engineer Recruitment 2025: Vacancy Details
Post NameNo. of PostEligibility Criteria
Assistant Engineer (AE)1014Candidates must have a B.E. (Civil) degree or an equivalent qualification recognized by the government. In addition, they should have a working knowledge of Hindi written in the Devanagari script and be familiar with Rajasthani culture.
Documents Required
  • Scanned copies of educational certificates (B.E. in Civil Engineering or equivalent).
  • Recent passport-size photograph (JPG format, as per RPSC guidelines).
  • Scanned signature (JPG format, as per RPSC guidelines).
  • Caste certificate (if applicable, for SC/ST/OBC/MBC).
  • Valid ID proof (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving License).
How to Check & Download RPSC Assistant Engineer Pre Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) website at rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • Go to the “Notifications” or “Recruitment” section.
  • Find the link titled “RPSC Assistant Engineer (AE) Pre Admit Card 2025”.
  • Enter your Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth.
  • Submit the details to view your admit card.
  • Download the PDF admit card.
  • Take a clear printout to carry to the exam centre.
