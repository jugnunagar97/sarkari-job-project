How to Fill RPSC Assistant Professor Online Form 2025
  • Visit official RPSC recruitment portal at https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in/.
  • Click on "Apply Online" or Assistant Professor recruitment notification link.
  • Select "New Registration" for first-time applicants.
  • Provide email address, mobile number, and set secure password.
  • Verify account through OTP received on registered mobile.
  • Log in using credentials to access application form.
  • Fill all mandatory personal information including name, date of birth, father's name.
  • Provide complete residential address with postal code details.
  • Enter educational qualifications including 10th, 12th, Bachelor's, Master's degree details.
  • Mention marks/percentage for each educational qualification.
  • Specify NET/SLET/SET qualification details with roll number and valid date.
  • If Ph.D., provide doctorate certificate details and subject specialization.
  • Select appropriate subject category matching your qualifications.
  • Upload recent passport-sized photograph (4.5cm x 3.5cm, white background, JPG).
  • Upload signature (3cm x 1.5cm, clear black/blue ink, white paper, JPG).
  • Upload Master's degree certificate or mark sheet for verification.
  • Upload NET/SLET/SET certificate proving eligibility qualification.
  • Upload Ph.D. certificate if applicable.
  • Upload caste certificate if applying under SC/ST/OBC category.
  • Upload date of birth proof (10th certificate or birth certificate).
  • Review all information thoroughly before submission.
  • Proceed to payment section and pay applicable fee.
