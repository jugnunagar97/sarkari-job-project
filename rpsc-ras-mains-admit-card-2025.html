RPSC RAS Mains Admit Card 2025

Post Date: June 16, 2025

The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the Mains Admit Card for the Rajasthan Administrative Service (RAS) and Rajasthan Taxation Service (RTS) Examination 2024 on its official website. The admit card was released on June 14, 2025, for the Mains exam scheduled to be conducted on June 17 and 18, 2025 in two shifts daily (9:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 2:30 PM to 5:30 PM) across Jaipur and Ajmer. This recruitment drive aims to fill 1096 positions, with 428 posts under Rajasthan State Service and 668 under Subordinate Service. Candidates can download their admit card using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the SSO portal or the official RPSC website.

How to Check & Download RPSC RAS / RTS Mains Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official RPSC website: https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in/.
  • Go to the “Admit Card” section on the homepage and click on the “Admit Card for RAS/RTS Mains 2025” link.
  • You will be redirected to the SSO portal: https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in/.
  • Log in using your SSO ID and password.
  • Navigate to the “Recruitment Portal” section and click on “Get Admit Card”.
  • Enter your Application ID and Date of Birth, then submit.
  • Your admit card will appear on the screen. Download and print it for exam day.
  • Note: Verify details like name, photo, signature, and exam center. Contact RPSC immediately if there are any discrepancies.
