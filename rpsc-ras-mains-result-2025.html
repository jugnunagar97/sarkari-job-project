How to Check RPSC RAS / RTS Mains Result 2025
  • Visit the Official RPSC Website: Go to rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • Go to the Result Section: Click on the “Results” link available on the homepage.
  • Select the Relevant Exam: Choose “RAS / RTS (Mains) Exam 2024” from the list.
  • Enter Required Details: Input your Roll Number or other required credentials.
  • Submit and Download: After entering the details, click on the “Submit” button. Your Result will appear on the screen. Download and print it for future reference.
