RPSC School Lecturer PGT Result 2025

Post Date: August 22, 2025 6:55 PM

Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC), has released the Result for the recruitment of School Lecturer PGT Teacher Examination 2025 on its official website. The RPSC application process started on 05 November 2024, and candidates were able to submit their applications until 04 December 2024. The exam is scheduled to be conducted on 23 June To 04 July 2025. Candidates can check the Exam Date and download their Admit Card by logging into the portal (link provided below) using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number.

RPSC School Lecturer PGT Teacher Examination 2025 : Short Details

How To Check & Download RPSC School Lecturer PGT Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the RPSC website: Open rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • Go to “Admit Card” section: On the homepage, find the Admit Card or News & Events tab. Click on “Download Admit Card for School Lecturer (School Education)”.
  • Enter credentials: Input your Application Number and Date of Birth, then fill in the Captcha. Press Submit.
  • View & download admit card: Your hall ticket will appear. Download it and take a printout for the exam.
