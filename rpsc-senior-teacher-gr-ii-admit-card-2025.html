RPSC Senior Teacher Gr II Admit Card 2025

Post Date: September 4, 2025

The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the admit card on its official website for the Senior Teacher Grade 2 TGT Teacher Examination 2025. The exam is scheduled for September 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, and 12, 2025, and the last date to submit the application form was January 24, 2025. As of today, September 12, 2025, at 10:10 AM IST, candidates can download their admit card using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the portal. The direct link is provided below. Visit rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in for more details.

RPSC Senior Teacher Examination 2024: Education Qualification

General Subjects:

  • A graduate or equivalent degree from a recognized university with at least two relevant optional subjects and a degree or diploma in education.

Specialized Subjects (Science, Mathematics, etc.):

  • A graduate with the required subject combination and necessary educational qualifications.
  • Proficiency in Hindi (Devanagari script) and knowledge of Rajasthan’s culture.

How to Check & Download RPSC Senior Teacher Gr II Admit Card 2025
  • Visit Official RPSC Website
    Go to rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • Find the Relevant Advertisement
    Look under Recruitment → Advertisements → “SR. TEACHER (SEC. EDU.) COMP. EXAM 2025” or check for a press note announcing the exam schedule.
  • View Exam Calendar/Press Note
    Click on the link for senior teacher recruitment (Adv. No. 07/2025-26).
    Download the exam date schedule (September 7–12).
  • Admit Card Download
    RPSC usually releases admit cards 10–15 days before the scheduled exam.
    Once available, go to “Admit Card” or “Candidate Corner”, log in with your application number + DOB/password, and download the PDF.
