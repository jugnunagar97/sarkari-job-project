RPSC Senior Teacher Examination 2024 : Education Qualification
  • General Subjects: A graduate or equivalent degree from a recognized university with at least two relevant optional subjects and a degree or diploma in education.
  • Specialized Subjects (Science, Mathematics, etc.): A graduate with the required subject combination and necessary educational qualifications.
  • Proficiency in Hindi (Devanagari script) and knowledge of Rajasthan’s culture.

How To Check & Download RPSC Senior Teacher Gr II Admit Card 2025
  • Visit Official RPSC Website: Go to rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in.
  • Find the Relevant Advertisement: Look under Recruitment → Advertisements → “SR. TEACHER (SEC. EDU.) COMP. EXAM 2025” or check for a press note.
  • View Exam Calendar/Press Note: Click on the link for senior teacher recruitment (Adv. No. 07/2025‑26) to download the exam date schedule.
  • Admit Card Download: RPSC usually releases admit cards 10–15 days before the scheduled exam. Once available, go to “Admit Card” or “Candidate Corner”, log in with your application number + DOB/password, and download the PDF.
