STEPS TO CHECK & DOWNLOAD RAILWAY RRB NTPC 10+2 RESULT 2025
  1. Visit the official Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) website of your region (e.g., RRB Mumbai, RRB Patna, RRB Chennai, etc.).
  2. On the homepage, locate the “Results”, “Recruitment”, or “Latest Notifications” section.
  3. Search for the notification titled “RRB NTPC (Undergraduate) 10+2 Result 2025” or “CEN 06/2024 NTPC Result”.
  4. Open the result link, which may be a PDF file with roll numbers of shortlisted candidates or a login page for individual scorecards.
  5. If required, log in using your Registration Number/Application ID and Date of Birth/Password, and enter the captcha code.
  6. Check your result: search for your roll number in the PDF or view your scorecard with marks and qualifying status.
  7. Download the result PDF or scorecard using the “Download” or “Save as PDF” option.
  8. Take a screenshot for quick reference and print a hard copy for future stages like CBT-2, Document Verification, or Skill Tests.
