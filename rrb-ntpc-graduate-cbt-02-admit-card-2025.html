RRB NTPC GRADUATE CBT-02 ADMIT CARD 2025

Post Date: October 9, 2025 | 5:27 pm

The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has announced the Admit Card for the Non-Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) Graduate Level Posts CBT-02. Steps To Check & Download Railway RRB NTPC Graduate Level CBT-2 Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the Official RRB Website: Go to the official website of your respective Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) region.
  • Find the Admit Card Section: Look for the link titled “CEN No. – NTPC Graduate Level CBT-2 Admit Card 2025” or “Download e-Call Letter”.
  • Click on the Relevant Link: Click the link for NTPC Graduate Level CBT-2 Admit Card to proceed to the login page.
  • Enter Your Login Details: Enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth / Password as provided during registration.
  • Submit Details: After filling in the required information, click on the Login / Submit button.
  • View Your Admit Card: Your RRB NTPC CBT-2 Admit Card 2025 will be displayed on the screen, showing your exam city, date, and shift details.
  • Download and Print: Click on the Download button to save the admit card and take a clear printout — it’s mandatory for entry into the exam hall.
  • Check All Details Carefully: Verify your Name, Roll Number, Exam Centre, Reporting Time, and Exam Instructions before the exam day.

    Candidates can download it from the official website of their respective RRB region under the “Admit Card / e-Call Letter” section. You can also use the direct link provided in the Some Useful Important Links section.

Why RRB NTPC Graduate CBT-02 Admit Card 2025 Matters

The RRB NTPC Graduate CBT-02 Admit Card 2025, released on 09 October 2025, is mandatory for all shortlisted candidates appearing for the 13 October 2025 Second Stage Computer Based Test. Preparation Strategies for RRB NTPC CBT-02 Exam

Preparation Strategies for RRB NTPC CBT-02 Exam

With the RRB NTPC CBT-02 exam scheduled for 13 October 2025, candidates should concentrate on General Awareness, Mathematics, and General Intelligence & Reasoning. Since the level of difficulty is higher than CBT-01, focus intensely on Speed and Accuracy. Attempt full-length mock tests designed specifically for the CBT-02 pattern (120 questions in 90 minutes). Review the General Awareness sections, paying special attention to current affairs and Indian Railways history. Practice typing/aptitude tests if applicable to your chosen post (Station Master/Typist) after clearing CBT-02. Find syllabus guides and practice sets linked via sarkaariresult.org.
