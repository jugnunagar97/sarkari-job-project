RSMSSB Stenographer / PA Phase II Exam Admit Card 2025

Post Date: May 28, 2025

The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB) has announced the Phase-II Exam Date for the Stenographer and Personal Assistant (PA) Grade-II recruitment. The Phase-II exam is scheduled for 29 June 2025, following the cancellation of the earlier scheduled dates of 19-20 March 2025. The Phase-I exam results were declared on 23 December 2024. Candidates can check the exam date and download their admit card by logging into the official portal using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number. The recruitment aims to fill 474 posts for Stenographer and Personal Assistant positions.

How to Check & Download RSMSSB Steno & PA 2024 Phase-II Exam Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official website of the Rajasthan Subordinate and Ministerial Services Selection Board (RSMSSB): https://rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in/.
  • Look for the “Exam Date” or “Latest Notifications” section on the homepage.
  • Click on the link for Stenographer & PA 2024 Phase-II Exam Date or Admit Card.
  • Enter your Registration Number, Enrollment Number, or Date of Birth in the required fields.
  • Click on the Submit/Login button.
  • Your Exam Date and Admit Card will be displayed on the screen.
  • Carefully check all the details, including exam date, time, and venue.
  • Click on the Download button to save the admit card.
  • Print a copy for future reference.

RSMSSB Stenographer / PA Phase-II Exam 2025: Important Questions

Question: When is the RSMSSB Stenographer / PA Phase-II Exam 2025 scheduled?

Answer: The Phase-II exam is scheduled for 29 June 2025.

Question: When were the Phase-I results declared?

Answer: The Phase-I results were declared on 23 December 2024.

Question: What is the total number of vacancies for the Stenographer and Personal Assistant posts?

Answer: There are a total of 474 vacancies (194 for Stenographer Non-TSP and 280 for Personal Assistant Grade II, including 257 Non-TSP and 23 TSP).

Question: What are the eligibility criteria for the Stenographer and Personal Assistant posts?

Answer: Candidates must have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) from a recognized board and possess a computer qualification such as O-Level, COPA/DPCS, Diploma/Degree in Computer Science, 10+2 with Computer, Polytechnic in CS, RSCIT, or equivalent.

Question: How can I download the RSMSSB Stenographer / PA Phase-II Admit Card?

Answer: Visit the official RSMSSB website, navigate to the “Exam Date” or “Latest Notifications” section, click on the Stenographer & PA 2024 Phase-II Admit Card link, log in with your Registration Number, Enrollment Number, or Date of Birth, and download the admit card.
