RSSB Exam Calendar 2025 Revised

Post Date: June 5, 2025

The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has released the Revised Exam Calendar for various post examinations for the 2025-26 recruitment cycle on its official website. The exams are scheduled to take place from 19 March 2025 to 13 September 2026. Candidates can download the admit cards for these exams using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the official portal. The calendar includes key dates for various recruitment exams, including both CET and non-CET exams, and tentative merit list release dates. Candidates are advised to visit the official website to download the detailed schedule and plan their preparation accordingly.

How to Check & Download RSSB Exam Calendar 2025
  • Visit the official RSSB website: https://rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in/.
  • On the homepage, look for the “Notifications” or “News” section.
  • Find the link titled “Revised Exam Calendar 2025-26” or similar.
  • Click on the link to open the exam calendar, which will be available as a PDF or image file.
  • Click the Download button or icon on the PDF viewer to save the document to your device.
  • Open the downloaded file to view the exam schedule and plan your preparation accordingly.

RSSB Exam Calendar 2025: Important Questions

Question: When are the RSSB exams scheduled for 2025-26?

Answer: The exams are scheduled from 19 March 2025 to 13 September 2026.

Question: Where can I download the RSSB Exam Calendar 2025?

Answer: You can download the calendar from the official RSSB website: https://rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in/.

Question: What exams are included in the RSSB Exam Calendar 2025?

Answer: The calendar includes exams for various posts such as Patwari (11 May 2025), Jail Prahari (12 April 2025), Block Programme Officer (02 June 2025), Community Health Officer (08 June 2025), Female Health Worker (12 June 2025), Livestock Assistant (13 June 2025), and others.

Question: How can I prepare for RSSB exams?

Answer: Understand the syllabus and exam pattern, create a study schedule, use standard books, practice previous papers, and stay updated with current affairs and Rajasthan-specific knowledge.

Answer: Yes, the exam dates are tentative and may be revised as per administrative requirements. Always check the RSSB website for updates.
