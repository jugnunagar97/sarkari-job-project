Computer Knowledge Requirement and Professional Responsibilities

Technical Proficiency and Administrative Position Requirements Overview

The RSSB Jamadar Grade-II position requires recognized computer qualifications reflecting modern administrative demands in government operations. Acceptable computer certifications include 'O' Level / Higher DOEACC certificates, NIELIT CCC, COPA/DPCS (Computer Operating and Programming Assistant/Diploma in Personal Computer Software), or Computer Science/Application diplomas/degrees ensuring comprehensive computer literacy. RSCIT (Rajasthan State Certificate in Information Technology) qualification from VMOU Kota represents state-approved computer certification equally accepted. Computer knowledge requirement reflects Jamadar positions' involvement in modern government administrative functions including digital documentation, database management, office automation, email communication, and computerized record-keeping systems.
