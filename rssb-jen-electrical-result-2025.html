RSSB JEN ELECTRICAL RESULT 2025

Post Date: September 20, 2025

RSSB JEN ELECTRICAL RESULT 2025: KEY INSIGHTS

Everything You Need to Know About RSSB JEN Electrical Result 2025

The RSSB JEN Electrical Result 2025 has been announced by the Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB) on September 19, 2025, for the RSMSSB Junior Engineer exam held from February 6 to 22, 2025. This recruitment drive fills 1226 Junior Engineer posts, including 970 Non-TSP and 141 TSP positions for Electrical, Mechanical, and Civil disciplines, and 115 Agriculture Engineering posts. Candidates can download the merit list and category-wise cut-off marks from rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in using their Enrollment Number or Date of Birth. The Rajasthan government jobs 2025 opportunity requires a Bachelor’s Degree or Diploma in relevant fields and knowledge of Hindi and Rajasthani culture.
