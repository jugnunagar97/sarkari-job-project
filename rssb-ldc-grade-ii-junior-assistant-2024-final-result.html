RSSB LDC Grade II / Junior Assistant 2024 Final Result

Post Date: August 27, 2025 3:50 PM

Rajasthan Subordinate & Ministerial Services Selection Board (RSMSSB), The Final Result for the LDC Gr-II & Junior Assistant recruitment has been released on the official website. The Phase-II Exam is scheduled to be conducted from January 21 to January 24, 2025. The Phase-I Exam Results were declared on November 25, 2024. Candidates can check and download their Result/Merit List by logging into the portal (link provided below) using their Registration Number / Date of Birth / Enrollment Number.

RSMSSB LDC and Junior Assistant Recruitment 2024 : Education Qualification
  • Candidates with a Senior Secondary qualification from a recognized board or its equivalent, along with a DOEACC or NIELIT computer certification (COPA/DPCS), are eligible for this recruitment.
  • Additionally, candidates should have proficiency in Hindi written in Devanagari script and an understanding of Rajasthani culture.
  • It is advisable for all candidates to carefully read the official notification before applying.

How To Check RSMSSB LDC and Junior Assistant Phase-II Result/Merit List 2025
  1. Visit the Official Website: Go to the official website of the Rajasthan Subordinate and Ministerial Services Selection Board (RSMSSB).
  2. Find the Result Section: On the homepage, look for the “Results” or “Merit List” section. Click on the link for LDC & Junior Assistant Phase-II Result/Merit List 2025.
  3. Enter Your Credentials: Enter your Registration Number, Enrollment Number, or Date of Birth as required.
  4. View & Download the Result: Your Result/Merit List will be displayed on the screen. Carefully check your name, roll number, and qualifying status and download the result.
