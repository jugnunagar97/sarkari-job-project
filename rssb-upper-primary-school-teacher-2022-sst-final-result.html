RSSB Upper Primary School Teacher 2022 (SST) Final Result

Post Date: August 27, 2025 3:48 PM

Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB), has released Final Result on official website for the recruitment of Community Health Officer CHO Post. This recruitment is for 3531 positions. The RSSB Exam was Conducted in 03 March 2024. The last date to submit the application form was 07 December 2022. and can be checked through Enrollment number / Registration number / Date of birth by login into the portal. Which has given below.

RSMSSB CHO Recruitment 2022 : Vacancy Details
Post Name No. Of Post Eligibility Criteria
CHO (Community Health Officer) Non TSP Area 3071 Candidates must have a B.Sc. degree in Community Health, Nursing, GNM, or a BAMS degree. Additionally, they should be registered with the Rajasthan Nursing Council.
CHO (Community Health Officer) TSP Area 460 Candidates should have a Bachelor’s degree in Science such as B.Sc. in Community Health, Nursing, GNM, or a BAMS degree. They must also be registered with the Rajasthan Nursing Council.

How To Check & Download RSSB CHO 2022 Final Result
  1. Visit the Official RSSB Website: Go to https://rssb.rajasthan.gov.in.
  2. Access the Results Section: Click on the “Results” tab or directly go to https://rssb.rajasthan.gov.in/results.
  3. Locate the CHO 2022 Final Result: Look for the link titled “Community Health Officer (Contract) 2022: Final Recommendation and Cut Off Marks”.
  4. Download the Result PDF: Click on the link to open and download the PDF file containing the list of selected candidates.
  5. Search for Your Roll Number: Open the downloaded PDF and use the search function (Ctrl + F) to find your roll number.
