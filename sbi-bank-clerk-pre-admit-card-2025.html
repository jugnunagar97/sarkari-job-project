SBI Bank Clerk Pre Admit Card 2025

Post Date: September 14, 2025 1:21 PM

The State Bank of India (SBI) has released the Pre Admit Card for the SBI Clerk Examination 2025 on its official website. The application process started on 06 August 2025 and closed on 26 August 2025. The exam is scheduled for 20, 21, 27 September 2025. Candidates can download their Pre Admit Card by logging into the portal using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number. The link is provided below.

How To Download SBI Bank Clerk Pre Admit Card 2025

  • Step 1:

    Visit the Official SBI Portal. Go to the site: ibpsonline.ibps.in.

  • Step 2:

    Access the Pre Admit Card Section. On the homepage, click on the "SBI Clerk Pre Admit Card 2025" link under the "Recruitment" or "Latest Notices" section.

  • Step 3:

    Log In with Credentials. Enter your Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number, and any displayed captcha.

  • Step 4:

    Download the Admit Card. Click Submit, then download the Pre Admit Card (PDF) and print multiple copies for the exam.
