SBI Bank Clerk Prelims Exam Date 2025

Post Date: September 6, 2025

The State Bank of India (SBI) has released the preliminary exam date for the SBI Clerk Recruitment 2025 on its official website. The application process ran from August 6, 2025, to August 26, 2025, and the prelims exam is scheduled for September 20, 21, and 27, 2025. Candidates can download their exam details by logging into the portal using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number. The recruitment aims to fill 5,180 Junior Associate (Sales & Support) positions. Candidates should verify details on the official SBI website bank.sbi.
