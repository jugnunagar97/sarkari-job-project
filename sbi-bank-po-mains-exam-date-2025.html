SBI BANK PO MAINS ADMIT CARD 2025

Post Date: September 4, 2025

The State Bank of India (SBI) has released the admit cards for the Probationary Officer (PO) Mains Examination 2025. The Mains exam is scheduled to be held this Saturday, on 13 September 2025. The application process for this recruitment concluded on 14 July 2025. Candidates who have qualified the preliminary exam can now download their Mains admit card from the official SBI careers portal using their Registration Number and Date of Birth. All necessary links and details are provided below.

SBI BANK PO EXAMINATION 2025 : SHORT DETAILS

Important Dates

  • Online Apply Start Date: 24 June 2025
  • Online Apply Last Date: 14 July 2025
  • Last Date For Fee Payment: 14 July 2025
  • Pre Exam Date: 02, 04 & 05 August 2025
  • Pre Admit Card: 25 July 2025
  • Pre Result: 01 September 2025
  • Mains Exam Date: 13 September 2025
  • Mains Admit Card: 04 September 2025
  • Candidates are advised to confirm from the SBI official website.

Application Fee

  • General / OBC / EWS: ₹ 750/-
  • SC / ST / PH: ₹ 0/-
  • Payment Mode: Online via Debit Card, Credit Card, or Net Banking.
SBI BANK PO NOTIFICATION 2025 : AGE LIMITS AS ON 01 APRIL 2025
Minimum Age 21 Years
Maximum Age 30 Years
SBI provides age relaxation as per government regulations.
SBI PROBATIONARY OFFICER PO RECRUITMENT 2025 : VACANCY DETAILS
Post Name Total Posts Eligibility Criteria
SBI Probationary Officer (PO) 541
  • Bachelor's degree in any stream from a recognized university.
  • Final year students can also apply provisionally.
  • Professional qualifications (CA, Engineering, etc.) are also eligible.
HOW TO DOWNLOAD SBI PO MAINS ADMIT CARD 2025
  1. Visit the official SBI Careers website at sbi.co.in/web/careers.
  2. Go to the "Current Openings" section.
  3. Find the link for "Recruitment of Probationary Officers" and click on it.
  4. Look for the link to "Download Mains Examination Call Letter".
  5. Enter your Registration Number/Roll Number and Password/Date of Birth.
  6. Your admit card will be displayed. Download, save, and print a clear copy.
