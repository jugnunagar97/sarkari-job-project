SCI JUNIOR COURT ASSISTANT DESCRIPTIVE TEST ADMIT CARD 2025

Post Date: July 30, 2025

The Supreme Court of India (SCI) has released the admit card for the Descriptive Test for the Junior Court Assistant (JCA) Examination 2025. This recruitment is for 241 positions. The Descriptive Test is scheduled for 01 August 2025. Candidates who have qualified the previous stages can now download their admit cards by logging into the official portal.

Important Dates

Application Start: 05 February 2025

Last Date to Apply: 08 March 2025

Exam Date: 13 April 2025

Typing Test Exam Date: 04 June 2025

Typing Test Result: 14 July 2025

Descriptive Test: 01 August 2025

Application Fee

General, EWS, OBC: ₹ 1000/-

SC / ST , PH: ₹ 250/-

Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, etc.

SCI Junior Court Assistant 2025: Age Limits as on 08/03/2025

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years
  • Maximum Age: 30 Years
  • Age relaxation is provided as per SCI regulations.

Total Post

241
SCI Junior Court Assistant Recruitment 2025: Eligibility
Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Junior Court Assistant
  • Bachelor's Degree in Any Stream from a recognized university.
  • Knowledge of Computer Operation with a typing speed of 35 WPM.
How To Check & Download SCI JCA Descriptive Test Admit Card 2025
  1. Visit the official Supreme Court recruitment portal at sci.gov.in.
  2. Navigate to the "Recruitment" section.
  3. Find the link for “Admit Card for Descriptive Test - Junior Court Assistant Examination 2025”.
  4. Log in using your credentials (e.g., Application Number, Date of Birth).
  5. Your admit card will be displayed on the screen.
  6. Download and print a clear copy to bring to the examination center.
