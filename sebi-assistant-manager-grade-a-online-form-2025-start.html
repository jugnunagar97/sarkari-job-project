SEBI Assistant Manager Grade A Recruitment 2025 – Apply Online

Post Date: October 30, 2025 | 10:45 AM IST

The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has officially released the notification for Assistant Manager Grade A (AM) Recruitment 2025 on its official website. This prestigious recruitment offers 110 positions across diverse specializations including General, Legal, Information Technology, Research, Official Language, and Engineering (Electrical & Civil) categories. The online application process started on 30 October 2025 and will continue until 28 November 2025. Candidates aged up to 30 years as on 30 September 2025 possessing graduate or postgraduate qualifications (category-specific) are eligible to apply. The selection process includes Phase-I Examination, Phase-II Examination, and Interview. The Phase-I exam is scheduled for 10 January 2026. Application fee is ₹1,180 for General/OBC/EWS and ₹118 for SC/ST/PH candidates with multiple online payment options available.

  • Log in to the application portal using your credentials to access the AM Grade A form.
  • Fill all mandatory personal details including name, date of birth, father's name, and address carefully.

  • Review all entered information thoroughly checking against original documents and certificates.
  • Proceed to payment section and pay applicable examination fee through available online methods.
  • After successful payment, save and print the application confirmation containing registration number.
  • Note the deadline: 28 November 2025 – Apply immediately to avoid last-minute technical issues or delays.

The SEBI Assistant Manager Grade A recruitment 2025 follows comprehensive three-stage selection process evaluating candidates' general knowledge, analytical capabilities, technical expertise, communication skills, and suitability for financial regulatory roles. The Phase-I Examination scheduled for 10 January 2026 comprises 100 objective-type multiple-choice questions covering General Awareness testing knowledge of financial markets, securities regulation, Indian economy, and current affairs; English Language evaluating grammar, vocabulary, comprehension, and communication proficiency; Quantitative Aptitude assessing mathematical and numerical problem-solving abilities; and Reasoning Ability testing logical thinking, pattern recognition, and analytical skills. The examination duration is 2 hours with negative marking for incorrect answers, requiring strategic question selection and time management throughout testing period. Candidates scoring above category-wise cutoff marks qualify for Phase-II Examination comprising descriptive questions testing subject-specific knowledge, technical understanding, and detailed analytical abilities.

Securities Exchange Board of India Overview and AM Recruitment Timeline

Understanding SEBI's Mission and Complete Assistant Manager Recruitment Process

The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), established in 1988 and headquartered in Mumbai, serves as India's apex regulatory authority for securities market under the Ministry of Finance. SEBI's constitutional mandate encompasses protecting investor interests, promoting securities market development, and regulating securities market activity ensuring market integrity, transparency, and fair dealing. SEBI oversees stock exchanges, mutual funds, brokerages, credit rating agencies, and participates market infrastructure maintaining highest regulatory standards comparable to international financial regulators. The organization employs thousands of professionals including economists, lawyers, engineers, technology specialists, and subject matter experts across operational, compliance, enforcement, and policy divisions ensuring effective market regulation and investor protection nationwide across India's securities market infrastructure.
