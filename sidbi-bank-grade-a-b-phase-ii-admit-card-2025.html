SIDBI Bank Grade A, B Phase-II Admit Card 2025

Post Date: September 27, 2025

The Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) has issued the Phase-II Admit Card for AAO / AE Examination 2025 on its official website. The Phase-II exam is scheduled for 04 October 2025, following the Phase-I result on 19 September 2025, with applications closing on 08 September 2025. A total of 76 vacancies are available for Assistant Manager (Grade A & B) positions. Candidates can download their Phase-II admit card using Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. Direct links are provided below.

Grade-A Eligibility Criteria (General Stream)
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree in Commerce, Economics, Mathematics, Statistics, Business Administration, or Engineering, or hold professional qualifications like CS, CMA, CFA, CA, MBA, or PGDM with at least 60% marks (50% for SC/ST/PwBD). Those without CA, MBA, or PGDM must also have 2 years of experience in MSME or Corporate Credit.

How to Check & Download SIDBI Bank Grade A, B Phase-II Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official SIDBI (Small Industries Development Bank of India) website at www.sidbi.in/index.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Career/Recruitment” section.
  • Look for the link that says “Download Admit Card for Grade A & B Phase-II Exam 2025”.
  • Click on the link – a login page will open.
  • Enter your Registration Number / Roll Number and Password / Date of Birth.
  • Submit the details to view your Admit Card.
  • Download the admit card and take a printout for the exam day.
