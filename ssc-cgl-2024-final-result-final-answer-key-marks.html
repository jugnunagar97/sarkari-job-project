SSC CGL 2024 Final Result/ Final Answer Key/ Marks

Post Date: March 18, 2025

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the Final Answer Key and Marks on its official website for the Combined Graduate Level Examination (CGL) 2024. This recruitment is for 17,727 positions. The application process ran from June 24, 2024, to July 27, 2024, and the Tier-II exam was conducted from January 18-20, 2025. As of today, September 11, 2025, the final results and marks are available for candidates to check using their enrollment number, registration number, or date of birth.

How to Check & Download SSC CGL 2024 Final Result/ Final Answer Key/ Marks

1. Visit the Official SSC Website: Navigate to ssc.gov.in.

Access the Results Section: Click on the ‘Results’ tab on the homepage.

Select ‘CGL’ Examination: Choose the ‘CGL’ tab to view relevant notifications.

Find the Final Result Link: Click on ‘Combined Graduate Level Examination, 2024 – Declaration of Final Result’.

Download the Result PDF: A PDF with roll numbers of successful candidates will open; use Ctrl + F to find your roll number.

2. Accessing and Downloading the Final Answer Key:

  • Click on the ‘Answer Key’ section on the homepage.
  • Find ‘Combined Graduate Level Examination, 2024 – Uploading of Final Answer Keys’.
  • Log in with Roll Number and Password to download the answer key and response sheet.

3. Viewing and Downloading Individual Marks:

  • Click on the ‘Marks’ tab.
  • Select ‘Combined Graduate Level Examination, 2024 – Candidates’ Marks’.
  • Log in with Registration Number and Password to view and download your marks.
