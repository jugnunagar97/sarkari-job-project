How To Check & Download SSC CGL Re-Exam Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the Official SSC Website ➤ Open the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) official website or your respective regional SSC website (like SSC NR, SR, CR, WR, etc.).
  • Go to the Admit Card Section ➤ On the homepage, click on the “Admit Card” tab.
  • Select Your Region ➤ Choose the region from where you have applied for the SSC CGL examination (for example, Northern, Central, Southern, Western region, etc.).
  • Find the Re-Exam Admit Card Link ➤ Look for the link titled “CGL Re-Exam Admit Card 2025” or “Download Admit Card for CGL Re-Examination 2025.”
  • Enter Your Login Details ➤ Enter your Registration ID / Roll Number and Date of Birth (DOB) to log in. ➤ You can also search by Name and Father’s Name if available.
  • View and Verify Your Details ➤ Check your Name, Roll Number, Exam Date, Reporting Time, and Exam City on the admit card carefully.
  • Download the Admit Card ➤ Click on the “Download” or “Print Admit Card” button.
  • Take a Printout ➤ Keep at least two copies of the printed admit card for entry at the exam center.
