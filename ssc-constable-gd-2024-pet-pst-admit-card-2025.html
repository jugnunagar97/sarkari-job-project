SSC CONSTABLE GD 2024 PET/ PST ADMIT CARD 2025

Post Date: August 12, 2025

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the admit card for the Physical Efficiency Test (PET) / Physical Standard Test (PST) for the GD Constable Recruitment 2024. The written exam was conducted from February 4 to February 25, 2025. This recruitment is for 53,690 positions. Candidates who have qualified the written exam can now download their PET/PST admit cards from the official link provided below.

Important Dates

Application Start: 05 September 2024

Last Date to Apply: 15 October 2024

Exam Date: 04 – 25 February 2025

Result Declared: 17 June 2025

Physical Test Date: 20 Aug – 11 Sep 2025

Application Fee

General / OBC / EWS: ₹100/-

SC / ST / All Female: ₹0/-

Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, etc.

SSC GD Constable 2024: Age Limits as on 01/05/2024

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years
  • Maximum Age: 23 Years
  • Age relaxation is provided as per SSC regulations.

Total Post

53,690
SSC GD Constable 2024: Eligibility & Physical Details
Eligibility Physical Standards (Male) Physical Standards (Female)
Class 10th (High School) passed from any recognized board in India.
  • Height: 170 cm (162.5 cm for ST)
  • Chest: 80-85 cm (76-81 cm for ST)
  • Running: 5 Kms in 24 Minutes
  • Height: 157 cm (150 cm for ST)
  • Running: 1.6 Kms in 8.5 Minutes
How To Download SSC GD Constable PET/PST Admit Card 2025
  1. Visit the official SSC website or your regional SSC website.
  2. On the homepage, click on the "Admit Card" tab.
  3. Find and click the link for “Download PET/PST Admit Card for Constable (GD) Examination, 2024”.
  4. Enter your Registration Number/Roll Number and Date of Birth.
  5. Your admit card will be displayed. Verify all details carefully.
  6. Download and print a clear copy to bring to the test center.
