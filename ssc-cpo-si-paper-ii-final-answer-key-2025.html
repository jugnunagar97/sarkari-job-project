SSC CPO SI Paper II Final Answer Key 2025

Post Date: August 14, 2025 6:47 PM

Staff Selection Commission (SSC), has released Final Answer Key on official website for the recruitment of Sub-Inspector in Delhi Police and Central Armed Police Forces Paper-II Examination Post. This recruitment is for 4187 positions. The SSC Paper-II Exam was Conducted in 08 March 2025. The last date to submit the application form was 28 March 2024. and can be checked through Enrollment number / Registration number / Date of birth by login into the portal. Which has given below.

How To Check & Download SSC CPO SI Paper II Result 2025
  • Visit the official SSC website: ssc.gov.in.
  • Click on the “Result” tab on the homepage.
  • Find and click on the link titled “Uploading of Tentative Result along with Candidates’ Response Sheet(s) of SI in Delhi Police and CAPF Examination (Paper-II) – 2024.”
  • You will be redirected to the login page. Enter your Registration Number and Password to log in.
  • Once logged in, your response sheet and the tentative Result will be displayed.
  • Download and save the documents for your reference.
