SSC CPO SI Paper II Marks 2025

Post Date: September 12, 2025 7:06 PM

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has announced the Paper II Marks for the Sub-Inspector (SI) in Delhi Police and Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF) Examination 2024, released on 12 September 2025. This recruitment drive aims to fill 4187 vacancies. The Paper II exam was conducted on 08 March 2025, with applications accepted until 28 March 2024. Candidates can access their marks by logging into the official SSC portal using their registration details. Detailed information about the SSC CPO SI Paper II Marks 2025 is provided below.

How To Check & Download SSC CPO SI Paper II Marks 2025

  • How to access the SSC website?

    Visit the official SSC website: ssc.gov.in.

  • Where to find the marks section?

    Click on the “Marks” tab on the homepage.

  • How to locate the Paper II marks link?

    Find and click on the link titled “Uploading of Tentative Marks along with Candidates’ Response Sheet(s) of SI in Delhi Police and CAPF Examination (Paper-II) – 2024.”

  • How to log in?

    Enter your Registration Number and Password to log in.

  • What to do after logging in?

    Your response sheet and tentative marks will be displayed.

  • How to save the marks?

    Download and save the documents for your reference.
SSC Sub Inspector in Delhi Police Recruitment 2024 : Mode Of Selection

  • What is the first step of selection?

    Online Written Examination

  • What is the second step?

    Physical Standard Test (PST) / Physical Endurance Test (PET)

  • What is the third step?

    Paper II (Written Examination)

  • What is the final step?

    Detailed Medical Examination (DME)
