SSC Delhi Police Constable Selection Process, Examination Pattern, and Physical Standards

Comprehensive Five-Stage Selection Framework and Rigorous Fitness Assessment Procedures

The SSC Delhi Police Constable recruitment 2025 follows a comprehensive five-stage selection process beginning with Computer Based Test (CBT) objective examination at designated centers nationwide. The CBT comprises 100-150 multiple-choice questions covering General Intelligence & Reasoning testing logical abilities, General Awareness evaluating current affairs and general knowledge, Numerical Aptitude assessing mathematical skills, and General Knowledge of India and Delhi. The examination duration is 90 minutes with negative marking of 0.25 marks per incorrect answer, requiring strategic question selection and time management. Candidates scoring above category-wise cutoff marks qualify for next stage proceeding to Physical Efficiency Test (PET) comprising gender-specific and age-differentiated standards ensuring fair evaluation accounting for physiological variations.

Physical Efficiency and Measurement Standards:

  • Male Constables (Up to 30 years): 1600M race in 6 minutes, long jump 14 feet (3 attempts), high jump 3.9 feet; Height minimum 170cm, chest 81-85cm expanded
  • Female Constables (Up to 30 years): 1600M race in 8 minutes, long jump 10 feet (3 attempts), high jump 3.0 feet; Height minimum 157cm
  • Age Relaxation Standards: Running times increase by 1 minute for 30-40 age group and 2 minutes for above 40 years; Long jump and high jump distances proportionally reduced
  • Category-wise Height Standards: SC/ST candidates enjoy 5cm height relaxation compared to General/OBC/EWS categories recognizing demographic diversity

Selection Methodology & Document Verification: Candidates clearing PET proceed to Measurement Test verifying height, weight, and chest expansion against prescribed standards. Successful MT candidates undergo comprehensive document verification examining 10+2 certificates with valid LMV driving licenses, category certificates for reserved candidates, age proof, and identity documents. Finally, stringent medical examination by Delhi Police authorized medical boards assesses physical fitness ensuring constables meet operational health standards. The entire selection process from application to final appointment spans 6-8 months ensuring only thoroughly vetted, physically fit, medically cleared candidates join Delhi Police force equipped to handle demanding law enforcement duties protecting national capital territory's safety and security with professionalism, integrity, and unwavering commitment to constitutional duties and public service objectives.
