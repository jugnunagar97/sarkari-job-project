Delhi Police Head Constable Ministerial: Administrative Backbone of National Capital Police

Transforming 509 Positions for Office Management in India's Premier Metropolitan Police Force

The SSC Delhi Police Head Constable Ministerial Recruitment 2025 fills 509 Group C positions providing administrative support to Delhi Police headquarters, districts, and specialized units. This SSC-conducted recruitment offers stable government career managing records, documentation, and office operations for India's largest metropolitan police force serving 2 crore+ population.

Post Distribution:

  • Male candidates: 341 posts (including 46 Ex-Servicemen quota)
  • Female candidates: 168 posts (33% horizontal reservation)
  • Category-wise: UR (250), EWS (51), OBC (115), SC (73), ST (20)

Key Responsibilities:

  • Office management: File handling, record maintenance, documentation at police stations/headquarters
  • Typing work: Preparing FIRs, charge sheets, case files, official correspondence
  • Computer operations: Data entry on CCTNS, e-office applications, digital record management
  • Administrative support: Assisting senior officers in administrative tasks, meeting coordination

Salary and Benefits:

  • Pay Level 4: ₹25,500-₹81,100 (7th CPC Pay Matrix)
  • Gross in-hand: ₹40,000-₹50,000/month (with DA, HRA, allowances)
  • Dearness Allowance: 50% of basic pay (revised twice yearly)
  • House Rent Allowance: 27% in Delhi (NCT classification)
  • Transport Allowance: ₹7,200/month for Delhi posting

Head Constable Ministerial serves as critical administrative link managing documentation for 80,000+ Delhi Police personnel handling law enforcement, traffic regulation, crime investigation, and public safety across 11 police districts and specialized units.

SSC Selection Process: CBT, Physical Tests, Typing and Computer Formatting Tests

Multi-Stage Assessment with Computer-Based Test in December 2025-January 2026

The SSC Delhi Police HCM selection 2025 follows comprehensive five-stage process combining written exam, physical fitness tests, typing proficiency assessment, computer skills evaluation, and document verification ensuring selection of competent ministerial staff for Delhi Police administrative operations.

Stage 1: Computer-Based Test (CBT):

  • Total marks: 100 (100 MCQs, 90 minutes duration)
  • General Intelligence & Reasoning: 25 marks (analogies, coding, series, puzzles)
  • General Awareness & Current Affairs: 25 marks (Indian polity, GK, sports, awards)
  • Numerical Aptitude: 25 marks (arithmetic, percentages, averages, data interpretation)
  • English Language/Hindi: 25 marks (grammar, vocabulary, comprehension)
  • Negative marking: 0.25 marks deducted for each wrong answer

Stage 2: Physical Endurance & Measurement Test:

  • Male candidates: Height 170 cm minimum, Chest 80-85 cm (expanded)
  • Female candidates: Height 157 cm minimum, Weight proportionate to height/age
  • Physical endurance: Walking/running test (qualifying, not scored)
  • Exemption: PwD candidates as per government guidelines

Stage 3: Trade Test (Typing Test):

  • English typing: 30 words per minute on computer keyboard
  • Hindi typing: 25 words per minute on computer keyboard
  • Duration: 10 minutes typing test (accuracy 85%+ required)
  • Qualifying nature: Minimum 30 wpm English OR 25 wpm Hindi mandatory

Stage 4: Computer Test (Formatting):

  • MS Word: Document formatting, table creation, mail merge operations
  • MS Excel: Basic spreadsheet operations, formulas, data entry
  • Duration: 15 minutes practical test on computer
  • Qualifying: Minimum 50% marks required to clear computer proficiency test

Final Merit Preparation:

  • CBT marks: 100% weightage for final merit list
  • Physical, typing, computer tests: Qualifying only (no marks in merit)
  • Tie-breaker: Higher CBT marks > Higher age > Alphabetical order
  • Document verification: After merit list publication for selected candidates

Expected CBT cutoffs: General (60-65 marks), OBC (55-60), SC (50-55), ST (45-50) out of 100 marks based on previous year trends and competition level for 509 vacancies.

Career Growth and Perks in Delhi Police Ministerial Cadre

₹25,500 Starting Pay to ₹81,100 Maximum with Central Government Benefits

The Delhi Police Head Constable Ministerial offers attractive Central Government 7th Pay Commission salary with regular increments, promotion opportunities, and comprehensive benefits including housing, medical facilities, pension ensuring stable career in premier police organization.

Salary Breakup:

  • Basic pay: ₹25,500-₹81,100 (Pay Level 4, annual increment ₹810)
  • Dearness Allowance: ₹12,750 (50% of basic pay, revised Jan/July)
  • House Rent Allowance: ₹6,885 (27% in Delhi for non-accommodation holders)
  • Transport Allowance: ₹7,200/month (Delhi NCT rate)
  • Total gross: ₹52,335/month initially (₹6.28 lakh annual CTC)

Additional Allowances:

  • City Compensatory Allowance: 4% of basic pay (Delhi posting)
  • Uniform allowance: ₹5,000 annually for maintenance
  • Children education allowance: ₹2,250/child/month (maximum 2 children)
  • Leave travel concession: ₹60,000 biennial for family travel

Career Progression Path:

  • Head Constable (Ministerial) (0-5 years): Entry-level administrative work
  • Assistant Sub-Inspector (5-10 years): Promotion via departmental exam (20% quota)
  • Sub-Inspector (10-15 years): Supervisory roles in police stations/units
  • Inspector (15-20 years): Gazetted officer rank with administrative authority
  • Top positions: Deputy SP, Additional DCP (25-30 years career progression)

Benefits and Perks:

  • Police accommodation: Government quarters in Delhi (Type-II/III allotment)
  • Medical facilities: CGHS coverage + free treatment at police hospitals (₹5 lakh limit)
  • Leave benefits: 30 days earned leave, 8 days casual leave annually
  • Pension: 50% of last drawn pay after 20 years service
  • Gratuity: ₹20 lakh maximum (based on years of service)
  • GPF: General Provident Fund with interest (current rate 7.1% annually)

Delhi Police ministerial staff also eligible for Delhi government employee benefits including subsidized canteen facilities, festival advances, and special allowances for working in sensitive/high-security assignments ensuring comprehensive financial security.
