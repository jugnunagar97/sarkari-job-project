Understanding SSC Delhi Police Head Constable Selection Process and Requirements

Complete Guide to Head Constable Selection Stages and Qualification Standards

The SSC Delhi Police Head Constable selection process follows rigorous multi-stage assessment framework evaluating candidates through Computer Based Test covering general awareness, reasoning abilities, numerical aptitude, and English language proficiency. The objective-type examination tests candidates' analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, and knowledge areas relevant to supervisory police duties ensuring selection of intellectually capable individuals.

Following written examination, shortlisted candidates undergo Physical Endurance and Measurement Test assessing physical fitness standards essential for active policing duties. The Trade Test evaluates specific skills relevant to Head Constable responsibilities including typing proficiency, computer knowledge, or specialized technical abilities depending on position requirements. Document verification confirms educational credentials and category certificates.

Selection Process Breakdown:

  • Computer Based Test: Objective questions on general knowledge, reasoning, quantitative aptitude, and English with negative marking for incorrect responses
  • Physical Endurance Test: Running, jumping, and physical fitness assessments meeting prescribed standards ensuring operational readiness for field duties
  • Trade Test: Skill assessment including typing speed test, computer proficiency evaluation, or specialized technical competency verification as applicable
  • Medical Examination: Comprehensive health assessment ensuring candidates meet Delhi Police medical standards for active duty including vision, hearing, and fitness tests

Final merit lists are prepared based on combined performance across all stages with category-wise selections ensuring reservation compliance. The stringent selection process ensures only qualified candidates possessing necessary educational background, physical fitness, technical skills, and aptitude join Delhi Police Head Constable cadre.
