Mode of Selection
  • Written Exam (लिखित परीक्षा)
  • Physical Efficiency Test (PET) (शारीरिक दक्षता परीक्षण)
  • Physical Standards Test (PST) (शारीरिक मानक परीक्षण)
  • Interview (साक्षात्कार)
  • Document Verification (दस्तावेज़ सत्यापन)
  • Medical Examination (चिकित्सा परीक्षण)
SSC GD Constable Exam Pattern Overview
  • Computer Based Examination (CBE): 80 questions, 160 marks, 60 minutes. Objective type (MCQs) with four sections.
  • Physical Efficiency Test (PET): Qualifying in nature (race as per category).
  • Physical Standards Test (PST): Height and chest measurement as per category.
  • Medical Examination: Final qualifying stage.

The exam is conducted online (computer-based). Total marks for selection are based on CBE, with PET/PST and medical exam qualifying. Negative marking: 0.50 marks deducted for each wrong answer.

Preparation Tips for CBE: Focus on 10th-grade NCERT books for Mathematics and General Knowledge. Practice previous year papers and mock tests to improve speed (60 minutes for 80 questions). Qualifying marks: UR: 35%, OBC/EWS: 30%, SC/ST: 25%.

Physical Efficiency Test (PET) & Physical Standards Test (PST)

PET: Qualifying race as per category (see Running Detail table above).

PST: Height and chest measurements as per category (see Physical Details table above).

Preparation Tips: Regular physical training focusing on endurance and stamina is essential. Follow SSC guidelines for PET/PST.

Additional Notes
  • Negative Marking: 0.50 marks deducted for each wrong answer in CBE.
  • Language: Bilingual (Hindi & English), with an option to choose either for the language section.
  • Syllabus PDF: Download the official SSC GD Syllabus PDF from the SSC website for detailed topics.
  • Preparation Resources: Use NCERT books (Class 9-10) for basics, Lucent GK for awareness, RS Aggarwal for Mathematics/Reasoning, and standard Hindi/English grammar books.
