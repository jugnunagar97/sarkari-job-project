SSC MTS & HAVALDAR 2024 FINAL ANSWER KEY

Post Date: March 26, 2025

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the Final Answer Key for the Multi-Tasking (Non-Technical) Staff (MTS) and Havaldar Examination 2024. The examinations for these 11,518 positions were conducted from September 30 to November 14, 2024. Candidates who appeared for the computer-based exam can now download the final answer key along with their question paper from the official SSC website.

SSC MTS EXAMINATION 2024 : SHORT DETAILS
Important Dates Application Fee

Exam Dates: 30 Sep - 14 Nov 2024

Answer Key: 29 November 2024

Result Date: 12 March 2025

Final Answer Key: 26 March 2025

Gen / OBC / EWS: ₹ 100/-

SC / ST / PWD / Female: ₹ 0/-

Payment Mode: Online

AGE LIMITS FOR SSC MTS 2024

  • 18 to 25 Years: For most posts under SSC MTS.
  • 18 to 27 Years: For specific posts as notified by SSC.
  • Age relaxation for reserved categories (SC/ST: 5 years, OBC: 3 years, PwD: 10+ years) is applicable as per government norms.

Total Post

11,518
Steps to Check & Download SSC MTS & Havaldar 2024 Final Answer Key
  1. Visit the official SSC website at ssc.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, click on the "Answer Key" tab.
  3. Find and click the link for “Multi-Tasking (Non-Technical) Staff, and Havaldar (CBIC & CBN) Examination, 2024: Uploading of Final Answer Keys”.
  4. A PDF notice will open containing the link to download the answer key. Click on it.
  5. Enter your Roll Number and Password (as per your admission certificate) to log in.
  6. Download the final answer key and your response sheet for your records.
