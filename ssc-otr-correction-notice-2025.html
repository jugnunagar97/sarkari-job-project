Post Date: August 13, 2025 4:52 PM

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC), has recently released official website – https://ssc.gov.in/. Along with this, SSC has also released the details for the SSC One Time Registration (OTR) Online Form 2025. According to the official notice, all candidates who want to apply for upcoming SSC vacancies must first complete the OTR process on the new website.
