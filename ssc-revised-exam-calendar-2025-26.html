How to Check & Download the SSC Exam Calendar 2025-2026
  • Visit the official SSC website: https://ssc.gov.in.
  • Navigate to the “Latest News” or “Notices” section on the homepage.
  • Look for a notification titled “SSC Annual Calendar 2025” or similar.
  • Click on the notification link to open the PDF document with the detailed schedule for various SSC exams.
  • Click on the download icon or right-click and select “Save As” to download the calendar to your device.
  • Open the downloaded PDF to check the tentative dates for exams like SSC CGL, CHSL, MTS, GD Constable, and others.
