Staff Selection Commission (SSC) The SSC Stenographer C, D Final Marks has been released. The skill test exam was held in April 16-17, 2025, at various centers across the country for Stenographer C and D posts. Now, to assist candidates, the commission has uploaded the CBT exam result. Only those who appeared for the online exam can check the SSC Stenographer C, D Final Marks 2025. and can be checked through Enrollment number / Registration number / Date of birth by login into the portal. How To Check & Download SSC Stenographer C, D Final Result 2025
  1. Visit the SSC Official Website: Go to the Staff Selection Commission’s official portal and navigate to the Results section.
  2. Select the Stenographer Grade C & D Result: In the Results section, click on the link labeled “Stenographer Grade C & D Examination 2024 (Final Result / Merit List)”.
  3. Download the Result PDF: A PDF file will open displaying the list of roll numbers of candidates shortlisted. Download and save this PDF.
  4. Search Your Roll Number: Open the PDF and use the Ctrl + F search function to find your roll number or name.
  5. Save & Print the Document: If your details appear, download and print a copy for your records.
