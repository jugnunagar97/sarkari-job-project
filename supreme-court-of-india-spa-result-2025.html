SUPREME COURT OF INDIA SPA RESULT 2025

Post Date: August 9, 2025

The Supreme Court of India (SCI) has released the result for the recruitment of SCI Personal Assistant, Senior Personal Assistant & Court Master (Shorthand) posts. This recruitment is for 107 positions. The skill tests were conducted from 01-07 May 2025. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now check their results by logging into the official portal with their User ID and Password.

How To Check & Download Supreme Court of India SPA Result 2025
  1. Visit the official SCI recruitment portal: sci.gov.in/recruitments/.
  2. Find the link for the specific result you want to check (e.g., "Result for the post of Senior Personal Assistant").
  3. Click on the link, which may redirect to a login page or open a PDF.
  4. If it's a login page, enter your User ID and Password.
  5. If it's a PDF, use the search function (Ctrl+F) to find your name or roll number.
  6. Download and save the result for future reference.
