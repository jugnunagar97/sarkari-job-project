UCO Bank SO Final Result 2025

How to Check & Download UCO Bank SO Final Result 2025
  • Open your browser and go to the official UCO Bank website at ucobank.com/job-opportunities.
  • On the homepage, look for a menu/tab labeled “Careers / Recruitment / Results”.
  • Within that section, find the link titled “UCO Bank SO Final Result 2025” (or “Specialist Officer Result 2025”).
  • Click on that link — it will likely open a PDF document showing shortlisted candidates.
  • Download the PDF and save it to your device.
  • Open the PDF and use Ctrl+F (or find) to search your Roll Number / Registration Number.
  • If you see your number in the list, you are shortlisted / qualified.
  • Take a printout of the result PDF for future reference.
