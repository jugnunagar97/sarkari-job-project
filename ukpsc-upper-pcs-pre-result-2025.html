UKPSC Upper PCS Pre Result 2025

Post Date: October 8, 2025 | Updated on: October 9, 2025 at 10:00 AM IST

The Uttarakhand Public Service Commission (UKPSC) has released Result on its official website for the recruitment of Civil Upper Subordinate Service Examination 2025. This recruitment is for 123 positions. UKPSC exam was conducted on 29 June 2025 & the candidates can apply until 27 May 2025. Candidates can check their Result using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth by logging into the portal. The direct link is provided below.

How to Check UKPSC Upper PCS Pre Result 2025
  • Visit the Official UKPSC Website: Go to psc.uk.gov.in.
  • Go to the Result Section: Click on the “Results” link available on the homepage.
  • Select the Relevant Exam: Choose “Upper PCS Prelims Result 2025” from the list.
  • Enter Required Details: Input your Enrollment Number / Registration Number and Date of Birth.
  • Submit and Download: After entering the details, click on the “Submit” button. Your Result will appear on the screen. Download and print it for future reference.
