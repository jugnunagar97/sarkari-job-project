UKSSSC Assistant Accountant and Other Post Recruitment 2025 : Vacancy Details
Post Name No. Of Post Eligibility Criteria
Assistant Accountant 57

Candidates should have a B.Com, BBA, or Master’s in Accountancy, along with Hindi typing speed of 4000 key depressions per hour.
Record Keeper-cum-Store Keeper 01

Candidates must have passed 10+2 in the Commerce stream from a recognized board and have a Hindi typing speed of 4000 key depressions per hour.
Office Assistant III (Accounts) 04

Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Commerce with knowledge of Hindi (Devanagari script). They should also have a Hindi typing speed of 6000 key depressions per hour and English typing speed of 7000 key depressions per hour.
Cashier Data Entry Operator 01

Candidates must have passed 10+2 in Commerce with Accountancy from any recognized board. They should also have a typing speed of 4000 key depressions per hour and knowledge of MS Office. For more details, refer to the official notification.
