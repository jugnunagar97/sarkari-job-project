UP Anganwadi Recruitment 2024

Post Date: February 8, 2025 1:15 PM

The Uttar Pradesh District Program Officer (Uttar Pradesh Government) has released a notification for the recruitment of Anganwadi Karyakarta on its official website. This UP Anganwadi recruitment is for 8,796 positions, and the application forms will be accepted until District Wise. Interested and eligible candidates who wish to apply for this recruitment can find all the related information and the application link in the Important Links section below.

Last Date for Apply Online : District Wise

Payment Mode (Online): You can make the payment using the following methods:

  • Debit Card

  • IMPS
  • Cash Card / Mobile Wallet

Eligibility Criteria
