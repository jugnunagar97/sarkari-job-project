How To Check & Verify UP Dakhil Kharij Bhulekh Online 2025

Visit the UP Bhulekh Portal:

  • Go to the official website: https://upbhulekh.gov.in

Access the Mutation Service:

  • On the homepage, click on the “Apply for Mutation (Dakhil Kharij)” option.

Fill in the Application Form:

  • Provide necessary details such as property information, reason for mutation, and personal details.
  • Upload required documents like the sale deed, death certificate, identity proof, etc.

Submit the Application:

  • After filling in all details and uploading documents, submit the application.
  • You will receive an acknowledgment number for future reference.
