UP Police SI Online Form 2025 (4543 Posts) – Last Date Today

Post Date: September 11, 2025

The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has released the official notification for the recruitment of Sub Inspector (SI) and Platoon Commander posts, offering a total of 4543 vacancies. The online application process started on August 12, 2025, and the last date to apply is September 11, 2025. Eligible candidates, aged between 21 and 28 years as of July 1, 2025, with a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university, can apply through the official website uppbpb.gov.in. This recruitment drive includes positions like Sub Inspector (Civil Police), Platoon Commander (PAC/Armed Police), and Special Security Force SI, with category-wise vacancies and specific eligibility criteria detailed below.

UP Police SI Online Form 2025: Eligibility Criteria
  • Educational Qualification: Candidates must hold a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university or institution in India or an equivalent qualification recognized by the Government.
  • Important Notes:
  • It is mandatory to upload the graduation mark sheet during the application process.
  • If the graduation degree certificate is unavailable, a provisional degree must be uploaded.
  • If a provisional degree is also unavailable, candidates should upload mark sheets instead.
  • During document verification and Physical Standard Test, candidates must present the original graduation degree certificate and mark sheets.
  • Preference Qualifications (Not Mandatory):
  • Passing the “O” Level Computer Examination conducted by NIELIT (formerly DOEACC) or an equivalent certification.
  • A minimum of two years of service in the Territorial Army.
  • A “B” certificate from the National Cadet Corps (NCC).
  • Recommendation: Candidates must read the official notification thoroughly before applying.

How to Fill UP Police SI Online Form 2025
  • Visit the official UPPRPB website: https://uppbpb.gov.in/.
  • Complete the One Time Registration (OTR) process if not already done, using a valid email ID and mobile number.
  • Log in using your OTR credentials or Aadhaar/Digilocker.
  • Navigate to the recruitment section and select “Direct Recruitment for Sub Inspector Civil Police and Equivalent Posts-2025”.
  • Fill out the application form with accurate personal, educational, and communication details.
  • Upload required documents (passport-sized photo, signature, educational certificates, caste certificate if applicable, Aadhaar card, etc.) in the specified format.
  • Pay the application fee online (₹500 for General/EWS/OBC, ₹400 for SC/ST).
  • Review the form, submit it, and download/print the confirmation page for future reference.
  • Note: Ensure all details are correct to avoid rejection. Apply before the deadline of September 11, 2025, to avoid technical issues.

UP Police SI Recruitment 2025: Mode of Selection
  • Written Exam (Objective type, covering General Hindi, General Knowledge, Numerical Ability, and Reasoning with 160 questions).
  • Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
  • Physical Standard Test (PST)
  • Document Verification
  • Medical Test
Important Exam Day Instructions
  • Carry a printed copy of the admit card and a valid photo ID (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Driving License, or Passport).

  • No entry will be allowed after the gate closing time.
