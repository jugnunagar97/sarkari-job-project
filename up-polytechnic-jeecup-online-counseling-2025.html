How To Fill UP Polytechnic JEECUP Counselling Form 2025

Visit the JEECUP portal: jeecup.admissions.nic.in

  • Click on “Fresh candidate Registration” under counselling section
  • Enter your details: name, DOB, category, valid ID info, email/mobile, password/security pin
  • Review & submit; note down the Application Number received via SMS/email
