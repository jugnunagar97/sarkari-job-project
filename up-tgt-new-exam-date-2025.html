UP TGT / PGT EXAM DATE 2025

Post Date: May 1, 2025

The Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Selection Board (UPSESSB) has officially announced the new exam dates for the TGT and PGT recruitment (Advt No. 01/2022 & 02/2022). The application period for this recruitment concluded on July 16, 2022. The PGT exam is scheduled for June 20-21, 2025, and the TGT exam is scheduled for July 21-22, 2025. Candidates who applied can find all relevant details below.

UTTAR PRADESH TGT/ PGT EXAMINATION 2022 : SHORT DETAILS
Important Dates Application Fee

Application Start: 09 June 2022

Application Last Date: 16 July 2022

Exam Date (PGT): 20-21 June 2025

Exam Date (TGT): 21-22 July 2025

How To Check & Download Uttar Pradesh TGT/ PGT Exam Date 2025
  • Visit the official UPSESSB website: upsessb.pariksha.nic.in.
  • Navigate to the "Notifications" or "Exam Dates" section on the homepage.
  • Find and click the link for the TGT/PGT Exam Date Notification 2025.
  • Download the official notice (PDF) to view the schedule.
  • The admit card link will be activated on the portal closer to the exam dates.
