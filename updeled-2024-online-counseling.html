Uttar Pradesh UP DELEd 2 Year Course Admissions 2024

Post Date: December 26, 2024 2:14 PM

Examination Regulation Authority UP, PNP Prayagraj , The Counseling Date for the UP DELED (BTC) Online Exam 2024 has been released. The result will be declared on 26th December 2024 and will be available as a PDF file on the official UP DELED website. It is important to note that UP DELED Phase-I Counseling will be conducted from 26th December 2024 to 12th January 2025.

How To Fill UPDELED 2024 Choice Filling & Counselling 2024

Steps to Fill UP DELED 2024 Choice Filling & Counselling:

  • Visit Website: Go to https://updeled.gov.in/.
  • Login: Enter your Registration Number and Password.
  • Verify Details: Check your personal information and rank.
  • Fill Choices: Select and arrange preferred colleges.
  • Lock Choices: Submit and lock your preferences.
  • Download Slip: Save the confirmation for reference.
  • Check Allotment: View seat allotment status online.
  • Admission: Visit the allotted college for document verification and fee submission.
