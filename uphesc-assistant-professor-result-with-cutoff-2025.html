UPHESC Assistant Professor Result with Cutoff 2025

Post Date: September 5, 2025

The Uttar Pradesh Higher Education Service Commission (UPHESC) has officially announced the result for the Assistant Professor recruitment examination. This recruitment drive aims to fill 981 vacancies. The examination was conducted on April 16th-17th, 2025, for applications submitted by August 29, 2022. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now check their results and cutoff marks on the official portal using their Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number.

UPHESC ASSISTANT PROFESSOR EXAMINATION 2022 : SHORT DETAILS

Important Dates

  • Apply Start Date: 09 July 2022
  • Apply Last Date: 29 August 2022
  • Fee Payment Last Date: 30 August 2022
  • Exam Date: 16-17 April 2025
  • Admit Card Date: 07 April 2025
  • Result Declared: 04 September 2025

Application Fee

  • General / OBC / EWS: ₹ 2000/-
  • SC / ST: ₹ 1000/-
  • Payment Mode: Online via Debit Card, Credit Card, or Internet Banking.
UPHESC ASSISTANT PROFESSOR NOTIFICATION 2024 : AGE LIMIT AS ON 07 AUGUST 2022
Minimum AgeNot Available
Maximum Age62 Years
Age relaxation is provided as per UPHESC regulations.
UPHESC ASSISTANT PROFESSOR RECRUITMENT 2024 : VACANCY DETAILS (Total: 981 Posts)
Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Assistant Professor
  • Must possess a Master’s degree in the relevant subject with at least 55% marks.
  • Must have cleared NET / SET / SLET examinations.
  • Candidates should refer to the official notification for detailed requirements.
HOW TO CHECK & DOWNLOAD UPHESC ASSISTANT PROFESSOR RESULT 2025
  1. Visit the official UPHESC website at https://uphesc.org/en.
  2. On the homepage, navigate to the results or notifications section.
  3. Find and click the link for the "Assistant Professor Result Advt. No. 51".
  4. You may be required to log in using your registration number, date of birth, or other credentials.
  5. Once logged in, your result and cutoff marks will be displayed on the screen.
  6. Download the result PDF and print a copy for your records.
