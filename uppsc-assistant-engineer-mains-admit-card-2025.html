How to Check & Download UPPSC Assistant Engineer Mains Admit Card 2025
  • Visit the official website at uppsc.up.nic.in.
  • Look for the “Admit Card / Download Admit Card” link under the Candidate Corner / Important Links section.
  • Click on the link titled “Assistant Engineer (AE) Mains Examination 2025 – Admit Card.”
  • Provide the required information such as Registration Number, Date of Birth, Gender, and Captcha Code (if displayed).
  • Click on the Submit / Download Admit Card button.
  • The admit card will appear on the screen. Verify all details carefully, including Candidate’s Name, Roll Number, Exam Date & Time, Exam Centre Address, and Important Instructions.
  • Save the admit card as a PDF file on your device.
  • Take a clear printout of the admit card to carry on the day of the exam.

  1. When will the UPPSC AE Mains Admit Card 2025 be released?

    It can be downloaded only from the official UPPSC website under the “Admit Card” or “Download Admit Card” section at uppsc.up.nic.in.
