UPPSC Assistant Professor Recruitment 2025: Apply for 1253 Posts

Published: September 4, 2025

A major recruitment drive for Assistant Professor positions has been announced for Government Degree Colleges in Uttar Pradesh. The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released a notification for 1253 Assistant Professor vacancies. This is a significant opportunity for candidates aspiring to become government assistant professors, as this recruitment is happening after nearly five years.

The online application process begins on September 4, 2025. The last date to apply and submit the application fee is October 6, 2025. Candidates can make corrections to their applications until October 13, 2025.

UPPSC Assistant Professor Recruitment 2025: Key Details

Recruiting Body Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC)
Post Name Assistant Professor
Total Vacancies 1253
Application Start Date September 4, 2025
Last Date to Apply October 13, 2025
Official Website uppsc.up.nic.in

Eligibility Criteria and Age Limit

This recruitment is announced under advertisement number A-7/E-1/2025. While detailed information about educational qualifications will be available in the full notification, the age limit has been specified. Candidates must be between **21 and 40 years of age** as of July 1, 2025.

This means candidates born before July 2, 1985, and after July 1, 2004, are not eligible to apply. Age relaxation will be provided to reserved categories as per state government rules.

Selection Process

The selection of candidates for the Assistant Professor posts will be based on a **written examination followed by an interview**. All applications must be submitted through the One-Time Registration (OTR) portal of UPPSC.

Related: UP Lecturer Recruitment 2025

In addition to the Assistant Professor posts, the UPPSC has also announced 1516 vacancies for Lecturers in Government Inter Colleges. The last date to apply for the Lecturer positions is September 12, 2025. Candidates for this post must have a postgraduate degree in the relevant subject along with a B.Ed degree.

For more detailed information on either recruitment, candidates are advised to visit the official UPPSC website.
