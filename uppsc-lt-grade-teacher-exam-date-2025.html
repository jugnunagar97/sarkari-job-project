Hindi-Candidates must have a graduation degree with Hindi as a subject and must have studied Sanskrit at the intermediate level or passed the Uttar Madhyama exam from UP Sanskrit Shiksha Parishad. They must also have a B.Ed. degree from an institute recognized by NCTE in India.
English-Candidates must have a graduation degree in English Literature from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Maths-Candidates must have a graduation degree with Mathematics as a subject from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Science-Candidates must have a graduation degree with Physics and Chemistry as subjects from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute recognized by NCTE in India.
Social Science-Candidates must have a graduation degree with at least two subjects from History, Geography, Political Science, and Economics from a recognized university, along with a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Computer-Candidates must have a B.Tech/B.E. in Computer Science, OR a graduate degree in Computer Science or Computer Applications, OR a graduation degree with ‘A’ Level certification from NIELIT, OR B.Tech in Computer Science and MCA. A B.Ed. degree recognized by NCTE is a preferential qualification.
Urdu-Candidates must have a graduation degree with Urdu as a subject from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Biology-Candidates must have a graduation degree with Zoology and Botany as subjects from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Sanskrit-Candidates must have a graduation degree with Sanskrit as a subject from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Arts-Candidates must have a graduation degree with Art as a subject along with a B.Ed. degree recognized by NCTE, or a Bachelor’s degree in Fine Arts (BFA) with a B.Ed. degree from a recognized university, where B.Ed. is considered a preferential qualification.
Music-Candidates must have a graduation degree with Music as a subject from a recognized university, or have a Sangeet Visharad from Bhatkhande Sangeet Mahavidyalaya or Sangeet Prabhakar from Prayag Sangeet Samiti, Allahabad along with a graduation degree from a recognized university. Additionally, they must have a B.Ed. degree recognized by NCTE in India.
Commerce-Candidates must have a graduation degree in Commerce from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Physical Education-Candidates must have a graduation degree from a recognized university and a B.P.Ed. or B.P.E. degree in a course approved by NCTE in India.
Home Science-Candidates must have a graduation degree with Home Science as a subject from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Agriculture / Horticulture (Male)-Candidates must have a graduation degree in Agriculture or Horticulture from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
