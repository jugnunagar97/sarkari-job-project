UPPSC PCS MAINS ADMIT CARD 2025

Post Date: June 20, 2025

The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released the Mains Admit Card for the Combined Upper Subordinate Service (PCS) Examination 2024. This recruitment is for 220 positions. The Mains exam is scheduled from 29 June to 02 July 2025. Candidates who have qualified the preliminary exam can now download their admit cards by logging into the official portal.

Important Dates

Mains Application Start: 07 March 2025

Mains Application Last Date: 24 March 2025

Mains Exam Date: 29, 30 June & 01, 02 July 2025

Admit Card: 19 June 2025

Application Fee (Mains)

General / OBC / EWS: ₹ 225/-

SC / ST / EXS: ₹ 105/-

PH Candidates: ₹ 25/-

Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, etc.

UPPSC PCS 2024: Age Limits as on 01/07/2024

  • Minimum Age: 21 Years
  • Maximum Age: 40 Years
  • Age relaxation is provided as per UPPSC regulations.

Total Post

220
UPPSC PCS Recruitment 2024: Eligibility for Mains
Exam Name Eligibility Criteria
Combined State / Upper Subordinate Services Examination 2024
  • Only candidates who have qualified the UPPSC PCS Preliminary Exam are eligible to apply for the Mains Exam.
How To Download UPPSC PCS Mains Admit Card 2025
  1. Visit the official UPPSC website: uppsc.up.nic.in.
  2. On the homepage, navigate to the "Admit Card" section.
  3. Find and click the link for “Download Admit Card for Combined State / Upper Subordinate Services (Main) Exam-2024”.
  4. Enter your Registration Number, Date of Birth, and other required details.
  5. Your admit card will be displayed on the screen.
  6. Download and print a clear copy to bring to the examination hall.
