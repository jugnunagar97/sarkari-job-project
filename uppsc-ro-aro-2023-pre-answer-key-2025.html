UPPSC RO/ARO 2023 Pre Answer Key 2025

Post Date: July 30, 2025 4:33 PM

The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released the Preliminary Answer Key for the recruitment of Samiksha Adhikari (Review Officer) and Sahayak Samiksha Adhikari (Assistant Review Officer) posts, covering 411 vacancies. The re-examination was conducted on 27 July 2025, with the application process concluding on 24 November 2023. Candidates can access the answer key by logging into the portal using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. The answer key, available in PDF format, allows candidates to estimate their scores and raise objections by 5 August 2025. Links and instructions are provided below for easy access.

How To Check & Download UPPSC RO/ARO Pre Answer Key 2025
  • Visit the official UPPSC website.
  • On the homepage, click on the “View Answer Key” or the “RO/ARO Answer Key 2025” link—usually found under the latest press release or notifications section.
  • Enter your registration/application number and date of birth (DD-MM-YYYY) as prompted.
  • Select your post (RO or ARO) and Paper set (if applicable) to access the Provisional Answer Key PDF.
  • Download and save the PDF for reference and offline use.
  • To raise objections, submit offline objections in the prescribed format by 5 August 2025, including the question number, question booklet bar code (7103809), and supporting evidence.
