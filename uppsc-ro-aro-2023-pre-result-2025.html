UPPSC RO/ARO 2023 Pre Result 2025

Post Date: September 16, 2025 | Last Updated: September 16, 2025 10:50 PM

The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released the UPPSC RO/ARO 2023 Pre Result 2025 on September 16, 2025 for the preliminary examination held on July 27, 2025. A total of 7,509 candidates have been shortlisted for the Mains examination, including 6,093 for 338 Review Officer (RO) posts, 1,386 for 79 Assistant Review Officer (ARO) posts, and 30 for 2 ARO (Accounts) posts. Candidates can check their results on the official website uppsc.up.nic.in by entering their registration number and date of birth. The recruitment aims to fill 411 vacancies for Samiksha Adhikari and Sahayak Samiksha Adhikari posts.

All About UPPSC RO/ARO 2023 Pre Result 2025

The UPPSC RO/ARO 2023 Pre Result 2025 was declared on September 16, 2025, for the preliminary exam conducted on July 27, 2025. Out of 4,54,589 candidates who appeared, 7,509 qualified for the Mains exam, including 6,093 for 338 RO posts, 1,386 for 79 ARO posts, and 30 for 2 ARO (Accounts) posts. Candidates can check their results at uppsc.up.nic.in using their registration number and date of birth. The recruitment targets 411 vacancies for Samiksha Adhikari and Sahayak Samiksha Adhikari posts. Eligibility includes a bachelor’s degree, with additional “O” Level and Hindi typing requirements for ARO posts.

UPPSC RO/ARO 2023 PRE RESULT 2025: FAQs

Question: When was the UPPSC RO/ARO 2023 Preliminary Result declared?

Answer: The result was declared on September 16, 2025, by the Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC).

Question: Where can I check my result?

Answer: On the official UPPSC website at uppsc.up.nic.in by entering your roll number/registration number and date of birth.

Question: What details are mentioned in the result?

Answer: Candidate’s name, roll number, marks obtained, cut-off marks, and qualifying status.

Answer: Shortlisted candidates are eligible to appear in the Mains Examination.
