How To Fill UPPSC RO / ARO Mains Online Form 2025
  • Only candidates who qualified UPPSC RO/ARO Prelims exam 2023 (result declared 16 September 2025) eligible to apply.
  • Visit official Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission website at uppsc.up.nic.in from desktop or mobile browser.
  • Click on "Online Application" or "Mains Application Form" link displayed on homepage prominently.
  • Login using your OTR (One Time Registration) number and password registered during prelims application.
  • Select "RO/ARO Mains Examination 2023" from available examination list displayed on candidate dashboard.
  • Fill all mandatory details including personal information, educational qualifications, category, contact details carefully.
  • Upload recent passport size photograph, signature scanned images as per specifications mentioned in notification.
  • For Assistant Review Officer posts, upload O Level certificate, Hindi typing certificate as additional documents.
  • Select preferred examination center city from dropdown list (availability subject to seat allocation).
  • Review all entered information thoroughly checking for any errors or omissions before final submission.
  • Pay examination fee online using debit card, credit card, net banking, or UPI as per your category.
  • Submit application form after successful payment and take printout for future reference purposes.
  • Note down application number and keep payment receipt safe for admit card download, future correspondence.
  • Complete application process before deadline 07 November 2025 (11:59 PM) to avoid last-minute technical issues.
UPPSC RO / ARO Recruitment 2023: Mode Of Selection
  • Preliminary Examination: Objective type test covering General Studies (140 questions) and General Hindi (60 questions) conducted 27 July 2025
  • Mains Examination: Descriptive type examination testing in-depth knowledge of subjects including General Studies, Essay, Hindi for shortlisted candidates
  • Skill Test: Hindi typing test (25 WPM) and computer proficiency test for Assistant Review Officer candidates qualifying mains
  • Document Verification: Checking original certificates including educational, caste, domicile, typing certificates for finally selected candidates
  • Final Merit: Based on mains examination performance (skill test qualifying nature) preparing category-wise merit lists for appointment

Review Officer: UP's Administrative Review Experts

Filling 411 RO/ARO Positions Across UP Secretariat, UPPSC, Board of Revenue

The UPPSC RO/ARO Recruitment 2023 fills 411 critical Review Officer and Assistant Review Officer positions across Uttar Pradesh Secretariat, UPPSC headquarters, and Board of Revenue. Also known as Samiksha Adhikari (RO) and Sahayak Samiksha Adhikari (ARO), these officers conduct administrative reviews, examine government files, verify procedural compliance, draft official correspondence, prepare detailed reports, and support senior officers in decision-making processes serving UP's 24 crore+ population across 75 districts. Review Officers work in state secretariat managing ministerial files, policy implementation, inter-departmental coordination, and administrative oversight under Chief Secretary supervision contributing efficient governance, transparent administration, and organizational excellence ensuring effective delivery of government schemes, welfare programs, and development initiatives.

Department-Wise Responsibilities:

  • UP Secretariat (362 posts): File review, policy analysis, ministerial coordination, cabinet note preparation, inter-departmental liaison
  • UPPSC (23 posts): Examination review, recruitment file processing, administrative support, candidate grievance resolution, procedural compliance
  • Board of Revenue (26 posts): Land revenue review, appeal processing, administrative coordination, policy implementation, file examination
  • Review duties: Examining government files, verifying procedures, identifying irregularities, suggesting improvements, ensuring compliance
  • Documentation: Drafting notes, preparing reports, maintaining files, correspondence, official communications in Hindi/English

Salary and Benefits:

  • Review Officer (Pay Level 7): ₹44,900-₹1,42,400/month initially with ₹55,000-₹70,000 gross in-hand
  • Assistant Review Officer (Pay Level 6): ₹35,400-₹1,12,400/month initially with ₹45,000-₹55,000 gross in-hand
  • Annual CTC: ₹6-10 lakh depending on post level including all components

UP Government officers enjoy comprehensive state benefits including DA (50%), HRA, transport allowance, medical facilities, GPF, pension, LTC ensuring prestigious administrative careers.

Prelims Result Analysis: Cutoff Marks and Qualified Candidates

Prelims Result Declared 16 September for Exam Held 27 July 2025

The UPPSC RO/ARO Prelims Result 2025 announced qualified candidates for Mains examination filling 411 positions based on merit performance. Released on 16 September 2025 for examination conducted 27 July (re-exam after February cancellation), the result includes category-wise cutoff marks with General category requiring 125-135 marks, OBC requiring 120-130 marks, SC requiring 104-110 marks out of 200. Answer key released 30 July 2025 enabled candidates calculating scores before result. Approximately 6,000-8,000 candidates qualified for Mains (approximately 15x vacancies) ensuring adequate competition for final selection through descriptive examination testing in-depth subject knowledge, analytical abilities, writing skills in Hindi and English languages.

Prelims Exam Pattern Structure:

  • General Studies (140 questions): Indian history, geography, polity, economy, science, current affairs, UP GK, environment
  • General Hindi (60 questions): Hindi grammar, vocabulary, comprehension, translation, official language proficiency
  • Total questions: 200 MCQs with 200 marks (1 mark each question)
  • Duration: 2 hours (120 minutes) for completing prelims examination
  • Negative marking: 1/3rd (0.33) marks deducted for each incorrect answer
  • Exam mode: Offline OMR-based examination conducted at centers across UP
  • Qualifying nature: Prelims only screening test; marks not counted for final merit preparation

Category-Wise Cutoff Marks (Expected):

  • General/UR category: 125-135/200 (62-67% marks) due to high competition for administrative posts
  • OBC-NCL category: 120-130/200 (60-65% marks) with 3-5 marks relaxation from general
  • SC category: 104-110/200 (52-55% marks) with 20-25 marks relaxation
  • ST category: 92-100/200 (46-50% marks) with highest relaxation for tribal candidates
  • Female candidates: 115-120/200 (57-60% marks) with additional relaxation promoting women representation
  • PwD category: 80-90/200 (40-45% marks) for persons with disabilities
  • Post-wise variation: Similar cutoffs for RO and ARO as single prelims exam for both

Qualified candidates must fill Mains application form by 07 November 2025 deadline preparing for descriptive examination testing comprehensive knowledge, analytical abilities, administrative aptitude.

Career Progression: Review Officer to Principal Secretary Level

₹55,000 Starting Salary with Promotions to Senior Secretariat Positions

The UP Review Officer career offers excellent progression advancing from entry-level file review to senior secretariat management positions. Officers gain valuable administrative experience, understand government functioning, develop policy analysis skills, and qualify for promotions to Under Secretary, Deputy Secretary, Joint Secretary levels managing departments, coordinating policies, advising ministers eventually reaching Principal Secretary positions heading government departments managing state administration, budget allocations, policy formulation contributing Uttar Pradesh's governance excellence serving diverse sectors including education, health, infrastructure, revenue, social welfare, industrial development ensuring efficient delivery of public services supporting state's development vision under Chief Minister's leadership.

Career Advancement Path:

  • Review Officer/ARO (0-5 years): File examination, note drafting, procedural compliance, report preparation under senior officers
  • Senior Review Officer (5-10 years): Complex file handling, policy analysis, junior staff supervision, administrative coordination
  • Under Secretary (10-15 years): Section management, policy formulation support, ministerial coordination, decision recommendations
  • Deputy Secretary (15-20 years): Department management, budget oversight, policy implementation, inter-departmental coordination
  • Joint Secretary (20-25 years): Senior management, strategic planning, ministerial advisory, state-level policy formulation
  • Principal Secretary (25+ years): Heading major departments, cabinet coordination, chief ministerial advisory, governance leadership

Job Responsibilities and Work Profile:

  • File examination: Reviewing government files, checking procedural compliance, verifying facts, identifying issues, suggesting actions
  • Note drafting: Preparing detailed notes analyzing issues, providing background, suggesting solutions, recommending decisions
  • Correspondence: Drafting official letters, circulars, notifications, orders in Hindi/English maintaining official format
  • Policy support: Analyzing policies, preparing comparative studies, researching best practices, drafting proposals
  • Coordination: Liaising with departments, conducting meetings, preparing agendas, recording minutes, follow-up actions
  • Administrative duties: Managing subordinate staff, monitoring work progress, ensuring deadlines, quality control

Comprehensive Benefits Package:

  • Basic salary: ₹35,400-₹1,42,400/month depending on post with regular annual increments
  • Dearness Allowance: Currently 50% of basic (revised biannually) compensating inflation
  • House Rent Allowance: 8-24% of basic depending on city (Lucknow being state capital offers higher HRA)
  • Transport Allowance: ₹3,600-₹7,200/month for official travel, commuting expenses
  • Medical facilities: Government hospital treatment + reimbursement for self, family members
  • GPF accumulation: General Provident Fund with interest building retirement corpus
  • Pension: Monthly pension after retirement ensuring post-retirement financial security
  • Gratuity: Lump sum payment on retirement (15 days salary per year)
  • LTC: Leave Travel Concession covering family travel expenses biannually
  • Government quarters: Official accommodation at state capital or HRA for private residence
  • Children education: Education allowance, concessional admissions in government schools

Work Environment and Culture:

  • Working hours: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM typically (Monday-Friday) with flexible hours during urgent work
  • Posting locations: State secretariat Lucknow, UPPSC Prayagraj, Board of Revenue Lucknow headquarters
  • Transfer policy: Generally stationed at headquarters with limited transfers ensuring family stability
  • Professional environment: Formal secretariat culture requiring discipline, confidentiality, protocol adherence
  • Job security: Permanent state government employment with pension ensuring lifetime career stability

UP Review Officer positions provide prestigious careers combining administrative authority, policy influence, excellent compensation, comprehensive benefits making secretariat service attractive for graduates.

UPPSC: Managing UP's Civil Services Since 1937

Conducting 20+ Annual Examinations for 5,000+ State Government Positions

The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) established 1937 serves as constitutional body conducting competitive examinations for civil services and other state government positions. UPPSC ensures merit-based transparent selection for Provincial Civil Services (PCS), Allied Services, and various Group A, B positions serving UP's 24 crore+ population across 75 districts maintaining high examination standards, fair evaluation processes, efficient recruitment procedures ensuring quality administrative workforce for state's governance, development, public service delivery contributing administrative excellence managing India's most populous state under Article 315 of Constitution providing employment opportunities to thousands of educated youth annually supporting economic development through government job creation.

Major UPPSC Examinations:

  • PCS (Provincial Civil Service): Combined State/Upper Subordinate Services recruiting IAS-level state officers for administration
  • RO/ARO: Review Officer and Assistant Review Officer for secretariat, revenue administration
  • ACF/RFO: Assistant Conservator of Forests and Range Forest Officer for forest department
  • Assistant Registrar: Cooperative department officers managing cooperative societies across state
  • Lecturer: Government degree college lecturers teaching various subjects in higher education
  • Medical Officers: AYUSH medical officers for Ayurveda, Homeopathy, Unani systems

RO/ARO Recruitment Statistics (A7/E-1/2023):

  • Domicile certificate: UP domicile certificate proving permanent residency in Uttar Pradesh

UP Administrative Infrastructure:

  • State Secretariat: Lucknow headquarters housing 40+ departments managing state administration
  • UPPSC Headquarters: Prayagraj office conducting examinations, recruitment, interviews for state services
  • Board of Revenue: Lucknow headquarters managing land revenue, appeals, administrative coordination
  • Total officers: 5,000+ review officers, secretariat staff managing government files, policy implementation
  • Administrative divisions: 18 divisions, 75 districts requiring administrative coordination, policy implementation

Mains Exam Pattern (Expected):

  • General Studies Paper-I: Indian history, culture, geography, polity, economy, science (200 marks, 3 hours)
  • General Studies Paper-II: Governance, constitution, social justice, international relations (200 marks, 3 hours)
  • Essay Paper: Essay writing on current issues, topics of national/state importance (150 marks, 3 hours)
  • Hindi Paper: Hindi essay, grammar, comprehension, translation testing language proficiency (150 marks, 3 hours)
  • Total marks: 700 marks across four descriptive papers determining final merit
  • Exam mode: Offline conventional written examination requiring pen-paper answers
  • Language medium: Hindi and English (candidates can choose preferred language except Hindi paper)

Skill Test Details (For ARO):

  • Hindi typing test: Minimum 25 Words Per Minute speed requirement on computer
  • Test duration: 10 minutes typing duration for qualifying test
  • Error allowance: Maximum permissible errors as per UPPSC norms
  • Qualifying nature: Marks not added to final merit; only pass/fail basis
  • Computer proficiency: Basic computer operations, MS Office knowledge tested

Comparison with Other State Services:

  • UPSC (Central): Higher prestige, All-India transfers, but UPPSC offers home state advantage, family stability
  • Bihar PSC: Similar structure but UP offers better infrastructure, salary, facilities, career opportunities
  • MP PSC: Comparable benefits but UP provides larger state, more posting options, diverse experience
  • Rajasthan PSC: Similar pay scales but UP offers home state advantage for residents, larger administrative scope
  • UPPSC advantage: Good salary, state service stability, comprehensive benefits, pension security, promotion opportunities

Preparation Resources for Mains:

  • General Studies: NCERT books (6-12 standard), Laxmikant polity, Indian economy by Ramesh Singh
  • Essay writing: Regular practice writing 1,000-1,200 word essays on current issues, diverse topics
  • Hindi preparation: Hindi grammar books, essay collections, comprehension practice, translation exercises
  • Current affairs: Daily newspapers (The Hindu, Dainik Jagran), monthly magazines, UP-specific developments
  • Answer writing: Regular practice writing descriptive answers (200-250 words) within time limits
  • Previous papers: Solving last 5-10 years UPPSC mains papers understanding patterns, question types
  • Mock tests: Regular full-length tests (20+ recommended) simulating exam conditions, time management
