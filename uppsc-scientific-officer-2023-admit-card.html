How To Download UPPSC Admit Card 2025
  • Go to the official UPPSC website: uppsc.up.nic.in.
  • On the homepage, click the link to download the admit card for the specific examination.
  • Enter your Registration Number, Date of Birth, Gender, and the verification code.
  • Click "Submit" to view your admit card.
  • Download the admit card and print a copy for the examination.
