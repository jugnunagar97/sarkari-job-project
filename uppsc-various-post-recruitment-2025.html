UPPSC Various Post Recruitment 2025

Post Date: March 27, 2025

The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) is inviting applications for 36 vacancies across several roles, including Direct Recruitment, Lecturer, and Professor positions. The application window opens on March 24, 2025, and closes on April 24, 2025. Candidates between the ages of 21 and 40 years (as of July 1, 2025) are eligible to apply for most posts. For complete details on this recruitment drive, please refer to the information below and the official notification on uppsc.up.nic.in.

How to Fill UPPSC Various Post Online Form 2025

Interested candidates who wish to apply for the UPPSC post can submit their application online before 24 April 2025. Use the link provided below under the important links section to apply directly or visit the official website of UPPSC, https://uppsc.up.nic.in/, to complete the application process. Make sure to complete the application before the deadline.

Documents Required While Form Filling
  • Photo: A passport-sized colored photo with a white or light-colored background.
  • Signature: Clear signature on white paper using a black or blue pen.
  • Educational Certificates: A bachelor’s degree from a recognized university. Please read the notification for post-specific details.
  • Caste Certificate: For reserved category candidates.
  • Aadhaar Card For ID Proof: Aadhaar card, Voter ID, or any other government-issued ID.
  • Domicile Certificate: Proof of residence for candidates from Uttar Pradesh state.
  • Income Certificate: For candidates applying under the Economically Weaker Section (EWS) category.
  • Other Certificates: Certificates for special categories (such as PH, Ex-servicemen) if applicable.
