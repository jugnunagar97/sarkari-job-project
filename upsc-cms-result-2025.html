UPSC Combined Medical Services CMS Result 2025

Post Date: September 4, 2025

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has published the results for the Combined Medical Services (CMS) Examination 2025. The application period for this recruitment was from February 19, 2025, to March 11, 2025, with the exam being held on July 20, 2025. Candidates who appeared for the examination can now view their results on the official UPSC website. To access your result, you will need to log in with your Registration Number, Date of Birth, or Enrollment Number. The necessary links are provided below.

UPSC COMBINED MEDICAL SERVICES (CMS) EXAMINATION 2025 : SHORT DETAILS

Important Dates

  • Apply Start: 19 February 2025
  • Apply Last Date: 11 March 2025
  • Exam Date: 20 July 2025
  • Admit Card: 10 July 2025
  • Result Date: 04 September 2025

Application Fee

  • General / OBC: ₹ 200/-
  • SC / ST / PH: ₹ 0/-
  • All Female Category: ₹ 0/-
  • Payment Mode: Online via Debit/Credit Card or Net Banking.
UPSC CMS NOTIFICATION 2025 : AGE LIMIT

Age relaxation is applicable as per UPSC Examination rules.

UPSC CMS RECRUITMENT 2025 : ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
  • Candidates must have successfully passed the written and practical components of the final M.B.B.S. examination.
  • Those who have appeared for their final M.B.B.S. exam and are awaiting results are also eligible, but they must provide proof of passing before the Personality Test.
HOW TO DOWNLOAD UPSC CMS RESULT 2025
  1. Navigate to the official UPSC website.
  2. Locate and click on the "Written Results" or "Examination Results" tab.
  3. Find the link for "Combined Medical Services Examination, 2025" and click on it.
  4. A PDF document containing the roll numbers of qualified candidates will be displayed.
  5. Use the search function (Ctrl+F) to find your roll number in the list.
  6. Download and print a copy of the result PDF for future reference.
UPSC CMS : MODE OF SELECTION
  • Computer-based Test (Part - I)
  • Personality Test / Interview (Part - II)
