UPSC NDA II Recruitment 2025: Eligibility Criteria
  • For Army Wing: Must have passed or be appearing in 10+2 (any stream) from a recognized board in India.

How To Check & Download UPSC NDA II Result 2025
  • Visit the official website of the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) – upsc.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Examination” or “What’s New” section.
  • Click on the link for “NDA II Result 2025”.
  • A PDF file containing the roll numbers/names of qualified candidates will open.
  • Use Ctrl+F to search your Roll Number/Name in the list.
  • Download the result PDF and save it for future reference.
  • Take a printout if required for SSB interview and document verification.
