UPSSSC AUDITOR & ASSISTANT ACCOUNTANT 05/2023 DV LETTER

Post Date: July 23, 2025

The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released the Document Verification (DV) Letter for the Auditor & Assistant Accountant posts. This recruitment is for 530 positions. The exam was conducted on 05 January 2025. Candidates who have qualified the written exam can now download their DV letters by logging into the official portal.

Important Dates

Application Start Date: 11 July 2023

Last Date for Apply: 01 August 2023

Exam Date: 05 January 2025

Result Declared: 09 July 2025

DV Letter Available: 22 July 2025

Application Fee

For All Categories: ₹ 25/-

Payment Mode (Online): Debit Card, Credit Card, etc.

UPSSSC Recruitment 2023: Age Limits as on 01/07/2023

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years
  • Maximum Age: 40 Years
  • Age relaxation is provided as per UPSSSC regulations.

How To Check & Download UPSSSC Auditor & Assistant Accountant DV Letter
  1. Visit the official UPSSSC website: upsssc.gov.in.
  2. On the homepage, find the link for “Download DV Letter for Auditor & Assistant Accountant (Advt. No. 05‑Exam/2023)”.
  3. Click the link to open the login dashboard.
  4. Enter your registration number, date of birth, gender, and the verification code.
  5. Your DV call letter will be displayed. Download and print a copy for verification day.
