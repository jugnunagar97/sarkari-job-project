UPSSSC Homoeopathic Pharmacist Result 2025 Declared

Published: September 4, 2025

The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has announced the result for the **Homoeopathic Pharmacist Main Examination 2024**. A total of 2,410 candidates have been shortlisted for the next stage, which is Document Verification (DV).

This recruitment drive is being conducted to fill a total of 397 vacancies. The main examination was held on February 2, 2025.

This is Not the Final Result

The commission has clarified that the currently released list is only for Document Verification. It is not the final selection list. Therefore, having your name on this list does not guarantee an appointment. The final result will be declared after the document verification process is complete.

Document Verification Starts September 16th

Based on their scores in the written exam, 2,410 candidates have been shortlisted for DV. This process will begin on **September 16, 2025**, at the commission's office. Candidates can check their results and cutoff marks on the official website, upsssc.gov.in, under the "Results Segment."

How to Check Your Result

  • First, visit the official website: upsssc.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, click on the "Homoeopathic Pharmacist Mains Result" link.
  • Enter your login details and click submit.
  • Your result will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the result and take a printout for future reference.
