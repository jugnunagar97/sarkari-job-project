How to Check & Download UPSSSC Junior Analyst Food Result 2025
  • Visit the Official Website: Open the Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) official website at upsssc.gov.in.
  • Go to the “Results” Section: On the homepage, click on the Results / Notifications tab.
  • Find the Relevant Result Link: Look for the link titled “Junior Analyst (Food) Result 2025”.
  • Open the Result PDF / Login Page: The result may be published as a PDF merit list (with roll numbers/names) or through the candidate login portal.
  • Enter Login Details (if required): Provide your Registration Number / Roll Number and Date of Birth / Password to view your individual result.
  • Download the Result: Once available, download the Result PDF / Scorecard and save it for future reference.
  • Check Your Details: Verify your Roll Number, Name, Category, and Marks obtained in the result.
  • Cut-Off Marks & Merit List: UPSSSC generally releases category-wise cut-off marks and the provisional merit list along with the result.
  • Next Stage: Candidates shortlisted will be called for Document Verification (DV). Make sure to keep all original documents and photocopies ready.
