How to Check & Download UPSSSC Junior Analyst Medicine Result 2024
  • Visit the Official Website: Open the Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) official site at upsssc.gov.in.
  • Go to the “Results” Section: On the homepage, find the tab labeled Results / Notifications.
  • Find the Relevant Result Link: Look for “Junior Analyst (Medicine) Result 2024” in the list of declared results.
  • Open the Result PDF / Login Page: The result may be published either as a PDF merit list (with roll numbers/names) or via candidate login.
  • Enter Login Details (if required): Provide your Registration Number / Roll Number and Date of Birth / Password to access the result.
  • Download the Result: Once visible, download the Result PDF / Scorecard and save it on your device.
  • Check Your Details: Verify your Roll Number, Name, Category, and Marks in the result.
  • Cut-Off Marks & Merit List: Along with the result, UPSSSC may release category-wise cut-off marks and the provisional merit list.
  • Next Stage: Shortlisted candidates will be called for Document Verification (DV). Keep all required documents ready.
