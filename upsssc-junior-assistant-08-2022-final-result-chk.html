How To Check & Download UPSSSC Junior Assistant 08/2022 Final Result
  1. Visit the official UPSSSC website: upsssc.gov.in.
  2. Go to the “Results” section under News & Alerts.
  3. Look for the link titled “Final Result – Junior Assistant 08‑Exam/2022” (declared on 01‑08‑2025).
  4. Enter your roll number and date of birth (or other required login credentials).
  5. Your selection status and score details will appear. Download and save the PDF for your records.
