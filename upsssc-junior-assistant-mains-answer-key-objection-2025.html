UPSSSC Junior Assistant Mains Answer Key/Objection 2025

Post Date: July 4, 2025 11:56 AM

The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released the Mains Answer Key and Objection link for the recruitment of Junior Assistant, Junior Clerk, and Assistant Grade III posts, covering 5512 vacancies. The Mains exam was conducted on 29 June 2025, with the application process concluding on 03 October 2023. Candidates can access the answer key and submit objections by logging into the portal using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. The objection window is open until 07leck July 2025. Links and instructions are provided below for easy access.

UPSSSC Junior Assistant and Other Post Recruitment 2023: Vacancy Details
Post Name Total Post Eligibility Criteria
Junior Assistant / Junior Clerk / Assistant Grade III 5512
  • Candidates must have passed the 10+2 (Intermediate) exam and qualified in the UPSSSC PET 2022.
  • Typing speed of at least 25 WPM in Hindi and 30 WPM in English.
  • Valid NIELIT CCC computer certificate.
  • For full details, refer to the official notification.
How To Check & Download UPSSSC Junior Assistant Mains Answer Key and Submit Objections 2025
  • Visit the official UPSSSC website.
  • Go to the “Answer Key” section on the homepage.
  • Find and click the link for “Junior Assistant Mains Answer Key 2025”.
  • Select your question paper set/code (A, B, C, or D).
  • Download the PDF file of the answer key and match it with your responses.
  • To submit objections, click the “Answer Key Objection” link, log in with your Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth, and follow the instructions to submit your challenge by 07 July 2025.
