UPSSSC Junior Assistant Mains Result 2025

Post Date: October 8, 2025 | Updated on: October 9, 2025 at 10:00 AM IST

This recruitment is for 5512 positions. The UPSSSC Exam was Conducted in 29 June 2025. UPSSSC Junior Assistant and Other Post Online Form 2023: Educational Qualification
  • Candidates must have passed the 10+2 (Intermediate) exam and qualified in the UPSSSC PET 2022. They should have a typing speed of at least 25 WPM in Hindi and 30 WPM in English, along with a valid NIELIT CCC computer certificate. For full details, refer to the official notification.
  • All candidates must read the official information completely before applying.
How to Check UPSSSC Junior Assistant Mains Result 2025
  • Visit the Official UPSSSC Website: Go to upsssc.gov.in.
  • Go to the Result Section: Click on the “Results” link available on the homepage.
  • Select the Relevant Exam: Choose “Junior Assistant Mains Result 2025” from the list.
  • Enter Required Details: Input your Enrollment Number / Registration Number and Date of Birth.
  • Submit and Download: After entering the details, click on the “Submit” button. Your Result will appear on the screen. Download and print it for future reference.
