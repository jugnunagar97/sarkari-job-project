UPSSSC PET Exam City Details 2025

Post Date: August 27, 2025 6:59 PM

Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released Exam City Details on the official website for the Preliminary Examination Test PET Advt No. 01-Exam/2025. UPSSSC application form was started from 14 May 2025 and the candidates could apply until 17 June 2025. The exam will be conducted on 06–07 September 2025. Candidates must check the complete details for UPSSSC PET exam date 2025 below.

Important Dates Application Fee

  • Last Date Apply Online: 17 June 2025
  • Last Date Pay Fee: 17 June 2025
  • Last Date Correction: 24 June 2025
  • Exam Date: 06–07 September 2025
  • Admit Card: Before Exam
  • Result Date: Will be updated here soon
  • General, OBC: ₹ 185/-
  • SC, ST: ₹ 95/-

  • Age relaxation extra as per UPSSSC PET rules.
