UPSSSC VDO 2023 Mains Revised Answer Key

Post Date: July 28, 2025 10:31 PM

The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released the Mains Revised Answer Key for the recruitment of Village Development Officer (VDO) posts, covering 1468 vacancies. The Mains exam was conducted on 27 April 2025, with the application process concluding on 12 June 2023. Candidates can access the revised answer key by logging into the portal using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. Links and instructions are provided below for easy access.

  • Visit the official UPSSSC website.
